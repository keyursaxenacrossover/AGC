, it is recommended to limit the chats to at most 1,000 posts. Furthermore, you can export the posts of old chats and subsequently delete the chats.</li>
</ul>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>Work steps</strong></p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Setting up a chat involves the following steps:</p>
<ul class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">
<li>Creating a chat section.</li>
<li>Creating a page for the chat and referencing the appropriate section.</li>
<li>Monitoring, and if necessary, actively controlling the chat if the panelists are allowed to post.</li>
</ul>
<h2 id="managing-chats" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>Managing Chats</strong></h2>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">The chats are managed from the <strong>Website → Panel websites → {Selected language version} → Chats</strong> menu. In this menu, you can edit existing chat sections, create new sections, and moderate ongoing chats.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"> </p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">With read rights for “panel_chats”, you can manage chats. With write rights, you can export the posts of a chat.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>Overview of chats</strong></p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">The overview table lists all the chat sections created for the respective language version along with their titles, descriptions, and the number of posts.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">If, in an installation with multiple websites and/or language versions, you want to view the chats of another language version, change the setting in the upper part of the dialog. With the <strong>View</strong> function, you can specify which table columns and how many rows per page will be displayed. You can search the contents of the overview table.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>Edit options</strong></p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Click on the<strong> Create section</strong> button to open the dialog for creating a new chat section.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Click on the title of a chat section to open the list of existing posts. Click on the topic of a post to edit its properties.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Via the icons in the “Actions” column you can edit individual discussion sections:</p>
<ul class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">
<li>
<strong>Edit</strong>: This opens the dialog for editing the section properties.</li>
<li>
<strong>Show posts</strong>: Opens the list of posts.</li>
<li>
<strong>Copy</strong>: The desired section will be copied. The posts will not be copied, though, as this is usually not very practical.</li>
<li>
<strong>Export posts</strong>: This triggers the export of all the posts in this section.</li>
<li>
<strong>Delete</strong>: The selected section will be deleted following a confirmation prompt. Please bear in mind that all data will be irretrievably lost. If you do not need a particular section anymore, you should make use of the possibility to export the posts before deleting them.</li>
</ul>
