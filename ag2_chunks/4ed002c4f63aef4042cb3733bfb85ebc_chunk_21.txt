<h1 id="h_01HGKSMPRSER9ENY5QWWRZZ863" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">FAQ</h1>
<h3 id="h_01HGKSMPRS9JVGC6E9EA3DB98K1" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">What is the maximum number of master data that can be created?</h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Up to 2,800 master data per installation can be created, but the actual maximum number may be lower depending on various factors such as type, size, and content of the master data.</p>
<h3 id="h_01HGKSMPRS9JVGC6E9EA3DB98K2" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">How can I improve the performance of my panel with many master data variables?</h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">To improve performance, regularly review and delete redundant variables, use recoding groups for efficient updates, and limit the use of text variables to no more than 300.</p>
<h3 id="h_01HGKSMPRS9JVGC6E9EA3DB98K3" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Can I automatically execute recodings?</h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Yes, you can configure automated execution of recodings by activating the "Auto recode" function when creating or editing a recoding.</p>
