<h3 id="Project-mail-overview" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-renderer-start-pos="13487"><strong data-renderer-mark="true">Project mail overview</strong></h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-renderer-start-pos="13510">The <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Project mail overview</strong> tab in the <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Options</strong> →<strong data-renderer-mark="true"> Platform cockpit</strong> menu gives a central overview of all mail processes that can be initiated from the current installation.</p>
<ul class="ak-ul wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-indent-level="1">
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="13682">You can display mail activity from any desired period in time.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="13748">Listed for each mail activity are the name of the project, precise dispatch date, project type, field time, e-mail subject, and number of mails submitted.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="13905">Excluded from the count are mail submission processes from deleted projects.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="13985">Mail activities are arranged by project.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="14029">Access to this tab is only possible for users with root or admin rights. These users may see mail activities for those projects for which their team holds read rights.<img src="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/21015136213010"><img style="height: 130.664px;" draggable="false" src="blob:https://qbdocs.atlassian.net/0b25e002-7193-4494-837e-abd8d84832fe#media-blob-url=true&amp;id=526eecf7-3623-4aca-a43e-99b97d172ccd&amp;collection=contentId-1450969079&amp;contextId=1450969079&amp;width=1687&amp;height=365&amp;alt=" alt="" data-testid="media-image">Read rights for <em data-renderer-mark="true">platform_cockpit</em> are required to view and open the <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Options </strong>→<strong data-renderer-mark="true"> Platform cockpit</strong> menu.</p>
</li>
</ul>

<h3 id="Interview-count" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-renderer-start-pos="14304"><strong data-renderer-mark="true">Interview count</strong></h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-renderer-start-pos="14321">You can use the <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Interview Check</strong> tab to examine how many interviews your organization has conducted. The following analyses can be carried out with the statistics tool:</p>
<ul class="ak-ul wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-indent-level="1">
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="14492">Interview numbers for specific projects</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="14535">Interview numbers for a custom time range</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="14580">Daily statistics for interview numbers</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="14622">Interview numbers for disposition codes &gt; 30 in detail</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-renderer-start-pos="14680">The following rules apply to the calculation:</p>
<ul class="ak-ul wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-indent-level="1">
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="14729">All interviews are charged with disposition codes &gt; 30 (31, 32, 35, 36, 37, 41).</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="14813">If you reset a participant and he participates again, this is counted as two interviews.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="14905">If the external survey start is used and the start and target project are both EFS projects, the interviews are counted twice: The participants reach the end page in both projects.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="15089">Interviews that were uploaded via data import are not counted
