<h3 id="using-an-existing-account-to-do-tests" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>Using an Existing Account to do Tests</strong></h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Alternatively, you can also assign a panelist who has already been registered with the “Tester” status. To do so, switch to the <strong>People → Panelists administration</strong> menu, and enter the panelist’s name into the corresponding field. Trigger the search function by clicking on<strong> Search</strong>. The data of the panelist you are searching for will be output as a result. Use the drop-down list to set the panelist’s status to “Tester” and then confirm by clicking on <strong>Submit</strong>.</p>
<h3 id="testing-panel-functions" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>Testing Panel Functions</strong></h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">With a newly created tester account set up according to the instructions above, you will not be able to see an active survey or promotional campaign, because the tester does not belong to a sample or a group yet.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>Adding a tester to a sample and inviting them to a panel survey</strong></p>
<ol class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">
<li>Create the sample.</li>
<li>Open the tab <strong>View tester list</strong>.</li>
<li>Click the <strong>Add testers</strong> button. An overview of all the panelists with the “Tester” status available on the EFS Panel installation will be displayed.</li>
<li>Select the desired testers and confirm the selection by clicking on<strong> Add</strong>.</li>
<li>The testers will be assigned to the sample and appear both in the list of testers currently assigned to the project and on the tab <strong>Sample contents</strong>.</li>
</ol>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">If you want to log in directly to the survey with your tester account, click the link in the column “Code”. If you invite the respondents in the sample using the “Inform” function, the tester will also be invited. Alternatively, you can copy the URL from the “Code” column into an e-mail to invite only the tester, e.g. for a pretest.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><em>Information: Another option is to assign the tester using the features of the detail view. Search for the tester in Panelists administration and open the detail view. On the tab Surveys you can assign the tester to a sample.</em></p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>Inviting testers to the master data survey</strong></p>
<ol class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">
<li>Switch to the<strong> People → Panelists administration</strong> menu. Display the respective person.</li>
<li>Tick the checkbox in the “Actions” column, and choose the “Master data survey” action from the drop-down list.</li>
<li>After that, click on the <strong>Submit</strong> button.</li>
</ol>
