<h3 id="faq_1" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">How many wildcards can I create in a project?</h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">The wildcard administration is designed for up to 250 wildcards. It's recommended to use meaningful names to prevent duplicates and develop your nomenclature if you use wildcards extensively.</p>
<h3 id="faq_2" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Can I change the size of an image inserted using a wildcard?</h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Yes, you can either change the image size before uploading it or use wildcard administration to insert the appropriate HTML attributes into the image's IMG tag.</p>
<h3 id="faq_3" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Are there any restrictions on wildcard names?</h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Wildcard names may contain characters a-z, A-Z, and 0-9, and must be enclosed in two hash signs (#).
