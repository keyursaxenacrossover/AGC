<p data-renderer-start-pos="7975">You are already in the <em data-renderer-mark="true">Statistics</em> menu. Now, select <em data-renderer-mark="true">Online statistics</em> from the left-hand menu.<img src="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/21013837958290"></p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="7975">If your project is multilingual, select the desired language from the drop-down menu in the <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Select language</strong> line.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="7975">If required, you can display variable names and types in the statistics.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="7975">If you want to include all questions in the statistics, activate the checkbox in the<strong data-renderer-mark="true"> Include all questions</strong> line, or alternatively select the individual questions to be included in the statistics.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="7975">Click on <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Continue</strong>.<img src="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/21013837961746"></p>
</li>
</ul>

</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="8706">Total: the total number of people who have answered a question</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="8772">Invalid/Missing: indicates how many questions were seen but not answered</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="8848">Average value<img src="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/21013837971474"></p>
</li>
</ul>
