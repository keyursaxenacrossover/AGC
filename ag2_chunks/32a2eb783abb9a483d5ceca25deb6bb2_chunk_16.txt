<h3 id="managing-news-lists" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>Managing News Lists</strong></h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Many panels offer various news pages with different contents within one language version, e.g. a page with current news and a news archive. This is realized with the help of so-called news lists:</p>
<ul class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">
<li>For each purpose, you create an individual news list.</li>
<li>Then you allocate the individual articles to the appropriate news lists.</li>
<li>The news pages are created with the “News” function module. From this module, the appropriate news list is referenced.</li>
</ul>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">When delivered, there is only one news list in the standard panel, containing all the articles of the news menu. Usually, it is labelled “main”.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">To access the news lists of your panel, click on the <strong>Manage lists</strong> button in the<strong> News management</strong>.</p>
<ul class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">
<li>The overview list shows all existing lists with their respective titles and descriptions.</li>
<li>To create another list, click on the <strong>Create List</strong> button and enter the desired title and description. After clicking on <strong>Save</strong> the list is created and you can start allocating articles to it.</li>
<li>You can rename or delete the lists.</li>
</ul>
