<h3 id="Creating-teams" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-renderer-start-pos="11961"><strong data-renderer-mark="true">Creating teams</strong></h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-renderer-start-pos="11977">With the <em data-renderer-mark="true">groupadmin</em> ACL right, you can create and configure new teams.</p>
<ul class="ak-ul wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-indent-level="1">
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="12051">Switch to the <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Users</strong> → <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Teams</strong> menu.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="12088">Click on the <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Create team</strong> button.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="12124">The following details are required:</p>
<ul class="ak-ul" data-indent-level="2">
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="12163">Team name: This is used in surveys in the standard URL. If you create a team entitled “Test account for student interns”, projects for members of this team will be created under the URL <a class="cc-1rn59kg" title="http://www.mydomain.com/sc/Test_account_-" href="http://www.mydomain.com/sc/Test_account_-" data-testid="link-with-safety" data-renderer-mark="true">http://www.mydomain.com/sc/Test_account_-</a> for_student_interns/something/. As umlauts and blank spaces are not permitted in URLs, EFS automatically replaces impermissible characters upon team creation.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="12553">Team title: Internal name.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="12583">Description: Serves internal purposes as well. <img src="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/21015166306450"></p>
</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>

<ul class="ak-ul wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-indent-level="1">
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="12640">When creating a new team, you can define the owner. You have a choice of yourself as the creating administrator and the team “Administrator“.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="12785">Assign access rights to the desired areas of EFS to the new team. It will then receive 'write' rights to the corresponding area rights.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="12922">In the drop-down list “Rights template”, you can select a user-defined rights template. By default, no rights template has been selected.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="13062">A list of the teams you have created is displayed under the caption “Which teams shall receive free access to the new team?”. If you grant an existing team access to the new team, the existing team will see the new team in user administration.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="13309">Click on the <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Create team</strong> button to confirm the operation.</p>
</li>
</ul>
