<p data-renderer-start-pos="7809">Matrix questions are used when a detailed evaluation of various aspects of an issue is required.</p>
<ul class="ak-ul" data-indent-level="1">
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="7909">Create a new standard page with the title “Evaluation”, using the button <strong data-renderer-mark="true">+ Page</strong> in the questionnaire view.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="8019">Create a question of the question type <em data-renderer-mark="true">Standard matrix 1 (311)</em> using the button <strong data-renderer-mark="true">+ New question</strong>.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="8118">Enter the question text and the dimensions – in other words, the various aspects to be evaluated.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="8219">Click on <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Save</strong>.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="8237">To edit the scale options, open the section “Scale” by clicking on the <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Arrow</strong> icon.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="8323">The entry dialog for the answer scale is opened. Enter four scale options from 1=“I strongly agree” to 4=“I absolutely disagree”.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="8456"> Enter “No answer” as the fifth scale option and define it as a missing value. To do so, activate the checkbox in the column <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Missing value.<img src="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/21018333414162"></strong></p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="8456">Click on<strong data-renderer-mark="true"> Save</strong> and check the result in the preview.<img src="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/21018333432850"></p>
</li>
</ul>

<h3 id="Setting-up-the-Final-Page" data-renderer-start-pos="8659">Setting up the Final Page</h3>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="8686">On the last page of the survey, you want to thank the participants for taking part in your survey. Therefore, the question type <em data-renderer-mark="true">Text and image (998)</em> is a good choice here, too.</p>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="8864"><strong data-renderer-mark="true">Steps:</strong></p>
<ul class="ak-ul" data-indent-level="1">
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="8874">Click on the title of the final page in the questionnaire view, and create a question of the question type <em data-renderer-mark="true">Text and image (998)</em>.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="9006">Enter your text.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="9026">Click on <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Save</strong>.</p>
</li>
</ul>
