<h1 id="h_01J4V96Z91K5RZ8PNG51WCK2YR">Tivian Access Overview</h1>
<p><span style="color: #2f3941; background-color: #ffffff;">Tivian Access is a security module that can be integrated into existing SSO infrastructure to mitigate bad password habits and enhance the overall security of Tivian applications. </span></p>
