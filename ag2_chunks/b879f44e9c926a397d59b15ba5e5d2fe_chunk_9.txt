<h3 id="history-log" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>History log</strong></h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Each task has a history log storing all modifications to this task chronologically. To view information in the history log, click on the tab <strong>History</strong> in the bottom area of the detail view, if not active yet.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>URL link</strong></p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">By clicking the task ID and the button <strong>Copy url</strong>, you can create a URL link for your task, routing responsible persons directly to the detail view of this task.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>Survey link</strong></p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">With this survey link, you can display the complete survey results of participants. To display the survey results, click on the form icon in the detail view. The corresponding survey is displayed in a new window in HTML print view.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">The survey link is only available if the respective task was created by the <em>Task Trigger</em>. To use the survey link, you need the access right to survey results. Without this right, the survey link is invisible to you.</p>

The receipt of push notifications requires the previous installation of the Portals app on your smartphone.</p>

<h1 id="h_01HGKSMPRSER9ENY5QWWRZZ863">FAQ</h1>
<h3 id="faq-1">Can I assign tasks to users outside my unit?</h3>
<p>No, you can only assign tasks to users within your own unit or to users in your unit and all levels down, depending on your configuration.</p>
<h3 id="faq-2">How do I escalate a task?</h3>
<p>Use the "Escalate to" workflow button in the task's detail view to escalate a task to a person in the next level up.</p>
<h3 id="faq-3">Can I reopen a closed task?</h3>
<p>Yes, you can reopen a closed task using the "Reopen" workflow button in the task's detail view.</p>
