<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-renderer-start-pos="647">First of all, you need to fill your panel with panelists who are willing to provide information. In the first step, we would therefore like to show you how panelists can enter your panel. There are several ways of entry: You can, for example, create panelists manually and also import an entire list of panelists into the panelist administration. Alternatively, panelists can also register for your panel via portals or websites that you have created using the Portals and Website modules, or via an offline campaign. In the following, we will show you how to import several panelists into the panelist administration at the same time.</p>

<h3 id="Importing-Panelists" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-renderer-start-pos="1286"><strong data-renderer-mark="true">Importing Panelists</strong></h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-renderer-start-pos="1307">To import several panelists into your panel at the same time, proceed as follows:</p>
<ul class="ak-ul wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-indent-level="1">
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="1392">On the EFS start page, select the <em data-renderer-mark="true">People</em> module.<img src="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/21018333441298"></p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="1392">Then select the <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Panelist administration</strong> menu item from the menu on the left-hand side.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="1392">Click on the <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Import panelists</strong> button.<img src="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/21018302886418"></p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="1392">The <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Import</strong> page opens. At the top of the page, under <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Upload file</strong>, click on <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Download import template</strong>.</p>
</li>
