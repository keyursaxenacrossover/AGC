 Feature is Deprecated?</h2>
<p>Tivian commits to providing advance notice for feature deprecation:</p>
<ul>
<li>At least 6 months notice before the End of Life date for most features</li>
<li>At least 9 months notice for high-impact features requiring customer training</li>
</ul>
<p>Deprecation plans are maintained on the Feature Deprecation page, and customers are notified through various channels to ensure awareness and preparedness.</p>
<h1 id="h_01HGKSMPRSER9ENY5QWWRZZ863">FAQ</h1>
<h3 id="faq_1">What is feature deprecation?</h3>
<p>Feature deprecation is the process of phasing out or removing existing features or modules from the EFS platform to make way for new and improved functionality.</p>
<h3 id="faq_2">How will feature deprecation affect my organization?</h3>
<p>Feature deprecation may require your organization to adapt to new features or find alternative solutions. Tivian aims to minimize disruption by providing ample notice and support during the transition period.</p>
<h3 id="faq_3">What should I do if a feature I use is being deprecated?</h3>
<p>If a feature you use is being deprecated, review the provided alternatives or new features. Contact Tivian support for guidance on transitioning to new solutions or adapting your workflows.</p>
