<h2 id="h_01J69X83VCX4RXG20320QBQXNX">How do I create a ticket?</h2>
<p>After signing in, click on the chat icon in the bottom right corner or select "<strong>Contact Us</strong>". A banner for ATLAS will appear; click on "<strong>Start Chat</strong>" to initiate a session with ATLAS.</p>
<p><img src="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/21015039641490" width="458" height="221"></p>
<ul>
<li>This will open a window for ATLAS chat, where our AI, specialized in DXi and CXi, will assist you with your questions and issues.</li>
<li>If ATLAS cannot resolve your problem, it will conduct an initial triage and gather relevant information before <strong>transferring you to an agent or creating a ticket</strong>, enabling us to assist you more efficiently.</li>
<li>While you have the option to raise a ticket directly with the help of ATLAS chat, we encourage you to <strong>interact with ATLAS first to ensure that the necessary information is collected</strong>, helping our agents address your issue more effectively. <br>
<ul>
<li>Please review <a href="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/en-us/articles/20342435048210" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Getting the most out of Discover XI ATLAS Support Chat</a> for an optimal ATLAS chat experience.</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
<h2 id="h_01J69XG7C0ABWWYCS9FT52WDNZ">What happens if I have an open ticket?</h2>
<p>After the migration to the new system all previously open/pending tickets will be moved and assigned a different ticket ID for better tracking purposes.</p>
<p>After signing in to the Portal you will be able to see all tickets created by you post clicking the “<strong>Requests</strong>” button.</p>
