<h3 id="exporting-discussion-boards" class="sc-eNNmBn lmEGgd wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>Exporting discussion boards</strong></h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">It is possible to export whole discussion boards including attached files from your portal. To export the board, click on the corresponding icon. <span style="color: #091e42;">Available export file types are XLSX and CSV.</span></p>
<h3 id="inviting-panelists-exterior-portal-view" class="sc-eNNmBn lmEGgd wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>Inviting panelists (exterior portal view)</strong></h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Panelists with access to the portal can be invited to participate in a discussion. Go to the exterior view of your portal and open the respective discussion. You will have to log in with your panelist account information. The discussion‘s header will display information on the author of the discussion, its ratings, and whether or not you are subscribed to this discussion. The button <strong>Invite users</strong> is located to the right.</p>
<ul class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">
<li>Click <strong>Invite users</strong>.</li>
</ul>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">A window opens. All panelists available for invitation will be displayed, together with their status indicating, whether or not they have been previously invited to this discussion.</p>
<ul class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">
<li>Select the panelists you would like to invite.</li>
<li>Click <strong>Invite</strong>.</li>
</ul>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><span style="color: #091e42;">Invited panelists will be notified through the notification module. Discussion or user-generated content can be assigned topics. This content may also include tags.</span></p>
<p class="auto-cursor-target wysiwyg-text-align-justify"> </p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Information</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><span style="color: #333333;">Please note that only panelists with the respective user rights can invite other panelists to discussions.</span></p>
<h3 id="tags" class="sc-eNNmBn lmEGgd wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>Tags</strong></h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Discussion or user-generated content can be tagged. Tags can be used in posts in the exterior portal view for bottom-up classification, e. g. describing, marking, or relating. All panelists can create tags for posts (discussions, comments, or replies) they create. These tags will start with a hashtag (#) immediately followed by a word or unspaced phrase. Tags created by panelists will be listed in the <strong>Tags</strong> tab where they can be monitored and maintained. New tags can also be added within the <strong>Tags</strong> tab which will also appear in the type-ahead function for panelists in the exterior view, as soon as they start tagging, i. e. as soon as the hashtag (#) is entered. Tags are configured to begin with a lower-case letter and are sorted automatically in either ascending or descending order (as selected).</p>
