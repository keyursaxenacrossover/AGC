<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-renderer-start-pos="8217">As you probably want to invite a larger number of participants to your survey, it’s best practice to import all participants into the participant administration at once. To do so, proceed as follows:</p>
<ul class="ak-ul wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-indent-level="1">
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="8420">You are in the <em data-renderer-mark="true">Participant administration</em> area. On the left side, click on the <em data-renderer-mark="true">Participant list </em>menu item. Then, click on the split button <strong data-renderer-mark="true">+ New participant </strong>and select the <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Import participants</strong> option.<img src="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/21017226381074"></p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="8420">Click on the button <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Download import template</strong>.<img src="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/21017213520658"></p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="8420">The import template is downloaded in the <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Downloads</strong> folder of your local system.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="8420">Open the import template, and enter your participant data. The variable u_children you have created in the previous step is present in the file as well. You don’t have to fill out all template fields, only the field „u_email“ (participant’s email address) is a mandatory field. However, we recommend entering at least the participant’s first name under “u_firstname” and their last name under “u_name”. The table below is an example of how the beginning of your file could look like.<img src="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/21017226393362"></p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="8420">Save your file.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="8420">On the page, from which you have downloaded the import file, click on the button <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Choose file</strong>. Choose the filled-out template, and click on<strong data-renderer-mark="true"> Open</strong>. The import template will be added.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="8420">Keep all default settings, and click on the button <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Send file</strong>.<img src="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/21017213525522"></p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="8420">On the next page, the columns of the import file are matched with the respective fields of the database. As you have used the recommended template, you don’t need to make any changes. Just click on <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Proceed to preview</strong>.<img src="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/21017213507858"></p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="8420">The preview is displayed and contains all data, which will be imported into the participant administration. The green traffic light symbol on the left-hand side indicates, that all data sets have been imported completely and are logical. Click on <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Import</strong>, to complete the import process.<img src="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/21017213508498"></p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="8420">In case the traffic light symbol on the left-hand side is yellow or red, an error might have occurred. Click on the pen symbol on the right-hand side.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="8420">An edit view is displayed and the traffic light symbol indicates, in which row an error has occurred. In the example below, the value for the time zone is not permitted. Select the correct value for Universal time from the drop-down menu on the right-hand side, and click on <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Save</strong>. The traffic light symbol turns to green. Click on <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Back</strong> in the upper left corner.<img src="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/210172263833
