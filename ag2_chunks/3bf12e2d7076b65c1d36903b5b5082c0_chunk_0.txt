<h2 id="feature_deprecation_overview">Feature Deprecation Overview</h2>
<p>Tivian aims to support customers by continually improving the EFS platform. This sometimes requires changes, replacements, or removal of existing features or modules. Feature deprecation allows Tivian to free up resources for building new and improved features and offering better support to customers.</p>
<h2 id="key_product_lifecycle_events">Key Product Lifecycle Events</h2>
<p>The feature deprecation process involves three key events:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<strong>End of Sale:</strong> The last date on which a feature or module is available for quoting/selling to customers.</li>
<li>
<strong>End of Service:</strong> The date after which customer or technical support (bug fixes) will no longer be provided.</li>
<li>
<strong>End of Life:</strong> The point at which the feature is no longer available within the platform.</li>
</ul>
