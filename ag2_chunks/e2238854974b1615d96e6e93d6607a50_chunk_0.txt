<h1 id="header-1">Portals</h1>
<h2 id="header-3">Improvements of the Report list</h2>
<p><strong>Page module Report list (small)</strong></p>
<p>The small Report list has been reduced to essential functionality. This page module has been optimized for usage in small layout columns.  </p>
<p> </p>
<p><img src="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/20998582442514" alt="c06b384f-6ba6-462d-8178-3e42522c9931"></p>
<p><strong>Downloaded reports are highlighted</strong></p>
<p>To see all downloaded reports at a glance, they are now highlighted with a background color.</p>
<p><strong>New archive data structure for download of several reports</strong></p>
<p>When downloading several reports at the same time, the reports are now delivered as a single ZIP archive.</p>
<h3 id="header-5">Portals CMS</h3>
<p><strong>Group filter, assignment of several roles, changing roles as mass processing</strong></p>
<p>You are now able to group Portals users by filter setting. Furthermore, you can assign several roles to a user simultaneously as well as change roles of Portals users simultaneously for several users via mass processing.</p>
<h3 id="header-6">Circles</h3>
<p>With Circles, Portals users can create their own contact lists from the participants of a portal and manage them (Personal Circles). Also, they can access circles generated for them (Managed Circles).</p>
<p><img src="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/20998625382162" alt="88b6d222-0e57-44a5-ba75-0b7008d3e57a"><img src="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/20998608665618" alt="9142da31-3850-4a95-85d0-3bb0fce11516"></p>
<h3 id="header-7">On-Demand Guided – Integration of Circles</h3>
<p>When creating a new survey in the page module On-Demand Guided, Portals users are now able to select contacts from their circles they want to invite to their survey.</p>
<p><img src="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/20998578089746" alt="610466b2-00ee-469d-ae5f-c5dc5dd62ef0"><img src="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/20998581681042" alt="e174d148-f7f4-47e5-8298-9e7c40d0a69a"></p>
<h3 id="header-8">Portal Switch</h3>
<p>With Portal Switch, Portals users are now able to switch from one portal to another without having to login again.</p>
