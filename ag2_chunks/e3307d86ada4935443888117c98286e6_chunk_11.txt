 or create new export templates.</p>
<h3 id="header-62">Password Column</h3>
<p>After activating the <em>High Security</em> mode, the option to display the password column will no longer be available. This applies to both PE and ES projects.</p>
<h3 id="header-63">Active Project</h3>
<p>If a guide project is connected to a PE project, you can no longer activate the <em>Privacy Assistant </em>(GDPR) subsequently. The <em>active</em> status in the <em>Survey</em> menu can no longer be set to <em>No further participations</em> and/or <em>Completed </em>manually. The <em>Define target value</em> icon and the Pop-up will not be displayed.</p>
<h3 id="header-64">The <em>Accordion Matrix </em>Question Type</h3>
<p>To configure the <em>Accordion Matrix</em> question type, you can enable the option "Inside title" to create a new dimension that will be displayed the entered text as a headline.</p>

<h2 id="header-66">Privacy Assistant (GDPR)</h2>
<p>The<em> Privacy Assistant</em> (GDPR) has also been enhanced. The record of the consent form has been modified and the deletion of participant data has been improved.</p>
<h3 id="header-68">The Record of the Consent Form</h3>
<p>The record of the consent form has been modified to improve the usability of the print version. The complete text of text field will be displayed completely without a scrollbar.</p>
<h3 id="header-69">Deleting Participants</h3>
<p>Participants who do not participate in a survey cannot give their consent to data processing. They have no access to the consent form. Nevertheless, the data of these participants will be stored in the participant administration. As of the Fall release, you will be able to delete this data.</p>
<p>You can tick the option "Automatic deletion of personal data" to activate this feature. After ticking this option, a period of time has to be defined. If a participant will not participate in the survey within this period of time the personal data (e-mail, first name, surname) will be deleted automatically. The disposition code 43 is described as <em>Personal data deleted</em>.</p>
