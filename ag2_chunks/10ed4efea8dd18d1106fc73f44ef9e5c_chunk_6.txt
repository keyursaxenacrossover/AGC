<h2 id="h_01J4PSTR3BDF1C47YBD4Z0XDR8">People Module</h2>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; color: #2f3941; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, ' Segoe UI' , Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 15px; font-style: normal; font-variant-ligatures: normal; font-variant-caps: normal; font-weight: 400; letter-spacing: normal; orphans: 2; text-align: start; text-indent: 0px; text-transform: none; widows: 2; word-spacing: 0px; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px; white-space: normal; background-color: #ffffff; text-decoration-thickness: initial; text-decoration-style: initial; text-decoration-color: initial;">People module combines powerful panel management, a content management system for designing the panel website, and the functions of the Enterprise Feedback Suite, the basic platform for performing data collection processes.</p>

People module is designed to manage panels with over 100,000 participants.</p>
