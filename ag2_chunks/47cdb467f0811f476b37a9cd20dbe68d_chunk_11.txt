<h4 id="Sending-out-the-Invitation-Mail" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-renderer-start-pos="14680"><strong data-renderer-mark="true">Sending out the Invitation Mail</strong></h4>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-renderer-start-pos="14713">You have now completed all necessary preparations and can now send out the invitation to your survey to participants.</p>
<ul class="ak-ul wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-indent-level="1">
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="14834">Go to the <em data-renderer-mark="true">Survey menu</em>.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="14860">In the <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Distribution</strong> section, click on the button <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Contact participant groups</strong>.<img src="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/21017213530898"></p>
</li>
<li>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-left" data-renderer-start-pos="14860">Several options for contacting participants with different disposition codes, or statuses (e.g. “invited”, “not invited yet” etc.), are displayed.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-left" data-renderer-start-pos="14860">Click on the link <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Send invitation mails.</strong> With this option, your invitation is sent to all participants in your sample.<img src="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/21017226404242"></p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="14860">A mask for configuring the invitation mail is opened.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="14860">Under <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Common settings</strong>, select the mail template you have created in the previous step from the drop-down menu. All the fields will be automatically populated with the settings from the mail template.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="14860">In case you don’t want to send out the invitation right away, you can adjust the date and time under <strong data-renderer-mark="true">When to send </strong>in the <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Delivery options</strong> on the lower part of the screen.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="14860">Next, click on <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Preview mail</strong> in the lower right corner.<img src="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/21017213534354"></p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="14860">A preview of your invitation mail is displayed. Check, whether the placeholders for first name, name, and the link to the survey have been replaced with the data you have set up for the participant in the participant administration. Only one mail for one participant will be displayed at a time. If you would like to check the emails for your other participants, click on the arrow symbol right over the mail content to display them.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-left" data-renderer-start-pos="14860">Finally, click on<strong data-renderer-mark="true"> Send</strong>.<img src="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/21017213535506"></p>
</li>
</ul>

<h3 id="faq-anonymous-personalized" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">What's the difference between anonymous and personalized surveys?</h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Anonymous surveys are for unknown participants and use public distribution methods. Personalized surveys are for known participants and use email invitations with participant management.</p>
