<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><span style="color: #091e42;"><span style="color: #091e42;"><span style="color: #091e42;">Finally, during the </span><strong>Confirmation</strong><span style="color: #091e42;"> step, you will receive an overview confirming the number of questions included in the guide, along with which group it will be sent to. From this screen, it is also possible to write a short personal message to the recipients of the guide. Once you confirm your selection the invitation emails will be sent.</span></span></span></p>
<h3 id="after-starting-a-guide" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong><span style="color: #091e42;"><span style="color: #091e42;"><span style="color: #091e42;">After starting a guide</span></span></span></strong></h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><span style="color: #091e42;"><span style="color: #091e42;"><span style="color: #091e42;"><span style="color: #091e42;">To find out more information about the status of your guide, click on the </span><strong>View status</strong><span style="color: #091e42;"> button. In the overview page, you can see which group was invited to take the survey, along with the current status of the survey (completed/running), and how many people have answered the survey. You also have the option to remind the group to participate, i.e. to send a reminder email, and to end the survey manually. Once the survey is complete, click the </span><strong>Results </strong><span style="color: #091e42;">button to switch to the results dashboard. When a survey is complete, you can invite the group to conduct the survey again if desired.</span></span></span></span></p>

<h1 id="h_01HGKSMPRSER9ENY5QWWRZZ863" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">FAQ</h1>
<h3 id="h_01HGKSMPRST9CCRQ13OOY5E8UM" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">How do I make a survey featured in the Cards module?</h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">To make a survey featured, navigate to Projects → [selected project] → Project properties → Portal settings. Edit the portal settings and mark the desired surveys as featured. These surveys will be highlighted with a golden star in the portal's exterior view.</p>
<h3 id="h_01HGKSMPRSUDDDSR24PPZ6E9VN" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Can I combine existing groups with new individuals when sending a guide?</h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Currently, when creating a new group from individual panelists and/or unregistered people for a guide, this cannot be combined with existing groups. You must choose either to send the guide to an existing group or create a new group from scratch.</p>
<h3 id="h_01HGKSMPRSVEEETR35QQA7FAWN" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">How can I ensure the confidentiality of guide results?</h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">In the Guides settings, you can set a minimum number of participants for the guide. This ensures that results are only made available once a minimum number of people have responded to the guide, maintaining confidentiality.</p>
