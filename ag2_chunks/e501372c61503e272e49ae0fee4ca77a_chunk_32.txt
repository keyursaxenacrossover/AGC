<h1 id="h_01HGKSMPRSER9ENY5QWWRZZ863" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">FAQ</h1>
<h3 id="h_01HGKSMPRSQX3YJGQX3YJGQX3Y" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">How do I control access to community features?</h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Each community feature (blogs, forums, discussions, chats, and downloads) has access control options. You can set access to the public, all logged-in panelists, or specific panel groups only.</p>
<h3 id="h_01HGKSMPRSYJGQX3YJGQX3YJGQ" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Can I moderate content before it's published?</h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Yes, you can set the publishing mode to "Publish after approval" for each feature. This allows you to review and approve content before it becomes visible to other panelists.</p>
