<h2 id="cards" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>Cards</strong></h2>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><span style="color: #333333;"><span style="color: #333333;">You can control the outer appearance of each panel survey displayed in the cards module by defining a name or changing its teaser image. </span>You can also decide to exclude specific portals from displaying individual panel surveys.</span></p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><img src="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/21015039414674" alt="Cards.png"></p>
<h3 id="settings" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>Settings</strong></h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">You navigate via the following path to portal settings:</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><em>Projects</em> → [<em>selected project</em>] → <em>Project properties</em> → <em>Portal settings</em></p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">A list of your portals will be displayed</p>
<ul class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">
<li>Add a name and description</li>
</ul>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">These will be the name and the description for the survey displayed in the exterior view of your portal.</p>
<ul class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">
<li>Select the check box <strong>Hide survey in the portal</strong> for those portals you would like to exclude from the survey</li>
</ul>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">The survey will not be displayed for panelists of excluded portals. You can upload an image that will be displayed as the background of your survey tile in the exterior view.</p>
<ul class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">
<li>click on the image icon displayed to the far right and activate the image by checking the <strong>Featured image</strong> check box</li>
</ul>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Your survey will be visible to your portal's panelists immediately.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><span style="color: #091e42;">Define in the module on page level whether surveys or featured discussions should be displayed. Discussions can be defined as featured discussions during the creation process. Surveys can be accessed and completed from here. Depending on the survey settings, surveys might disappear from the list once completed.</span></p>
<h3 id="employee-projects" class="sc-eNNmBn lmEGgd wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>Employee Projects</strong></h3>
<p class="sc-eNNmBn lmEGgd wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><span style="color: #091e42;">Employee projects will automatically appear in a panelist's survey list if the panelist is contained in the project's list of participants and has not yet completed the survey.</span></p>
<h3 id="featured-surveys" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong><span style="color: #333333;"><span style="color: #333333;">Featured Surveys</span></span></strong></h3>
