<h1 id="h_01HGKSMPRS9JVGC6E9EA3DB98K">Overview</h1>
<p>This article provides a detailed explanation of the Field Report, focusing on the values used in different sections and their significance in both anonymous and personalized projects.</p>
<p> </p>
<h1 id="h_01HGKSMPRSYYE7GMM7DZ0VCKXK">Information</h1>
<p>The Field Report is a comprehensive tool that breaks down survey participation data into various categories, helping researchers understand the composition and performance of their survey sample.</p>
