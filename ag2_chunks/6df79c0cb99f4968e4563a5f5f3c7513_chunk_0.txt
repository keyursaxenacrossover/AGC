<h1 id="h_01HGKSMPRS9JVGC6E9EA3DB98K" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Overview</h1>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">This article provides a comprehensive guide to survey messages used in Questback's survey system. It covers various types of messages, including general messages, mandatory questions, type checks, plausibility checks, login messages, system messages, and pretest comments.</p>
<h1 id="h_01HGKSMPRSYYE7GMM7DZ0VCKXK" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Information</h1>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">This guide focuses on the different messages and texts used throughout the survey process, from general interface elements to specific error messages and instructions. It is essential for customizing and localizing surveys to meet specific project needs.</p>
