<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-renderer-start-pos="15755">You use one of the templates created in <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Libraries</strong> by selecting one of these consent form templates via the drop-down list. The selection of the templates that are displayed to you depends on the language you have previously selected. The <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Manage templates</strong> link takes you to the <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Libraries</strong> where the consent forms created are managed.</p>

<h2 id="Portal-registration" data-renderer-start-pos="16808">Portal registration</h2>
</div>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-renderer-start-pos="16830">It is possible to obtain consent to process personal data from new users registering on your portals. When the setting is configured, the portal login dialog has a second checkbox under the<strong data-renderer-mark="true"> Register</strong> tab.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-renderer-start-pos="16830"><img src="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/21017221234322"></p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-renderer-start-pos="16830">You activate this under <strong data-renderer-mark="true">General settings</strong> in the CMS of <em data-renderer-mark="true">Portals</em> by ticking the option “Activate Privacy consent form”.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><img src="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/21017191626642"><span style="font-size: 1.2em; font-weight: 600;">Configuring the consent form in the People module</span></p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-renderer-start-pos="17212">In the module People under <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Panel Configuration,</strong> you will find the menu item “Consent form”, where you can create the consent form for the purpose of registering a panelist. In order to be able to use this feature to capture the consent of future portal participants, you must configure the consent form in the default language, otherwise, the checkbox will not be displayed. One configuration applies to all portals. This feature is available both for the login dialog via the portal and for inviting other panelists via e-mail. Otherwise, the checkbox is not displayed.</p>
