<h3 id="01J35TJY7NYKKPBFHJ027CY45Y" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-renderer-start-pos="17982"><strong data-renderer-mark="true">Granting Read rights to a specific team to other teams</strong></h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-renderer-start-pos="18038">As the owner of a user team, you can grant read rights to your team to other selected admin teams. The members of authorized teams can then see the team in question in the <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Teams</strong> menu, select it, and view the available information.</p>
<ul class="ak-ul wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-indent-level="1">
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="18270">If you are the owner of a staff team, you can find in the <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Users</strong> → <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Teams</strong> menu a <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Change rights</strong> icon in the “Action” column. Click on it.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="18408">A dialog opens in which you activate the checkbox in the column “Grant access rights”, which enables you to grant read rights to your team to one or several other teams. You have a choice of all the teams available on the installation, not only those to which you belong.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="18683">After that, confirm by clicking on <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Save</strong>.</p>
</li>
</ul>
