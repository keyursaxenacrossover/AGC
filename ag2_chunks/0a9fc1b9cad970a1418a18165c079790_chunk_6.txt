wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Detailed instructions for editing these variables can be found in the "EFS Survey" manual, Chapter "Managing Participants in Personalized Surveys".</p>

<h1 id="h_01HGKSMPRSER9ENY5QWWRZZ863" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">FAQ</h1>
<h3 id="h_01HGKSMPRSJKQXJ8QXJ8QXJ8QX" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">What is the difference between Master Data and Process Data?</h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Master Data refers to participants' personal information that remains constant, such as name and email. Process Data contains information about a participant's current position or status in a specific feedback constellation, which may change throughout the project.</p>
<h3 id="h_01HGKSMPRSPKQXJ8QXJ8QXJ8QX" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Can I create custom variables for Master Data and Process Data?</h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Yes, you can create custom variables for both Master Data and Process Data to meet your specific project requirements. This can be done through the Participant administration menu in EFS.</p>
<h3 id="h_01HGKSMPRSVKQXJ8QXJ8QXJ8QX" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">How can I use Master Data and Process Data in my questionnaire?</h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">You can use Master Data and Process Data in your questionnaire for personalization, dynamic routing, and answer validation. The piping feature in EFS 10.3 makes it easier to select and insert these data points into your questionnaire.</p>
