<h1 id="bonuses" class="auto-cursor-target wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>Bonuses</strong></h1>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Bonus points are usually exchanged for bonuses on special pages in the password-protected, internal panel area. EFS Panel helps you present and manage bonuses:</p>
<ul class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">
<li>You can create different bonuses and sort them into self-defined categories for easy browsing.</li>
<li>You can set the value of a bonus yourself or let the panelist enter a payment amount in a text field.</li>
<li>In addition to conventional standard bonuses, you can also offer your panelists vouchers from affiliated companies. Optionally, voucher codes can be sent to panelists automatically.</li>
</ul>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Bonus payment is handled by EFS Panel’s proven bonus system.</p>
