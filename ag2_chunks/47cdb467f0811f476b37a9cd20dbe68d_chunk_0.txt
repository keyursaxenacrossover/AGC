<h2 id="Anonymous-Survey" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-renderer-start-pos="465"><strong data-renderer-mark="true">Anonymous Survey</strong></h2>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-renderer-start-pos="483">In the case of anonymous surveys, you don’t know the participants beforehand. In the <em data-renderer-mark="true">survey menu</em> under <em data-renderer-mark="true">project information</em> you can find several options in the<strong data-renderer-mark="true"> Distribution</strong> section for delivering your anonymous survey to potential participants:</p>
<ul class="ak-ul wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-indent-level="1">
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="730">Publishing the survey link on a website, in a newsletter, or in an email</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="805">Publishing a QR code</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="829">Posting the survey link on social media</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="872">Integrating a pop-up window into a website</p>
</li>
</ul>

<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-renderer-start-pos="949">If you would like to share the link to your survey on a website, in an email, or at any other place, just copy the URL displayed under <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Distribution</strong> in the line <strong data-renderer-mark="true">URL</strong>. Afterward, you can insert it at the desired place. In case you want to change the URL, for example in order to adapt it to your company name, please proceed as follows:</p>
<ul class="ak-ul wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-indent-level="1">
<li>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-left" data-renderer-start-pos="1286">Click on the Pen symbol on the right-hand side of the URL.<img src="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/21017213492498"></p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="1286">In the free text field in the line <strong data-renderer-mark="true">New URL</strong>, enter the desired text – e.g. the name of your company.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="1286">The checkbox for <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Replace URL in questions?</strong> is activated by default. Just keep this setting; it ensures that the new URL is used throughout the whole survey.<img src="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/21017226370322"></p>
</li>
</ul>
