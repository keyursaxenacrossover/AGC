<h2 id="send-invitation-mails" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Send Invitation Mails</h2>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">You will go through the entire mail dispatch process, from selecting the recipients and filling in the form to checking that the mail has been sent success- fully.</p>
<ul class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">
<li>
<p>Switch to the <strong>Contact Participant Groups </strong>tab in <strong>Participant Administration</strong>. It offers various options for contacting participants with a specific status.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Click on the <strong>Send invitation mails </strong>link.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Select the desired mail template, and confirm by clicking on <strong>Apply mail template</strong>.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Please take into consideration that only those mail templates that fit the respective action area will be offered for selection. For example, if you are in the area of the “Send invitation emails” function, only the templates of the “Invitation” type and of the “Default” type, which can be used anywhere, will be offered.</p>
<ul class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">
<li>
<p>Make sure that the proper mail format has been selected. Otherwise, change the mail format and confirm by clicking on <strong>Apply mail format</strong>.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">It is important to select the proper mail format <em>before </em>entering content: different input boxes are used for the various formats.</p>
<ul class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">
<li>
<p>If you wish to make use of the advanced mail settings, open this section and make the desired changes.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Check the content and settings of the mail template.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Tick the “Personalized dispatch” checkbox if you wish to use the recipient’s real name.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Enter the subject.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Select the sender. If the desired sender is not available, you can click on the <strong>Add mail sender </strong>link to open the menu for creating new senders (Chapter <em>12.4, p. 536</em>) in a new window.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Formulate the content of the invitation mail. You can display an overview of the wildcards you can use by clicking on the <strong>question mark </strong>icon.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>If you wish to add an attachment, it should be uploaded in the “Add an attachment” field. Locate the desired file, and click on <strong>Attach file </strong>to add it.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Click on the <strong>Preview mail </strong>button to check the result.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>In particular, check whether the wildcards are replaced correctly.</p>
</li>
<li>
