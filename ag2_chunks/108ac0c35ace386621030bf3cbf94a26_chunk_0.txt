<h1 id="h_01JD51M7T1ARC1ENFD6H316YDX">Overview</h1>
<p>If you have sent emails through a specific Tivian DXI mailing template and need to see who received those emails, this guide will provide you with detailed instructions on how to do so.</p>
<h1 id="h_01JD51M7T1BMVXRN3QKPG596BC">Solution</h1>
<ol>
<li>Login to your DXi instance</li>
<li>Go to <strong>System &gt; Options &gt; Mail Queue</strong><br><img src="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/22757396828178" width="348" height="175">
</li>
<li>You are now in the <a href="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/en-us/articles/20371076859410" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Mail Report</a> section. Select the tab that you wish to view the email report for (Projects, Panel, Panel Group, Website, Portals or System)<br><img src="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/22757396832402" width="448" height="105">
</li>
<li>Use <strong>CTRL+F</strong> or <strong>CMD+F</strong> to search for the email template ID you are interested in on the current page. <br><em><strong>Note that there may be more entries on the same page and/or multiple pages.</strong></em>
</li>
<li>Click on the first entry of the <strong>Mailing</strong> entry corresponding to the row that was found in the above step.<br><img src="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/22757396833810" width="457" height="128">
</li>
<li>Set to <strong>DISPLAY ALL</strong> and then you can <strong>view</strong> or <strong>export</strong> the list of email addresses associated with that Mailing entry<br><img src="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/22757396835602" width="432" height="217">
</li>
<li>Repeat <strong>steps 3-6</strong> as necessary for the remainder of the entries where the required template ID was used.</li>
</ol>
<h1 id="h_01JD51M7T1SEW2KT218ECKY2QY">Summary</h1>
<p>By following these steps, you can check who received emails sent through a specific Tivian DXI mailing template. This can be useful for tracking your email campaigns and ensuring that your messages are reaching the intended recipients.</p>
<h2 id="h_01JD51M7T149578EZDC55FBC1A">FAQ</h2>
<ol>
<li>
<strong>Can I export the list of email recipients?</strong><br>Yes, you can export the list of email recipients from the Mailing entry page.</li>
<li>
<strong>What if the email template ID is not found on the current page?</strong><br>If the email template ID is not found on the current page, it may be on a different page. You can navigate through the pages using the page navigation options at the bottom of the Mail Queue page.</li>
<li>
<strong>Can I use this method to check recipients for all email templates?</strong><br>Yes, this method can be used to check recipients for any email template in your Tivian DXI instance.</li>
</ol>432" height="217">
</li>
<li>Repeat <strong>steps 3-6</strong> as necessary for the remainder of the entries where the required template ID was used.</li>
</ol>
