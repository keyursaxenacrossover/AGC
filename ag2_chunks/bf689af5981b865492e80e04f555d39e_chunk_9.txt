<h3 id="Creating-Recodes" data-renderer-start-pos="13566"><strong data-renderer-mark="true">Creating Recodes</strong></h3>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="13584">Next, we would like to perform two different recodes for our test report. In statistics, recoding refers to the transformation or re-categorization of data. This can be helpful to better analyze data. With Reporting+, different types of recodes can be realized: <em data-renderer-mark="true">Simple recoding, Multiple recoding, Recoding for use as weighting template, Top responses in open-ended answers</em> and – under certain circumstances – <em data-renderer-mark="true">Grouping of numbers</em>.
