<h3 id="using-rich-text-editor-for-item-design" class="auto-cursor-target wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>Using Rich Text Editor for Item Design</strong></h3>
<p class="auto-cursor-target wysiwyg-text-align-justify">FAQ items, news items and quick polls may be edited using the TinyMCE rich text editor. This allows you to create appealing items without great effort.</p>
<ul class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">
<li class="auto-cursor-target">TinyMCE is a Javascript-based WYSIWYG editor. Detailed information is available at <a href="http://tinymce.moxiecode.com/">http://tinymce.moxiecode.com/</a>.</li>
<li class="auto-cursor-target">The admin area features a comprehensive toolbar. In addition to standard formatting options such as bold, italic, underline, alignment, and ordering, it also includes text and background color, “Paste from Word” and full-screen mode.</li>
<li class="auto-cursor-target">If you prefer working with HTML, use the <strong>Edit HTML source</strong> button to open an alternative entry field.</li>
</ul>
<p class="auto-cursor-target wysiwyg-text-align-justify">If you wish to use the rich text editor, open the <strong>Options → Personal preferences</strong> menu and activate the “Use rich text editor for news etc.” option.</p>
