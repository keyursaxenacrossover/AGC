<h1 id="h_01HGKSMPRSER9ENY5QWWRZZ863">FAQ</h1>
<h3 id="faq_1">Why should I avoid using deadlines in my survey invitations?</h3>
<p>Deadlines can lead to procrastination and forgotten surveys. Recipients may put the survey aside, intending to complete it closer to the deadline, but then forget about it entirely. Requesting a response as soon as possible encourages more immediate action.</p>
<h3 id="faq_2">How many reminders should I send for an external survey?</h3>
<p>For external surveys, such as those directed at customers, it's best to send only one reminder. Multiple reminders can be perceived as pushy and may potentially harm your relationship with the respondents.</p>
<h3 id="faq_3">What's the best time frame for sending reminders after the initial survey invitation?</h3>
<p>It's recommended to send reminders within 3-5 days of the main distribution. This timing capitalizes on the initial interest while ensuring the survey doesn't get forgotten amidst other priorities.</p>
