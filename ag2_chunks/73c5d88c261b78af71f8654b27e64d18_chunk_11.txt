<h1 id="h_01HGKSMPRSER9ENY5QWWRZZ863" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">FAQ</h1>
<h3 id="faq_1" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">How do I change the default language of my survey?</h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">To change the default language, go to the Survey Languages section, find the desired new default language in the list, and click on the "Use as default language" icon.</p>
<h3 id="faq_2" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Can I reward participants with bonus points at different stages of the survey?</h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Yes, you can allocate bonus points on the system final page, on intermediate final pages, and use bonus triggers for specific events within the survey.</p>
<h3 id="faq_3" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">How can I highlight important surveys in the survey list?</h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">In the Website Settings menu, you can use user-defined labels to highlight selected surveys with specific notes or icons. You can also mark surveys as "Featured" and add images to make them stand out.</p>
