<p>The IAM (Identity and Access Management) Project has several goals:</p>
<ul>
<li><p>Give users an SSO experience throughout Tivian products</p></li>
<li><p>Handle login in a secure manner from a central place</p></li>
<li><p>Federate the IdPs of clients to give a more seamless authentication experience</p></li>
<li><p>Create a flexible solution for multiple tenants and use cases</p></li>
</ul>
