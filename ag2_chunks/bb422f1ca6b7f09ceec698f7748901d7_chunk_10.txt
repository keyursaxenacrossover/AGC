<h1 id="h_01HGKSMPRSER9ENY5QWWRZZ863" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">FAQ</h1>
<h3 id="faq-quality-correction" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">How often should I activate Quality Correction?</h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">It's recommended to reactivate Quality Correction after new participations and before exporting results data to ensure all responses have an updated quality score.</p>
<h3 id="faq-data-deletion" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Can I restore deleted data records?</h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">No, once a data record is deleted using the Quality Correction feature, it cannot be restored. This applies to both the participant's result data and their information in the participant administration.</p>
