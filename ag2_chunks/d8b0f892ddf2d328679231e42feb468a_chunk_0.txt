<h2 id="general_conditions">General Conditions</h2>
<p>Before proceeding with data restoration, it's important to consider the following:</p>
<ul>
<li>Data analysis and recovery may incur costs, including:
<ul>
<li>Setting up a mirror EFS instance (<span style="color: #2f3941; font-family: system-ui, -apple-system, ' system-ui' , ' Segoe UI' , Roboto, Oxygen-Sans, Ubuntu, Cantarell, ' Helvetica Neue' , Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 14px; font-style: normal; font-variant-ligatures: normal; font-variant-caps: normal; font-weight: 400; letter-spacing: normal; orphans: 2; text-align: start; text-indent: 0px; text-transform: none; widows: 2; word-spacing: 0px; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px; white-space: normal; background-color: #ffffff; text-decoration-thickness: initial; text-decoration-style: initial; text-decoration-color: initial; display: inline !important; float: none;">2.5 k (Euro) or 2.75 k (USD) </span>)</li>
<li>Deeper analysis by EFS Technical Support (if no software bug or exception caused the loss)</li>
<li>Actual recovery costs per hour</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
<h2 id="required_information">Required Information</h2>
<p>To recover lost data, the following information is needed:</p>
<ul>
<li>Type of missing or lost data (e.g., whole project, respondent data, panelist data)</li>
<li>Installation details (Customer and EFS installation URL)</li>
<li>Date of data deletion or when it was first noticed as missing</li>
<li>Desired location for data restoration (e.g., live EFS instance, copy of the project)</li>
</ul>
<h2 id="process_steps">Process Steps</h2>
<h3 id="h_01J4NZ5G2V7GH08QM5V27ZYFFD">Step 1: Information Collection</h3>
<p>1st Level Support collects all necessary information from the customer.</p>
<h3 id="h_01J4NZ5G2V156VFR5EPYRN4C0A">Step 2: Issue Creation</h3>
<p>1st Level Support opens an issue with SaaS Team.</p>
<h3 id="h_01J4NZ5G2VJS894B2FVD7B1RPH">Step 3: Cause and Recovery Assessment</h3>
<p>The Professional Service Team checks the cause of data loss and determines if recovery is possible.</p>
<h3 id="h_01J4NZ5G2VR1D9MP6WZ0HE8GQ1">Step 4: Recovery Options</h3>
<p>If recovery is possible, the Professional Service Team provides options and time estimates to 1st Level Support.</p>
<h3 id="h_01J4NZ5G2VA629BAAAFQK5XD75">Step 5: Customer Agreement <em>(Need Internal Review)</em>
</h3>
<p>1st Level Support discusses options and costs with the customer.</p>
<h3 id="h_01J4NZ5G2VBQ7CGEDMTAGV2RMS">Step 6: Mirror Setup (if needed)</h3>
<p>If a mirror is needed, 1st Level Support requests SaaS Team to create one.</p>
<h3 id="h_01J4NZ5G2VCTYHCRRA4THKXF9Z">Step 7: Data Recovery</h3>
<p>Depending on the situation, either the customer or the Professional Service Team does the data recovery.</p>
