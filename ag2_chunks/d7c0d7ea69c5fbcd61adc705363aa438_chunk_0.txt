<h2 id="h_01HGKSMPRT0YJQCQ3JXBP5XNQM" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Contact Request List</h2>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Visitors to the panel site and panelists can write to the panel operator via the contact form available in the external view. The contact requests are delivered to the specified recipient via email and documented in the admin area.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>Viewing contact requests in the admin area</strong></p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">In the People → Contacts menu, you can view, search, and edit contact requests. The overview lists all requests along with their dispatch dates, subjects, contents, senders, editors, request status, and language versions. You can download this list via the Excel export and CSV export buttons.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>Editing options available</strong>:</p>
<ul class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">
<li>
<strong>Detail view for contact request</strong>: Opens the full content of the request.</li>
<li>
<strong>Send reply</strong>: Respond directly using the standard mail dialog.</li>
<li>
<strong>Assign request status</strong>: Available statuses are “Open”, “Currently edited”, “Closed”, “Assigned to contact manager”, and “Feedback from panelist requested”.</li>
<li>
<strong>Assign an editor</strong>: Assign to your own account or any previous editor.</li>
<li>
<strong>Delete</strong>: The request will be deleted.</li>
</ul>
<h2 id="h_01HGKSMPRT8YJXQX3JDQKX3NQN" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Groups (Portals)</h2>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">With Groups, portal users can create predefined or individual groups and use them as contact lists for the Feedback Assistant module by inviting contacts from Groups to participate in surveys. Groups enable quick access to a circle of members and dynamically adapt the display of logged-in members for portal users.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Groups are used in two ways: as an integrated module in Feedback Assistant to invite participants to surveys, and as a separate module in Portals for organizing groups. There are two types of circles: Personal Groups and Managed Groups.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>Personal Groups:</strong></p>
<ul class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">
<li>Created in the separate Groups module.</li>
<li>Users can create their own contact lists from portal participants.</li>
<li>Flexible and only available to the circle creator.</li>
</ul>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>Managed Groups:</strong></p>
<ul class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">
<li>Generated in the EFS administration area.</li>
<li>Composed of hierarchically dependent groups.</li>
<li>Accessible to any portal user who is a member of the circle.</li>
</ul>
