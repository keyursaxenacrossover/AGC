<h3 id="h_01HGKSMPRS7YJHG8XJDE1QW3TN6" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">What is the maximum recommended number of active groups per installation?</h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">It is recommended to use not more than 1,000 active groups per installation. Superfluous panel groups should be archived regularly.</p>
<h3 id="h_01HGKSMPRSM4YJXHXJ8YJDE1QW6" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">How often should update rules be executed automatically?</h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">It is recommended to use the automated execution of update rules not more than 20 times per installation to maintain optimal performance.</p>
<h3 id="h_01HGKSMPRS3TNQXCQX8YJXBP6N6" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Can I delete a group that is used elsewhere in the system?</h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">No, it is no longer possible to delete a People group that is used elsewhere in EFS. You can check the dependencies of a group in the "Usage" tab of the group's edit dialog.</p>
