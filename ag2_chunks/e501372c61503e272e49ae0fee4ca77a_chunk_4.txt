<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">As a moderator, you can use additional features when posting from the admin area or, if necessary, change or delete posts by bloggers and readers.</p>

<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Blog posts should not be deleted or changed subsequently without good reason. Use your edit options sparingly and observe the netiquette.</p>
