<h3 id="faq_1" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">How many quotas can I have in a project?</h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">It is recommended to use not more than 300 quotas per project to maintain optimal performance.</p>
<h3 id="faq_2" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Can I reactivate participants if I increase the size of an external quota?</h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Yes, in projects that use external quotas exclusively, you can reactivate participants by increasing the quota size, changing the status to "active," and following the prompts to reactivate participants for the changed quotas.</p>
