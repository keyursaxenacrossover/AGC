 class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">On the <strong>Additional Information</strong> tab, you can add texts, photos, videos, and links in order to. inform the guide initiator about the purpose of the guide project. This information will be available by clicking on the <strong>More Details</strong> Button.</p>
<h3 id="categories" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>Categories</strong></h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">On the <strong>Categories</strong> tab, categories can be applied to the guide. To create a category, click on the link icon and switch to the<strong> Categories </strong>menu item (<strong>Libraries</strong>).</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><img src="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/21015166720402" alt="Categories.png"></p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">The selected categories will be available in the guide setup. To assign a question to a category, you have to switch to the <strong>Questionnaire Editor</strong> of the PE project. Open the configuration menu of the question and select the desired category.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><img src="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/21015166717970" alt="Questionaire Editor Categories.png"></p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Then switch to <strong>Project Properties</strong> and click on the <strong>Template Configuration</strong> menu item. In the "Question Preview in Guide Setup" area, you can now add the question to which you have assigned the category and click <strong>Save</strong>. All questions of the questionnaire can be selected.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><img src="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/21015166716178" alt="Question's Preview.png"></p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>Multilingualism (categories)</strong></p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">The labels of the guide categories can be displayed in the e-mail conversation of your guide via the <em>#guidewave_category_label# </em>placeholder. They can also be displayed in the language preferred by the participant. You only need to create the labels of the guide categories in the language preferred by the participant.</p>
<h3 id="questionnaire" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>Questionnaire</strong></h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">On the <strong>Questionnaire</strong> tab, you can select the questionnaire. You can also decide whether additional questions should be shown in the guide setup. The guide initiator can then activate these additional questions to dive deeper.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><img src="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/21015136661522" alt="Questionnaire.png"></p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">To provide these additional questions, you must first create a context variable. Switch to the <strong>Template Configuration</strong> menu item. In the "Context Variables" area, create a context variable by clicking on <strong>Create Context Variables</strong> and selecting the type checkbox. Enter a name such as "Deep Dive".</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><img src="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/21015136660114" alt="Context Variables.png"></p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Then open the questionnaire editor of your PE project. Create a filter and its condition for the “Deep Dive” questions. In order to specify the filter condition, you must select the context variable created as a variable in the filter definition, set the condition to <strong>greater than or equal</strong> <strong>to,</strong> and the code to 1.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><img src="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/21015136658066" alt="Filter Definition.png"></p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Now, the additional questions are ready to use. On the <strong>Questionnaire</strong> tab, you can also enter a description of the context variable. You can use a slide switch to determine whether the additional questions should be enabled by default. If you activate this option, the additional questions will be part of the survey.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">After you have published the guide, you will not be able to select another questionnaire. The selection will be final.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>Multilingualism</strong></p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">In order to send out the survey in the language preferred by the participants, switch to the Language editor and define a language selection variable like <em>u_wave_locale</em>. If participants have been assigned one of the standard languages via <em>Locale</em>, the survey is sent out in the assigned language. Another prerequisite is that the survey is available in the language.</p>
