 the Sales Representative's response, either extend the organization's expiry date or initiate the deletion process:</p>

<h4>Step 6a: Extend organization</h4>
<ul>
  <li>Edit the Expiry Date in EFS</li>
  <li>Make a note for documentation (e.g., TIVI-20, 04.06.2021, extended by 1 year, fwo)</li>
  <li>Save correspondence with Sales in the ticket</li>
</ul>

<h4>Step 6b: Delete organization</h4>
<ul>
  <li>Save correspondence with Sales in the ticket</li>
  <li>Open a new ticket for Sabine</li>
  <li>Sabine will delete the organization on the date specified by Sales</li>
  <li>Link the new ticket to the ticket with the Sales Representative</li>
</ul>

<p> </p>

<h2 id="close-tickets">Close Tickets</h2>
<h3>Step 7</h3>
<p>Once you have received definitive feedback on each organization and implemented the necessary actions, close the ticket with the Sales Representative.</p>

<h3>Step 8</h3>
<p>After receiving feedback from all Sales Representatives and extending or deleting the organizations accordingly, close the collective ticket.</p>

<p>Hello,</p>

If you want me to <strong>delete</strong> an organisation, please tell me when to delete it.</p>

<p>All users of the organisation can no longer log in to the named installation.</p>
<p>Best regards,<br>
[Your Name]</p>
