strong> menu item.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-renderer-start-pos="1252">In the upper part of the screen, a graphic shows all emails sent for the given project. In the bottom left part, you can see the total number of emails sent – in the below example, it’s 6. To the right, you can see the different statuses of the mails. In our example below, 5 emails have been sent and one is queued, i.e. it has been created, but not been sent yet. This information is also presented on a percentage basis in the form of colored bars.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><img src="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/21013837923474"></p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-renderer-start-pos="1252">In the lower part of the page, a table lists all mailing processes for your project.</p>
<ul class="ak-ul wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-indent-level="1">
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="1792">Click on the internal ID or on the subject of the mailing process to display details on all individual mails sent.<img src="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/21013837939346"></p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="1792">In the lower part of the detail page for the selected mailing process, a list of all email recipients is displayed. The drop-down menu in the <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Display</strong> line allows you to filter the mails by status (Queued, Sending, Sent, Rejected, Delivery failed, Not reachable, Cancelled).</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="1792">If you would like to display the mail content for the mailing process, click on the <strong data-renderer-mark="true">E-mail view</strong> button in the upper right corner.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="1792">By clicking the arrow button, you will be directed back to the summary page.<img src="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/21013850172178"></p>
</li>
</ul>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-renderer-start-pos="1792">For queued mailings, you can intervene in the delivery process on the summary page. Just proceed as follows:</p>
<ul class="ak-ul wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-indent-level="1">
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="2520">If you would like to stop the distribution of mail, click on the <em data-renderer-mark="true">Play</em> symbol in the <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Queued</strong> column. In case you want to resume the distribution of the mail and put it back in the queue, click on the <em data-renderer-mark="true">Pause</em> symbol.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="2733">To delete a mailing process from the queue, click on the <em data-renderer-mark="true">Cross</em> symbol.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="2808">If you would like to change the date and time for the mail delivery, click on the <em data-renderer-mark="true">Calendar </em>symbol in the <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Date</strong> Column. Select the desired date and time from the two drop-down menus and click on <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Change date</strong>.</p>
</li>
</ul>
