Synchronized users could be activated directly or later.</p><p>Directly activating users has the drawback that users might receive an activation e-mail before Questback Access is enabled. <li><p>User Sync with activation: Users will be synchronized to Okta and activated. Users that have been previously synchronized without activation will get activated.</p></li>
