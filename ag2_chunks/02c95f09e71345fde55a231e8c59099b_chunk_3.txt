<h3 id="Accessing-the-Reporting-Functions-of-a-Project" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-renderer-start-pos="4543"><strong data-renderer-mark="true">Accessing the Reporting Functions of a Project</strong></h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-renderer-start-pos="4591">Starting from the project list, there are two possible ways to enter the reporting functions of a specific project:</p>
<ul class="ak-ul wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-indent-level="1">

<p data-renderer-start-pos="4710">Via the icons in the right-hand column, you can directly access the reporting functions.</p>

</li>
</ul>
