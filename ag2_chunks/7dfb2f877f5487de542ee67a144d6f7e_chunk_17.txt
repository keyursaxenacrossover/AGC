</div>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-renderer-start-pos="14841">You can edit the privacy consent form in the questionnaire editor to use it for the current project. Before the survey will start, the consent form is going to be presented to the participants.</p>
<h3 id="The-sections-of-the-consent-form" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-renderer-start-pos="15033">The sections of the consent form</h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-renderer-start-pos="15067"><strong data-renderer-mark="true">Survey language and welcome message</strong></p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-renderer-start-pos="15104">Use the drop-down list to select a language. The default language is the default language of the survey. You create a welcome message, for which placeholders and known formats can be used.</p>
<ul class="ak-ul wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-indent-level="1">
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="15296">In the section "Survey language" you generate a record, which allows you as the data protection officer or the data protection officer to print out and archive the consent form together with the entries in the configured languages. In this print version, additional information such as comments, date, and options can be ticked.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="15626">Use the<strong data-renderer-mark="true"> Preview</strong> button to call the questionnaire preceded by the consent form.<img src="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/21017221239826"></p>
</li>
</ul>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="15626"><strong data-renderer-mark="true">The selection of the consent form template</strong></p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-renderer-start-pos="15755">You use one of the templates created in <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Libraries</strong> by selecting one of these consent form templates via the drop-down list. The selection of the templates that are displayed to you depends on the language you have previously selected. The <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Manage templates</strong> link takes you to the <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Libraries</strong> where the consent forms created are managed.</p>
