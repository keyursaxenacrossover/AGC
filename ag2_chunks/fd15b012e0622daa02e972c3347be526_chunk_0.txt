<h2 id="accessing_user_account">Accessing Your User Account</h2>
<p>There are two ways to access your user account settings:</p>
<ul>
<li>Navigate to <strong>User</strong> → <strong>Own user account</strong> in the main menu.</li>
<li>Click on your <strong>Username</strong> in the top right corner, then select <strong>Edit account</strong>.</li>
</ul>
<p>From here, you can:</p>
<ul>
<li>Update your address data and security question under <strong>User data</strong>.</li>
<li>Change your password using the <strong>Password</strong> menu item.</li>
</ul>

<li>Save last entries in search screen</li>
<li>Time zone</li>
<li>Standard directory for SAS exports and SAS library</li>
<li>Size of preview window</li>

<h2 id="time_zone_settings">Time Zone Settings</h2>
<p>You can set your preferred time zone, which will be used throughout the entire software:</p>
<h3 id="h_01J361JMJ3CRA19131ZBME87P8">Setting Your Time Zone</h3>
<ol>
<li>Go to <strong>Options</strong> → <strong>Personal Settings</strong> → <strong>Common Settings</strong>
</li>
<li>Choose a time zone by either:
<ul>
<li>Selecting it on the map</li>
<li>Clicking the <strong>Detect time zone</strong> button for automatic detection</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ol>
<p>Important notes about time zone settings:</p>
<ul>
<li>The selected time zone will be applied across the entire software.</li>
<li>Data export and service layer will continue to use UTC/GMT for technical reasons.</li>
</ul>

<h1 id="h_01HGKSMPRSER9ENY5QWWRZZ863">FAQ</h1>
<h3 id="faq_change_password">How do I change my password?</h3>
<p>To change your password, access your user account settings and select the <strong>Password</strong> menu item. Follow the prompts to set a new password.</p>
<h3 id="faq_language_change">Can I change the language of the interface?</h3>
<p>Yes, you can change the language in the <strong>Personal preferences</strong> under <strong>Common settings</strong>. Select your preferred language from the available options.</p>
<h3 id="faq_time_zone_effect">How does changing my time zone affect my projects?</h3>
<p>Changing your time zone will affect how dates and times are displayed throughout the software. However, it won't affect the actual data in your projects, as exports and the service layer use UTC/GMT.</p>
