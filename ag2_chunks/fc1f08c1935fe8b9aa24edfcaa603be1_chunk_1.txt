<h2 id="ticketing_procedure">Ticketing Procedure between IT Security and Support</h2>

<h3>Case 1: IT Security → Technical Support</h3>
<ol>
  <li>IT Security opens an ITS Ticket</li>
  <li>For Tenable Vulnerability scan findings, forward the notification email to support@tivian.com</li>
  <li>For other cases, IT Security opens a TIVI Ticket and links the ITS Ticket as an internal note</li>
  <li>Support proceeds with the TIVI ticket and leaves an internal note in the ITS ticket after completion</li>
</ol>

<h3>Case 2: Technical Support → IT Security</h3>
<ol>
  <li>Technical Support opens a TIVI Ticket</li>
  <li>Technical Support opens an ITS Ticket and links the TIVI Ticket as an internal note</li>
  <li>IT Security proceeds with the ITS ticket and leaves an internal note in the TIVI ticket after completion</li>
</ol>

<h3 id="faq_1">How should 1st Level Support handle Tenable scan tickets?</h3>
<p>
  For Tenable scan tickets:
  </p>
<ul>
    <li>Tickets with prefix "[Intra]" should be forwarded to 3rd level support</li>
    <li>Tickets with prefix "[TS]" should be forwarded to 2nd level support</li>
  </ul>
  Set priority based on the highest severity of the finding:
  <ul>
    <li>Critical → Blocker</li>
    <li>High → High</li>
    <li>Medium → Medium</li>
  </ul>
