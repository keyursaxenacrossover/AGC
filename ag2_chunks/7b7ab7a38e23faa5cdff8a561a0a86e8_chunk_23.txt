<h3 id="stars-rating">Stars Rating</h3>
<p>The question type “Stars rating” allows us to easily visualize answer options, by integrating e.g. smileys, thumbs for Yes/No questions, dice, numbers, gender icons, or other visual elements.</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Depending on the screen width of the device used, the star rating scale will be displayed horizontally or vertically. Optionally vertical alignment can be enforced for all devices.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>When creating a new question, smileys are integrated automatically.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>The rating direction is from left to right.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>You can resort to other predefined images in the Tivian library or upload your images to the media library.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Technically, the new question type is based on the familiar single response list (111).</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>3 - 10 answer options are recommendable for methodic reasons. Technically, up to 20 stars can be displayed.</p>
</li>
</ul>
