<h2 id="subnavigation-look-feel" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><span style="color: #091e42;"><strong><span class="auto-cursor-target" style="color: #091e42;">Subnavigation: Look &amp; Feel</span></strong></span></h2>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><span style="color: #091e42;"><span class="auto-cursor-target" style="color: #091e42;"><span style="color: #091e42;">Let the design of your portal express your corporate identity or reflect your concerns by adding certain colors as primary and font colors or adding an image as a logo. <span style="color: #091e42;">These adjustments can be made when opening the sub-navigation field </span><strong>Look &amp; Fee</strong><span style="color: #091e42;">l. The overall color scheme is designed to offer slightly varying colors for specific elements to create a more dynamic outlook and will be automatically defined according to your desired primary color.</span></span></span></span></p>
<h3 id="editing-color-scheme" class="sc-eNNmBn lmEGgd wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>Editing color scheme</strong></h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><span style="color: #091e42;">Adding specific colors to your portal‘s font and elements using a primary color will add a personal touch to your portals. Once you have opened the subnavigation </span><strong>Look &amp; Feel</strong><span style="color: #091e42;"><strong> </strong>you will find the possibility to adjust the primary color, font color, and profile color of your portal.</span></p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><img src="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/21015039223314" alt="Colors.png"></p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Change colors of either field by</p>
<ul class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">
<li>selecting any color from the color palette opening from the drop-down menu</li>
<li>adding the exact hex color code of a specific color into the input field next to it</li>
</ul>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Clicking <strong>Publish colors</strong> will change the colors of all elements affected (either the font or primary color or both) immediately for all pages and content of your portal.</p>
