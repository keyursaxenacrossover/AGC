p>
<p>If you do not select any additional information for transmission to the tableau server, the email address, panelist names and panelists IDs are transmitted.</p>

<h1 id="publish-a-project"><strong>Publish a Project</strong></h1>
</div>
<p>After you have made all the settings in the sub-navigation field <strong>Settings</strong>, you switch back to the sub-navigation field <strong>Overview</strong>. You can check the settings on the information board before you click the green button to publish the project.</p>
<p><img src="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/21015311970450" alt="publish_EN.png"></p>
<ul class="ak-ul">
<li>
<p>How long it takes to transfer the data depends on the size of the project and the amount of data selected.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>While the data is still being synchronized, you can start working in <em>Tableau</em>.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>New survey data from active <em>EFS</em> projects that have been published as <em>MySight</em> projects are automatically collected in the background and periodically transferred.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>If you do not unlock the <em>MySight </em>project by clicking on the U-lock icon, you can only make changes under the tab Access Control after a project has been published. You can both remove the already added access group and add new ones. After the changes have been made, you must synchronize the project again.</p>
<p> </p>
<div class="fabric-editor-block-mark sc-ixltIz dsUKKC">
<h2 id="after-publishing-a-project"><strong>After Publishing a Project</strong></h2>
</div>
<p>After the project has been published, the Dashboard and Sheet appear to the right of the information board. </p>
<p><img src="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/21015311968914" alt="dashboard_sheet_EN.png"></p>
<ul class="ak-ul">
<li>
<p>With a click on it, you can switch directly to the workbook in <em>Tableau</em>. All you need to do is complete the login dialog for <em>Tableau</em>.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>Data entered by participants marked as testers or from a test project will not be transferred to <em>Tableau</em> by <em>MySight</em>. This also applies if the project has completed participation (Disposition code 31 or 32).</p>
<p> </p>
