<p data-renderer-start-pos="2848">The questionnaire editor has three levels: the <em data-renderer-mark="true">questionnaire view</em>, the<em data-renderer-mark="true"> page view,</em> and the <em data-renderer-mark="true">detail view</em>.</p>
<ul class="ak-ul" data-indent-level="1">
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="2953">You’re presently on level 1, the <em data-renderer-mark="true">questionnaire view</em>. It provides an overview on the questionnaire, including all pages, the key routing elements, and (if they exist) questionnaire branches. Optionally, the questions on the pages can be displayed, too.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="3207">If you click on a page title in the <em data-renderer-mark="true">questionnaire view</em>, you get to level 2, the <em data-renderer-mark="true">list view</em>. It shows a list of all questions on the selected page.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="3356">If you click on a question title in the <em data-renderer-mark="true">page view</em>, you get to level 3, the <em data-renderer-mark="true">detail view</em>. It shows all the details for the selected question, including all texts and configuration options.<img src="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/21018333401362"></p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><strong style="font-size: 1.2em;" data-renderer-mark="true">Creating the First Questionnaire Page</strong></p>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="3586">At the beginning of the survey, you want to welcome the participants. Therefore the first page does not contain any questions but a welcome text.</p>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="3733"><strong data-renderer-mark="true">To create the first questionnaire page:</strong></p>
<ul class="ak-ul" data-indent-level="1">
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="3776">You have already entered the questionnaire view via the <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Questionnaire editor</strong> menu item. Click on the <strong data-renderer-mark="true">+ Page</strong> button.<img src="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/21018333403154"></p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="3776">Enter a title – e.g. “First page”. Activate the option “Jump to next page after creation”.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="3776">Click on <strong data-renderer-mark="true">OK</strong>.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="3776">The new page is created. You are automatically directed to the list view.<img src="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/21018333404690"></p>
</li>
</ul>
