<h3 id="Panelist-Compliance-Index" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-renderer-start-pos="25317"><strong data-renderer-mark="true">Panelist Compliance Index</strong></h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-renderer-start-pos="25344">With the Panelist Compliance Index (PCI) you can capture the reliability of panelists and store this information in a variable. By default, EFS contains the PCI codes "Green" to "Black". However, you can change these codes as you wish by deleting existing codes and adding new codes. By default, new panelists start with the code "Green".</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-renderer-start-pos="25685">PCI codes can be managed under <strong data-renderer-mark="true">People &gt; Panel configuration &gt; PCI configuration</strong> under the <strong data-renderer-mark="true">PCI codes</strong> tab.</p>
