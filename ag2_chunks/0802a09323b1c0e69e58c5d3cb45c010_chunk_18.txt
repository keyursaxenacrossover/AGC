<h3 id="the-guide-process" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>The Guide Process</strong></h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">By clicking on the <strong>Confirm &amp; Send </strong>button the invitations will be sent. The participants can access the survey. The guide status of each started guide can be called up via <em>Guide Waves</em>.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><img src="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/21015166670482" alt="Leadership Guide.png"></p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Using the 3-point menu, you can remind the group, end the survey, or view the participant list. In the case of the <em>Leadership-Guide</em> (ME-Guide), reminder e-mails can only be sent once per rater-group. This also applies to reminder e-mails sent automatically. Therefore, once a reminder e-mail has been sent - manually or automatically - you cannot send another reminder e-mail.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">An hour after participants have completed the survey, the <strong>Summary</strong> button will be enabled and the results email sent.</p>
<h3 id="the-phases-of-the-ideation-guide" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>The Phases of the Ideation Guide</strong></h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">After the participants of the Ideation-Guide have been invited, the first phase of the IDEA-Guide is completed. Then the second phase begins.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>Second phase: Making suggestions</strong></p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">In the second phase, the participants make suggestions according to the categories given. The survey can be started by clicking the <strong>Suggest</strong> button.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><img src="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/21015166665362" alt="Ideation Guide Second Phase.png"></p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Via the 3-point menu, you can also start the upcoming phase, call up the list of participants, call up the summary of the IDEA-Guide, or end the phase.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>Third phase: Grouping suggestions</strong></p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">In the third phase, the grouping phase, the suggestions made will be grouped. This will be done by the guide initiator. To start the grouping phase, the <strong>Group </strong>button must be clicked.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><img src="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/21015136604562" alt="Grouping Phase.png"></p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">On the grouping page, the suggestions made are listed. You can now decide which of the suggestions should be grouped.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><img src="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/21015166658706" alt="Grouping.png"></p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">First, check the suggestions that you want to group. Then assign an appropriate title for this group. If you find other suggestions that you want to group, repeat the procedure.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><img src="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/21015136598290" alt="Grouping 2.png"></p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">You then release the grouped suggestions for voting, which brings the Ideation Guide into the voting phase.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>Fourth Phase: Voting Phase</strong></p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Once the grouping phase has been completed, the Voting phase is going to start. The participants can vote on the suggestions.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><img src="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/21015166635410" alt="Voting Phase.png"></p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">How many votes are necessary to trigger an action on the <em>Action Board</em> can be configured in the guide configuration. The default value is 3 votes.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>Fifth phase: taking action</strong></p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">The fifth phase, which is the final phase, is called “Taking Actions”. The suggestion that received the most votes is created as an action in the <em>Action Board</em>. By clicking on the Summary button you can still request the most important information about the <em>Ideation Guide</em>.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><img src="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/21015166629394" alt="Summary.png"></p>
