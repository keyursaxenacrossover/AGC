<h3 id="Applying-a-Grouping-Filter" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-renderer-start-pos="10008"><strong data-renderer-mark="true">Applying a Grouping Filter</strong></h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-renderer-start-pos="10036">Our newly created group is still empty. We will now create a grouping filter that defines the conditions under which a panelist will be assigned to the "Car owner" group – namely, when they indicate that they own a car in the master data survey. To do this, proceed as follows:</p>
<ul class="ak-ul wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-indent-level="1">
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="10316">Select the <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Grouping filters</strong> menu item from the menu on the left.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="10384">Click on <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Create filter condition</strong>.</p>
<ul class="ak-ul" data-indent-level="1">
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="10384"><img src="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/21018333463698"></p>
</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li data-renderer-start-pos="10384">Enter a name for your grouping filter. In our case: "Car".</li>
<li data-renderer-start-pos="10384">If necessary, enter a filter description.</li>
<li data-renderer-start-pos="10384">In the <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Filter condition</strong> area, select your master data variable "md_car" from the <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Variable</strong> drop-down menu.</li>
<li data-renderer-start-pos="10384">From the <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Condition</strong> drop-down menu, select the option <strong data-renderer-mark="true">equal</strong>.</li>
<li data-renderer-start-pos="10384">Select the option <strong data-renderer-mark="true">1 (Yes)</strong> from the <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Value</strong> drop-down menu.</li>
<li data-renderer-start-pos="10384">Click on <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Save</strong>.<img src="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/21018302903826">
</li>
</ul>
<p>If a panelist now indicates that they own a car in the master data survey created in the previous step, they will be added to the "Car owner" group.</p>
