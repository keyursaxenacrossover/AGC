<h1 id="h_01J4RMQ7E9HYJE37GR33GT19PV">Overview</h1>
<p>The People module combines a powerful panel management, a content management system for designing the panel website, and the functions of the Enterprise Feedback Suite, the basic platform for performing data collection processes.</p>
<p>You can employ all the tools you already know from EFS in creating, conducting, and evaluating online surveys. People module is designed to manage panels with over 100,000 participants.</p>
<p>For detailed information, please check the<strong> <a href="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/en-us/articles/20229143671058-People">People</a> topic article</strong>.</p>

<h3 id="h_01J4RNPQF9RJGMNKPMF0B1N14T">Additional sub-topics</h3>
<h4 id="h_01J4RNRXF2TWFEHDMCC3FMK2MN"><a href="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/en-us/articles/20229153131026" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Master Data</a></h4>
<p>Master Data in EFS Panel refers to the basic characteristics of panelists that are specified by the panel administrator. This data is stored independently from specific survey projects and is always available for evaluation purposes or for creating subpanels, which are also known as "groups."</p>
