<h2 id="prize_draws" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Prize Draws</h2>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">In the People → Incentive management → Prize draws menu, you will find a complete list of prize draws set up for the panel. The table includes:</p>
<ul class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">
<li>Short description, Date of creation, Last change, Status, Number of prizes, Participating group</li>
</ul>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">You can display or hide columns using the View function and search the table contents using the standard search functions.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>Actions Available:</strong></p>
<ul class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">
<li>
<strong>Create Prize Draw</strong>: Click to open the dialog for creating a new prize draw.</li>
<li>
<strong>Edit Prize Draw</strong>: Click the icon to open the dialog for the selected prize draw.</li>
<li>
<strong>Archive</strong>: Appears once winners have been paid, allowing you to archive the prize draw.</li>
<li>
<strong>Delete Prize Draw</strong>: Click to initiate the removal of the prize draw.</li>
</ul>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>Note:</strong></p>
<ul class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">
<li>Access to the Prize draws menu is protected by the ACL right<em> panelgrat</em>. Read rights are required to access or create new prize draws.</li>
</ul>
