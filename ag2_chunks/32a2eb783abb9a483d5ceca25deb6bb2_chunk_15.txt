<h3 id="incorporating-images" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>Incorporating Images</strong></h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">The <strong>Insert image</strong> icon is located to the right of the “Teaser” and “Content” input fields. Clicking on it will open a pop-up window displaying the <strong>Asset library</strong>. If you wish to incorporate an image, click on the <strong>Insert image</strong> icon. The &lt;img&gt; tag of the image will be inserted into the input field.</p>
