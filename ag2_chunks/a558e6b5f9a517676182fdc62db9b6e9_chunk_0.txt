<h1 id="h_01HGKSMPRS9JVGC6E9EA3DB98K" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Overview</h1>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">This article provides information about Tivian Access, a module designed to increase the security of Tivian applications. It covers the main scenarios for implementation, identity providers and protocols, architectural overview, and frequently asked questions.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"> </p>
<h1 id="h_01HGKSMPRSYYE7GMM7DZ0VCKXK" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Information</h1>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Tivian Access is a security module that can be integrated into existing SSO infrastructure to mitigate bad password habits and enhance the overall security of Tivian applications.</p>

<h2 id="increase-the-security-of-your-tivian-applications" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>Increase the security of your TIVIAN applications</strong></h2>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"> </p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">To increase the security of your <em>TIVIAN</em> applications - after the implementation of the <em>Privacy Assistant</em> (GDPR) - the new <em>TIVIAN Access</em> module has been implemented. By the use of this module, which can be integrated into your existing SSO infrastructure, bad password habits will be mitigated.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"> </p>
