="true">Steps:</strong></p>
<ul class="ak-ul" data-indent-level="1">
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="8874">Click on the title of the final page in the questionnaire view, and create a question of the question type <em data-renderer-mark="true">Text and image (998)</em>.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="9006">Enter your text.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="9026">Click on <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Save</strong>.</p>

<h2 id="After-the-Questionnaire-Creation" data-renderer-start-pos="9045">After the Questionnaire Creation</h2>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="9079">Now your questionnaire is complete – the hard work is done. Although, it wasn’t that hard at all. Before starting your survey, there are just some small tasks, which need to be performed.</p>
<h3 id="Activating-the-Survey" data-renderer-start-pos="9268">Activating the Survey</h3>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="9291">In the <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Survey menu</strong>, adjust the following basic settings:</p>
<ul class="ak-ul" data-indent-level="1">
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="9352">Set the beginning and end of the survey. Just click on the date under <strong data-renderer-mark="true">BEGINNING OF SURVEY/END OF SURVEY</strong>, and select the desired date. Your changes are saved automatically.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="9527">In the “Status” line, you can see that the survey has been created and is currently in the status “Survey compiled”. But it has not been activated yet. Click on the Button <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Active</strong>: the status will change to “active”.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<h3 id="Testing-the-Survey" data-renderer-start-pos="9746">Testing the Survey</h3>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="9766">Before entering into the field, you have to test the questionnaire.</p>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="9835"><strong data-renderer-mark="true">Testing in preview and productive mode</strong></p>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="9875">You have repeatedly used the preview while creating the questionnaire to check completed questions and pages. After the configuration is completed, the whole questionnaire needs to be tested. To do so, change to the <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Survey menu</strong>. In the “Distribution” section, click on the <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Preview questionnaire</strong> icon and check the survey from the point of view of a participant. Go through the questionnaire and check, whether everything works as expected.</p>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="10316">As the preview does not support all features, you should test the questionnaire in the productive mode, too, before finally releasing it. In the <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Survey menu</strong>, click on the icon <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Open survey in a new window</strong> in the line “URL”.<img src="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/21018302873234"></p>
