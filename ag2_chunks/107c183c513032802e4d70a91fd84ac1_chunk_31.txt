<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Consider carefully which survey type fits for your project, and select the appropriate type now at the very start of the setup phase. We strongly advise against a change of survey type at a later date: Depending of the target type already-created data might be deleted.</p>
<ul class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">
<li>
<p>When changing from a paper-pencil or hybrid to an online survey, all paper-pencil questionnaire data will be deleted: they are superfluous in an online survey.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>When changing from an online or hybrid survey to a paper-pencil questionnaire, all data of online participants will be deleted.</p>
</li>
</ul>
