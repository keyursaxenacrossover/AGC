<h3 id="sending-e-mail-push-notification-or-assigning-a-role" class="sc-eNNmBn lmEGgd wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>Sending e-mails, push notifications, or assigning a role</strong></h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><span style="color: #091e42;">On the right, you will find three buttons: </span><strong>Send e-mail</strong><span style="color: #091e42;">, </span><strong>Send push notification,</strong><span style="color: #091e42;"> and </span><strong>Role assignment</strong><span style="color: #091e42;">. <span style="color: #091e42;">The two buttons </span><strong>Send e-mail</strong><span style="color: #091e42;"> and </span><strong>Send push notification</strong><span style="color: #091e42;"> allow you to send an e-mail or a push notification. You use the third button </span><strong>Role Assignment</strong><span style="color: #091e42;"> to assign one of the two roles or both of them to the selected participant(s). The buttons will be activated as soon as you have selected a participant.</span></span></p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><span style="color: #091e42;"><span style="color: #091e42;"><img src="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/21015039219218" alt="Sending an e-mail.png"></span></span></p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>Send an e-mail</strong></p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">After selecting one, several, or all participants, you click on the button <strong>Send e-mail</strong>. A modal opens immediately, with which you specify a type and a template. To specify a type you choose one of the available e-mail types. The following e-mail types are available: <strong>All</strong>, <strong>Default E-Mail</strong>, <strong>Invitation E-Mail</strong>, <strong>Password recovery E-Mail</strong>, and <strong>Welcome mail</strong>. Under "template" you find the templates are created, Templates can be created and edited in the <strong>Library</strong>. To create templates specially for <em>Portals</em> you open the drop-down menu by clicking on the <strong>New draft</strong> arrow and select <strong>New draft for portals</strong>.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>Send a push notification</strong></p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">The button for sending push notifications is only available if the user has already registered for the portal with the app. In addition to that, a claim must have been assigned to the subnavigation field <strong>Settings</strong> under the tab <strong>Mobile Integration</strong>, via which the portal app can be reached. After clicking the button a modal opens and the title and the message text can be entered by you.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>Role assignment</strong></p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">If you want to assign a role, click either the button or the drop-down arrow in each table row. A modal opens immediately, with which one of the two roles or both of them are assigned to the selected participant(s). The roles you can select are <strong>Moderator and Survey Manager</strong>.</p>
