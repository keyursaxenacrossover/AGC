<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-renderer-start-pos="16136"><strong data-renderer-mark="true">Update Rule for the Group "New Employees – Phase 3"</strong></p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-renderer-start-pos="16189">Now we create the update rule for the "New employees – phase 3" group.</p>
<ul class="ak-ul wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-indent-level="1">
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="16263">As described under <a class="cc-1rn59kg" title="https://qbdocs.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/DOC/pages/edit-v2/4149608460?draftShareId=77f2e92c-cc13-4bb3-abc2-2cbc316317bb#Update-rules" href="#:~:text=Temporarily%20(1).-,Update%20Rules,-Update%20rules%20ensure" data-testid="link-with-safety" data-renderer-mark="true">Update rules</a>, create a new update rule with the name "New employees – phase 3" under <strong data-renderer-mark="true">People &gt; Groups &gt; Update rules</strong>. Apply the same settings as for the update rule "New employees – phase 2".</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="16476">Now define the required actions:</p>
</li>
</ul>
<ol class="ak-ol wysiwyg-text-align-justify" start="1" data-indent-level="1">
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="16516"><strong data-renderer-mark="true"><u data-renderer-mark="true">Empty group</u></strong></p>
</li>
</ol>
<ul class="ak-ul wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-indent-level="1">
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="16533">Proceed as described above.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<ol class="ak-ol wysiwyg-text-align-justify" start="2" data-indent-level="1">
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="16568"><strong data-renderer-mark="true"><u data-renderer-mark="true">Apply grouping filter</u></strong></p>
</li>
</ol>
<ul class="ak-ul wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-indent-level="1">
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="16595">Proceed as described above and select the created grouping filter <strong data-renderer-mark="true">New employees – phase 3</strong> in the <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Apply grouping filter</strong> line.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<ol class="ak-ol wysiwyg-text-align-justify" start="3" data-indent-level="1">
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="16727"><strong data-renderer-mark="true"><u data-renderer-mark="true">Add to sample</u></strong></p>
</li>
</ol>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-renderer-start-pos="16744">In phase 3, the employee should receive the survey relating to the end of their onboarding. They must therefore also be automatically added to the sample for this survey.</p>
<ul class="ak-ul wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-indent-level="1">
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="16918">Proceed as described above and select the survey that employees should receive after 6 months in the job under <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Value</strong> in the <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Project/Sample</strong> line. Then, for <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Mail template for invitation mail (optional)</strong>, select the invitation to the survey that you have created.<img src="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/21014859767186"></p>
</li>
</ul>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-renderer-start-pos="17184">By defining the update rules you have completed the preparations for your automated workflow for new employees. As soon as you add a new employee to your panel, they will automatically receive the welcome email you have created and will be invited to take part in a survey after 2 and additional 4 months in the job. This allows you to keep track of your employees' satisfaction with the onboarding process in your company and – thanks to automation – you can archive this with far less effort.</p>
