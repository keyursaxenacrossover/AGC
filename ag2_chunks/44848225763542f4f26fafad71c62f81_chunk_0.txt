<p>This article explains how to prevent participants from printing a questionnaire page or downloading images in a survey project. It covers the steps to disable the context menu and discusses additional considerations for content protection.</p>
<h1 id="h_01HGKSMPRSYYE7GMM7DZ0VCKXK">Information</h1>
<p>Learn how to protect your survey content by disabling printing and image downloading capabilities for participants.</p>

<h2 id="h_01HGKSMPRT0YJXQ3QJXD5XBPNM">Disabling Context Menu</h2>
<p>To prevent participants from printing a questionnaire page or downloading images, you can disable the context menu for the entire questionnaire. Here's how to do it:</p>
<h3 id="h_01HGKSMPRTGJ8QXPJ8X3KP9QNW">Step 1: Access Project Properties</h3>
<p>Navigate to Projects → Survey menu → Project properties</p>
<h3 id="h_01HGKSMPRTMJ8QXPJ8X3KP9QNW">Step 2: Locate the Setting</h3>
<p>Look for the option to disable the context menu. This setting is primarily used to protect content by deactivating the context menu throughout the entire questionnaire.</p>
<p><img src="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/21015359005074"></p>
<h2 id="h_01HGKSMPRT8YJGQX3JXBKP9QNW">Additional Considerations</h2>
<p>When using this feature to protect your survey content, keep the following points in mind:</p>
<ul>
<li>Keyboard commands will not be suppressed by this setting.</li>
<li>For page-specific protection, you can use a question of the type "Text and image" to achieve similar behavior on a particular questionnaire page.</li>
<li>This feature can also be used to prevent the usage of the browser's Back button. To do this, the survey must be opened in a pop-up window without a menu bar.</li>
</ul>
<h1 id="h_01HGKSMPRSER9ENY5QWWRZZ863">FAQ</h1>
<h3 id="h_01HGKSMPRSQR9ENY5QWWRZZ864">Can participants still use keyboard shortcuts to print or save images?</h3>
<p>Yes, keyboard commands are not suppressed by this setting. Additional measures may be needed to prevent keyboard-based actions.</p>
<h3 id="h_01HGKSMPRSWR9ENY5QWWRZZ865">Is it possible to disable printing for only specific pages of the questionnaire?</h3>
<p>Yes, you can use a question of the type "Text and image" to implement similar protection for a particular questionnaire page.</p>
<h3 id="h_01HGKSMPRSZR9ENY5QWWRZZ866">How can I prevent participants from using the browser's Back button?</h3>
<p>To prevent the use of the browser's Back button, open the survey in a pop-up window without a menu bar and use the context menu disabling feature.</p>
