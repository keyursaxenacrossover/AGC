<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Changes to address or master data, whether made by panelists or moderators, can be logged and viewed for up to 60 days (this period can be adjusted by support).</p>

<h2 id="h_01HGKSMPRS5YJGQX3YBVHXKP6N" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Candidates</h2>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">The basic version of EFS Panel uses a two-stage entry procedure. In the first stage, potential participants register and are admitted temporarily. They are then invited to complete the master data survey by the panel administrator. The process involves three steps:</p>
<ol class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">
<li>
<p><strong>Review and Validation</strong>:</p>
<ul>
<li>Validate data and ensure the candidate is a real person with a functional email address.</li>
<li>Perform plausibility checks and duplicate checks during registration.</li>
<li>Ensure contact address and other data are complete.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Processing</strong>:</p>
<ul>
<li>Reject temporarily registered panelists or make further inquiries if needed.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Invitation to the Master Data Survey</strong>:</p>
<ul>
<li>Invite candidates to complete the master data survey to transition to fully valid panelists.</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ol>
<h3 id="h_01J3FY7CT3J200WKA5Z8ZZ4A85" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Validating Panelists</h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">The administrator’s central task is to validate panelists to ensure they are real individuals with valid email addresses, and to exclude multiple participations. EFS Panel supports quality assurance through:</p>
<ul class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">
<li>Plausibility checks during registration.</li>
<li>Immediate check for existing email addresses in the system.</li>
<li>An overview list of newly registered panelists.</li>
<li>Optional automated duplicates check.</li>
</ul>
