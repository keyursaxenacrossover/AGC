<h2 id="header-29">Privacy Assistant (GDPR)</h2>
<h3 id="header-31">Automatic deletion of invited panelists</h3>
<p>In the<strong> Panel configuration</strong> of the <em>People</em> module you have the new menu item "Automatic deletion of invited panelists". Now you can automate the deletion of both the <em>Tell-a-friend</em>-panelists (module: <em>Panel Website</em>) and the panelists with panel status (module: <em>Portals</em>).</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p><strong>Tell a friend:</strong> You check the option "Delete invited panellists without reaction after" and set the number of years, months or days after which the invited panellists who did not respond to the invitation should be deleted.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Panelist with panel status:</strong> You check the option "Delete invited panelists without response after" and set the number of years, months or days after which the invited panelists who did not respond to the invitation should be deleted.</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>In addition, you select the panel statuses of the panelists to be deleted.</p>
</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>

</li>
