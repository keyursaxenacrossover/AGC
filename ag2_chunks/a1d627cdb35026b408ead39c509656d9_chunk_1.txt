<h3 id="faq_password_change">How can I change the password for the Sales Account?</h3>
<p>You can change the password by requesting a reset link via the login page. Since the account owner usually remains the same, this process should not cause any issues.</p>

<h3 id="faq_account_limitations">What are the limitations of the Sales Account?</h3>
