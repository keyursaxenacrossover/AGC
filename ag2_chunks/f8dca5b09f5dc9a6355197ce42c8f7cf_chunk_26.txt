<h3 id="Uploading-a-new-file" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-renderer-start-pos="21538"><strong data-renderer-mark="true">Uploading a new file</strong></h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-renderer-start-pos="21560">You may upload files of any format, except for PHP and HTML. The maximum possible file size of the respective installation is displayed in the import dialog.
