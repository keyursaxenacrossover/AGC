The options Anonymous survey and <em data-renderer-mark="true">Personalized survey </em>are available on all installations. Depending on your license, you may also have access to the options<em data-renderer-mark="true"> Employee survey, Multi-source feedback, Panel survey</em> and <em data-renderer-mark="true">Master data survey</em>. Detailed information on the different project types can be found <a class="cc-1rn59kg" title="https://qbdocs.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/DOC/pages/1425702932/Projects#Project-Types" href="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/en-us/articles/20370699710866-Projects#project-types" data-testid="link-with-safety" data-renderer-mark="true">here</a>.</p>

<li>

<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="1451">Select a layout template: Just keep the default setting as it is. This ensures the usage of a modern layout, which adapts automatically to the device used by the survey participants.</p>

<h3 id="Creating-the-Questionnaire" data-renderer-start-pos="1940">Creating the Questionnaire</h3>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="1968">After the project creation, the functional area <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Project Information</strong> of the <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Survey menu</strong> is displayed. This is your central starting point for editing your project. It provides access to all relevant submenus. Open the<strong data-renderer-mark="true"> Questionnaire editor</strong> to start creating the questionnaire.<img src="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/21018333419666"></p>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="1968">For your sample questionnaire, only the final page has been created automatically so far. You will soon create all other pages yourself. If you have activated the privacy assistant, you have to configure the Data retention period and the Consent form before you can activate your survey. To do so, click on the two red highlighted links, and make the appropriate changes. Detailed information on the privacy assistant and information on how to configure the Data retention period and the Consent form can be found under <a class="cc-1rn59kg" style="background-color: #ffffff;" title="/wiki/spaces/DOC/pages/1193443341" href="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/en-us/articles/20229434833554-How-to-Privacy-Assistant-GDPR" data-testid="link-with-safety" data-renderer-mark="true">How-to: Privacy Assistant (GDPR)</a>.<img src="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/21018333421586"></p>
