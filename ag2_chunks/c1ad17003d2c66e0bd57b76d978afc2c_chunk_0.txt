<h1 id="h_01HGKSMPRS9JVGC6E9EA3DB98K" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Overview</h1>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">This article provides a comprehensive guide on using the Standard Editor to customize the layout and appearance of questionnaires. It covers various aspects such as logos, page settings, fonts, text formatting, form elements, and progress bar configuration.</p>
<h1 id="h_01HGKSMPRSYYE7GMM7DZ0VCKXK" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Information</h1>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">The Standard Editor offers a wide range of customization options to tailor the look and feel of your questionnaires. This guide will walk you through each section of the editor, explaining the available settings and their impact on the final questionnaire design.</p>

<h2 id="logos" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Logos</h2>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">On the <strong>Logos</strong> tab, you can upload your logo. The color picker checks the uploaded logos automatically and offers fitting color suggestions for texts, form elements, etc. If you want to remove a logo, click on the <strong>Delete</strong> button.</p>

<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">You will need the logos in a web-enabled format, i.e. GIF (preferably for drawings and figures with few colors), JPEG (preferably photos, and figures with many colors), or PNG. Pay attention to the file size. All images in one questionnaire page should under no circumstances exceed 30 to 40 kilobytes.</p>
