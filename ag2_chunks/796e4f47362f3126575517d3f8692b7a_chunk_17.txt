<h3 id="continuation-with-scale" class="sc-lhVmIH iJLaKb"><strong>Continuation with Scale</strong></h3>
<p>The path does not have to end after you have decided on a question or a dimension item. The element<strong>Scale</strong>provides you with a continuation option so that you move on the path which you have already continued with <strong>Dimension</strong>. Therefore you pick an item x of the scale property or a result type.</p>
<p>By means of this branching, your results achieve accuracy, because you not only take the questions into account, as is possible with the continuation by question or dimensional expression, but also evaluate the answer page in a differentiated way.</p>
<p><img src="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/21013838049298" alt="Continuation with scale.png"></p>
<p>You pick:</p>
<p><em>Unit</em> → [<em>Unit element</em>] → <em>Dimension</em> → [<em>Question text/Dimension item</em>] → <em>Scale</em> → [<em>Scale </em><em>item</em>]</p>
<p>You continue with scale properties and result types:</p>
<ul>
<li>Code</li>
<li>Label</li>
<li>N</li>
<li>N as percent</li>
</ul>
<p>If you continue with a scale property or a result type, the term is immediately valid.</p>
<p>Another branching is not possible.</p>
