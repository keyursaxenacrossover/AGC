<h1 id="h_01HGKSMPRSER9ENY5QWWRZZ863" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">FAQ</h1>
<h3 id="h_01HGKSMPRV9YJQX8QJXBXP3ZQY" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">What is the Panelist Compliance Index (PCI)?</h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">The Panelist Compliance Index (PCI) is a measure of panelist reliability in EFS. It uses color codes (e.g., Green, Yellow, Red) to classify panelists based on their participation and behavior in surveys.</p>
<h3 id="h_01HGKSMPRVCYJQX8QJXBXP3ZQZ" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">How often should I update PCI values?</h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">The frequency of PCI updates depends on your panel's activity level. For most panels, updating every 4 weeks is sufficient, but you can adjust this based on your specific needs.</p>
<h3 id="h_01HGKSMPRVEYJQX8QJXBXP3ZR0" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Can I customize the automated email templates?</h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Yes, you can fully customize all email templates used in automated processes. This allows you to maintain your brand voice and tailor messages to your specific audience.</p>
