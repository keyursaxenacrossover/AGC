<h1 id="h_01HGKSMPRSER9ENY5QWWRZZ863" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">FAQ</h1>
<h3 id="h_01HGKSMPRTG4YJXD1YJXQX3QXF" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">What happens if I activate the Privacy Assistant for an existing project?</h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">The survey status will be set to "No further participation" until you configure the data retention period.</p>
<h3 id="h_01HGKSMPRTJ4YJXD1YJXQX3QXG" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Can I deactivate the Privacy Assistant after setting a project to "Active"?</h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">No, once a project is set to "Active", the Privacy Assistant cannot be deactivated.</p>
