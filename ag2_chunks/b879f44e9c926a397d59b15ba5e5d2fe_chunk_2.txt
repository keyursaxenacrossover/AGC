<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>Allocating access right to survey results</strong></p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">If a task was created by the <em>Task Trigger</em>, a survey link is created in the detail view of this task routing to the results of the respective survey participation. The responsible user of this task has the possibility to open the survey link by clicking on the form icon in the detail view and to access the survey results of the participants. The form icon is not displayed in the detail view by default. <h3 id="activating-deactivating-notifications" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>Activating/Deactivating Notifications</strong></h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">In the Panelist administration, you can set whether participants, being panelists at the same time, receive a push notification on their smartphone as well as on their installation and/or an e-mail notification informing them about new assignments of tasks. The activation of push notifications requires the installation of the <em>Portals</em> app on the participants‘ smartphones.</p>
<ul class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">
<li>
<p>To activate or deactivate notifications for a participant, open the<strong> Panelists administration</strong> in the<em> People</em> module.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Enter the respective participant in the search field and click<strong> Search</strong>.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Select the participant by clicking on their name.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>The tab<strong> Detail view</strong> of the participant‘s account is opened.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>In the section <strong>Notifications</strong>, you can activate or deactivate the<strong> Push notifications for tasks</strong> and / or the<strong> E-mail notifications for tasks</strong> via the respective checkboxes.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>To save your changes, click on the button <strong>Change account</strong>.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">You have changed the settings of Notifications for the selected participant.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">To display a list of all created participants / panelists from which you can select the desired participant, enter “@” in the search field and click <strong>Search</strong>.</p>
