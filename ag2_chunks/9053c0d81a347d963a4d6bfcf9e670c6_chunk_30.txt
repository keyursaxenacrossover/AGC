<h3 id="h_01J3FYDYN1VG2V70B1WSCFDHJR">Access Rights to Configure the Duplicates Check</h3>
<ul>
<li>
<strong>Write Rights:</strong> Required for “panel_dupcheck” to configure the duplicates check.</li>
<li>Without this right, the <strong>People → Panelists administration → Duplicates check</strong> menu is not visible.</li>
<li>
<strong>Protection:</strong> Prevents accidental overwriting of data by new users, as the duplicates check applies to all websites of an installation.</li>
</ul>

<h1 id="h_01HGKSMPRSER9ENY5QWWRZZ863" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">FAQ</h1>
<h3 id="h_01HGKSMPRSAYJGQX3YBVHXKP6N" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">How often is the duplicates check performed?</h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">The duplicates check is performed automatically at hourly intervals once activated.</p>
<h3 id="h_01HGKSMPRSBYJGQX3YBVHXKP6N" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Can I import panelists from an Employee project?</h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Yes, you can import users or participants from Employee projects into the panel. This feature allows you to import Org Processor users and add them to existing or new panel groups.</p>
<h3 id="h_01HGKSMPRSCY8YJXBHXKP6N" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">How can I reset a panelist's two-factor authentication?</h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">If a panelist has set up two-factor authentication, you can reset it by clicking the "Reset 2FA" button in the panelist administration for that specific panelist.</p>
