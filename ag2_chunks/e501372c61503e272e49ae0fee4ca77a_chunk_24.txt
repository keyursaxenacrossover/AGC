-align-justify">Via the icons in the “Actions” column you can edit individual posts:</p>
<ul class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">
<li>
<strong>Edit</strong>: This also opens the dialog for editing the properties of an existing post.</li>
<li>
<strong>Delete</strong>: The selected post will be deleted following a confirmation prompt.</li>
</ul>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">With the “Actions” checkbox and the drop-down list below the table, you can also apply various actions to several posts at once:</p>
<ul class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">
<li>Set release status to “approved” / “not checked” / “blocked”: Changes the release status of the selected posts.</li>
<li>Delete posts: The selected posts will be deleted following a confirmation prompt.</li>
</ul>
