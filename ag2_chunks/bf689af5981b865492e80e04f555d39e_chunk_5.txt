<h3 id="Applying-Filters" data-renderer-start-pos="7739"><strong data-renderer-mark="true">Applying Filters</strong></h3>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="7757">By means of filters, you can restrict the basis of your report – i.e. the number of people to be included in your analysis. In the following, we would like to use two examples to show you in detail how you can apply filters to your entire report or to specific sections of your report.</p>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="8043"><strong data-renderer-mark="true">To apply a filter to the entire report:</strong></p>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="8084">In the following, we will first create a report-wide filter to evaluate only the results of participants, who took part in the survey in a specific time period.</p>
<ul class="ak-ul" data-indent-level="1">
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="8249">Click on <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Report</strong> above the <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Sources</strong> area.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="8292">Select <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Report filter</strong>.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p class="wysiwyg-indent2" data-renderer-start-pos="8292"><img src="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/21015166422290"></p>
<ul>
<li data-renderer-start-pos="8292">Under <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Date of participation</strong>, click in the empty fields and select the desired date and time.</li>
<li data-renderer-start-pos="8292">Then, click on <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Save</strong>.</li>
<li data-renderer-start-pos="8292">For the entire report, only results data from participants, who took part in the survey within the specified time period, will be analyzed.   <img src="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/21015166401554">
</li>
</ul>

<p data-renderer-start-pos="8590"><strong data-renderer-mark="true">To apply a filter to a specific section in the report:</strong></p>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="8646">We now want to create a filter to restrict only the basis of a specific section in the report. For example, for a specific question, we only want to show responses from participants from Germany. We can create this filter because we have activated the checkbox for participant data when creating the report, and the data from the participant administration is therefore available for evaluation.</p>
<ul class="ak-ul" data-indent-level="1">
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="9046">Click on the <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Filter</strong> menu item in the lower part of the <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Sources</strong> area.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="9118">Click on the plus icon.</p>
</li>
</ul>

In the middle, you can select the disposition codes you want to filter by and at the bottom you can filter by the date of participation.</p>
