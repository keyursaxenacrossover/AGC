<h2 id="managing-blogs" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>Managing Blogs</strong></h2>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>Blog list of a section</strong></p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">To view the blogs of a particular section, simply click on the section title in the overview. Alternatively, you can also use the Edit Blogs icon.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">The list will show you all the blogs within the selected section along with their descriptions, names of their authors, number of posts and comments, and other properties.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>Edit options</strong></p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">The blogs are created automatically when the panelists click on a corresponding link on the website. If you want to create additional blogs for moderators or testers, open the corresponding form by clicking on the <strong>Add Blog</strong> button.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Clicking on the title of a blog takes you directly to the individual posts.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Via the icons in the “Actions” column you can edit individual blogs:</p>
<ul class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">
<li>
<strong>Edit settings</strong>: This opens the dialog for editing the blog properties</li>
<li>
<strong>Edit posts</strong>: Opens the list of posts.</li>
<li>
<strong>Export posts</strong>: This triggers the export of all posts in this blog.</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Delete:</strong> The selected blog will be deleted following a confirmation prompt. Please bear in mind that all data will be irretrievably lost. If you do not need a particular blog anymore, you should make use of the possibility to export the posts before deleting them.</p>
</li>
</ul>
