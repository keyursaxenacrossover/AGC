<p>This article explains the validity period of recovery tokens and what happens when they expire.</p>
<p> </p>
<h1 id="h_01HGKSMPRSYYE7GMM7DZ0VCKXK">Information</h1>
<p>Recovery tokens are used to help participants regain access to their accounts. Understanding their expiration time is crucial for a smooth recovery process.</p>

<h2 id="h_01HGKSMPRT1QJXZ3NQJD5XBPEM">Recovery Token Validity</h2>
<p>Recovery tokens have a limited validity period to ensure security. They expire after 2 hours from the time they are requested.</p>
<p> </p>
<h2 id="h_01HGKSMPRT2KGQX6YJHG8YBPNM">Expired Token Behavior</h2>
<p>If participants attempt to use a recovery link that was requested more than 2 hours ago, they will encounter an error message. In this case, they will need to request a new recovery token via email to regain access to their account.</p>
<p> </p>
<h1 id="h_01HGKSMPRSER9ENY5QWWRZZ863">FAQ</h1>
<h3 id="h_01HGKSMPRT3MWQZ7JQJN1X8PVT">How long are recovery tokens valid?</h3>
<p>Recovery tokens are valid for 2 hours from the time they are requested.</p>
<h3 id="h_01HGKSMPRT4NRAX8KQJP2X9QVV">What happens if I try to use an expired recovery token?</h3>
<p>If you try to use an expired recovery token, you will see an error message. You will need to request a new recovery token via email to proceed with account recovery.</p>
<h3 id="h_01HGKSMPRT5PSCY9LQJR3XAQWX">Can I extend the validity of a recovery token?</h3>
<p>No, the 2-hour validity period cannot be extended. If your token expires, you must request a new one.</p>
