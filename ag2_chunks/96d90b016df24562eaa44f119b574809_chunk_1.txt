<h3 id="h_01J3N2HWA9FDHTTRFCWTRTV5B3"><strong>Navigation in the Organizational Structure</strong></h3>
<p>From the start page, go to the <strong>Action Planner</strong> tab. It shows the organizational structure for your area of responsibility.</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>For each unit, information on the status of the follow-up process is displayed, as e.g. status and number of actions.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>If a unit is opened, i.e. its subunits are currently displayed, it is marked by a <strong>[-]</strong> icon. By clicking on the <strong>[-]</strong> icon, the subunits are hidden i.e. the branch is closed.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>If a unit contains additional subunits that are currently not displayed, it is marked by a <strong>[+]</strong> icon. By clicking on the <strong>[+]</strong> icon, you can open the unit, i.e. display its subunits.</p>
</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Display and Editing options</strong></p>
<p>Above the organizational structure, various buttons allow to change the display of the structure or to start import and export:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p><strong>Import actions</strong>: Use this button to open the import dialog</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Export actions</strong>: Use this button to open the export dialog</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Expand all</strong>: All branches will be shown, so you can see all hierarchical levels.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Collapse all</strong>: All branches will be closed, so you can only see the first level of your area of responsibility.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Activate auto expansion</strong>: All subunits will be opened automatically.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>View</strong>: This button opens a dialog that allows to display of additional columns with information or to hide superfluous columns.</p>
</li>
</ul>
