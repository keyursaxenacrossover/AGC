HGKSMPRUFBJ8XQJXF1QXCWN3G" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Exporting Return Statistics</h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">To download the return rates of one or several waves:</p>
<ol class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">

</ol>
<h3 id="h_01HGKSMPRUJBJ8XQJXF1QXCWN4H" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Exporting Results</h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-renderer-start-pos="5773">In addition to the usual EFS result exports, two dedicated MSF-specific result exports are available.</p>
<ul class="ak-ul wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-indent-level="1">
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="5878">MSF participant export with result data:</p>
<ul class="ak-ul" data-indent-level="2">
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="5922">The familiar EFS Survey result data, the feedback identifier (u_link_id), all master data and some selected process data.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="6047">Requires read rights to “loft_export_combined”.</p>
</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="6100">MSF export with anonymous result data:</p>
<ul class="ak-ul" data-indent-level="2">
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="6142">The familiar EFS Survey result data, the feedback identifier (u_link_id), and merely the process data feedback role (role) and wave (u_group).</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="6288">Requires read rights to “loft_export_anonym”.</p>
</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
