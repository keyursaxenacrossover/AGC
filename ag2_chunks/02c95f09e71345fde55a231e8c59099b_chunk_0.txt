<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">This article provides a comprehensive guide on how to use and manage the Survey Status feature in EFS Survey. It covers setting up access, navigating the interface, understanding reporting functions, and managing export data.</p>
<h1 id="h_01HGKSMPRSYYE7GMM7DZ0VCKXK" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Information</h1>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">EFS Survey Status is a separate area that allows staff and customers to view survey results without direct access to internal project settings. This guide explains how to set up and use this feature effectively.</p>

<h2 id="Setting-Up-EFS-Survey-Status-Access" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-renderer-start-pos="593"><strong data-renderer-mark="true">Setting Up EFS Survey Status Access</strong></h2>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-renderer-start-pos="630">If you wish to grant your staff or customers access to <em data-renderer-mark="true">EFS Survey Status</em>, you need a team with appropriate access rights and staff accounts for the desired candidates.</p>
<h3 id="Creating-a-Team" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-renderer-start-pos="799"><strong data-renderer-mark="true">Creating a Team</strong></h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-renderer-start-pos="816">Create a new team (<strong data-renderer-mark="true">System</strong> → <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Users</strong> → <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Teams</strong>). In the field “Access rights to the following areas”, activate the area “EFS Survey Status”: This corresponds to read rights to the <em data-renderer-mark="true">area_www</em> ACL right. Make sure that no other area is activated.</p>
