<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><span style="color: #091e42;">You delete a page by clicking on the trashcan icon. <span style="color: #091e42;">There are two types of pages: </span>Standard pages and Target pages<span style="color: #091e42;">.</span></span></p>
<p class="auto-cursor-target wysiwyg-text-align-justify"> </p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Information</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><span style="color: #333333;">Any standard page can be defined as a target page on the sub-navigation field </span><a class="unresolved" href="https://qbdocs.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/DOC/pages/24478022/Pages+Menu">Settings</a><span style="color: #333333;"> under the tab </span><strong>Page Connections</strong><span style="color: #333333;">. Pages defined as target pages cannot be deleted as they are crucial to intact navigation between pages. If a target page must be deleted it must first be replaced as a target in the general settings menu by defining another page as the target. Once a replacement target page has been defined, the former target page will be displayed as a standard page (if no target is defined for this page) and can be deleted.</span></p>
<h3 id="status" class="auto-cursor-target wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>Status</strong></h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><span style="color: #091e42;">There are two statuses on page level: offline and online. The colored page header informs you about the status of the page you are currently on.</span></p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><span style="color: #091e42;"><img src="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/21015069301010" alt="Offline-Status EN.png"></span></p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><span style="color: #091e42;"><span class="legacy-color-text-red2">Red</span><span style="color: #091e42;"> indicates that the page is offline currently. </span><strong>Offline</strong><span style="color: #091e42;"> means: The page is in the offline mode and cannot be loaded from panelists with access permission.</span></span></p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><span style="color: #091e42;"><span style="color: #091e42;"><img src="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/21015039251730" alt="Online-Status EN.png"></span></span></p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><span style="color: #091e42;"><span style="color: #091e42;"><span class="legacy-color-text-green2">Green</span><span style="color: #091e42;"> indicates that the page is online. </span><strong>Online</strong><span style="color: #091e42;"> means: The page is in live mode and can be loaded from panelists with access permission.</span></span></span></p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><span style="color: #091e42;"><span style="color: #091e42;"><span style="color: #091e42;"><span style="color: #091e42;">You click on the sliding switch to switch the status of your page from offline to online mode and vice versa. You can easily edit a published page in online mode since changes to the page will only be visible when you publish the changes specifically.</span></span></span></span></p>
