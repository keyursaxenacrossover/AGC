<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>Edit options</strong></p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Click on the <strong>Create section</strong> button to open the dialog for creating a new download section.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Click on the title of a download section to open the list of existing files. Click on the title of a file to edit its properties.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Via the icons in the “Actions” column you can edit individual download sections:</p>
<ul class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">
<li>
<strong>Edit</strong>: This opens the dialog for editing the section properties. Table 19.11 provides a detailed explanation of the properties.</li>
<li>
<strong>Edit files</strong>: This opens the list of files in the respective section.</li>
<li>
<strong>Copy</strong>: The desired section will be copied. The files will not be copied, though, as this is usually not very practical.</li>
<li>
<strong>Delete</strong>: The selected section will be deleted following a confirmation prompt. Please bear in mind that all files will be irretrievably lost.</li>
</ul>
