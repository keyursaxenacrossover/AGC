<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-renderer-start-pos="5143">You can also filter the field report by disposition codes, common variables, and survey variables. In this way, you can, for example, only display the participants, who suspended the survey, who started the survey at a certain point in time, or who answered a question in a certain way. Proceed as follows to filter:</p>
<ul class="ak-ul wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-indent-level="1">
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="5461">Click on <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Filter results</strong> at the top right of the field report.<img src="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/21013837937426"></p>
</li>
</ul>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="5461"><strong data-renderer-mark="true">To filter by one or more disposition codes:</strong></p>
<ul class="ak-ul wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-indent-level="1">
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="5576">Select one or more disposition codes from the <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Restrict to Disposition Codes</strong> area and click on <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Save status selection</strong>.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="5696">The filtered field report is immediately displayed.<img src="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/21013850195602"></p>
</li>
</ul>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="5696"><strong data-renderer-mark="true">To filter by a common variable:</strong></p>
<ul class="ak-ul wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-indent-level="1">
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="5789">In the <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Restrict to variable</strong> area under <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Common variables</strong>, select a variable you want to filter by – for example, “Beginning of survey”. If you select this variable, only participants, who started the survey in a certain time period, will be displayed.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-left" data-renderer-start-pos="6043">Click <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Continue</strong> a little further up.<img src="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/21013850213394"></p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="6043">Now, set the period for the start of the survey by selecting a date from both drop-down menus.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="6043">Click on <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Save</strong>.<img src="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/21013837952146"></p>
</li>
</ul>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong data-renderer-mark="true">To filter by a survey variable:</strong></p>
<ul class="ak-ul wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-indent-level="1">
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="6241">In the <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Restrict to Variable</strong> section under <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Survey Variables</strong>, select a variable by which you want to filter the results – for example, by the answer to a specific question. In the example below, we’ll pick the question "How did you find out about our product?" of the question type "Single response list (vertical)".</p>
</li>
<li>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-left" data-renderer-start-pos="6559">Then, click <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Continue</strong> at the bottom of the page.<img src="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/21013837955474"></p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="6559">On the next page, select the answer to the question you want to filter by from the drop-down menu.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-left" data-renderer-start-pos="6559">Click on <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Save</strong>.<img src="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/21013850214546"></p>
</li>
</ul>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-renderer-start-pos="6559"><em>Note: If required, you can filter for specific disposition codes and a common OR a survey variable at the same time. However, you cannot filter for common variables and survey variables at the same time.</em></p>
