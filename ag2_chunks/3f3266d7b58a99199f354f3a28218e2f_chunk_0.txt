<h1 id="h_01J4VQ3KRAJXHQGKDT37XVE51N">MySight Overview</h1>
<p><span style="color: #2f3941; background-color: #ffffff;">MySight gives users the power to manage, edit, and customize online dashboards. It includes advanced functionality for data analysts in your organization who can perform an ad-hoc analysis of any of your data. MySight is embedded in the Portals module and therefore can be combined with action management, discussions or even data collection modules. This allows the user to have all feedback-related activities in one place. You can do integration with data reporting tools such as Tableau and publish it to Mysight.</span></p>
