-profile-dropdown" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>User profile dropdown</strong></h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">The same is valid for the <strong>User profile dropdown</strong> - you can add any content you like but ideally, it stands out from the <strong>Bar navigation</strong> to offer the panelist specific user-oriented functions, such as their profile or log out.</p>
<h3 id="tab-navigation" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>Tab navigation</strong></h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">The <strong>Tab navigation</strong> has a horizontal menu navigation, in contrast to the <strong>Bar navigation</strong>, and can either be used in addition to the main navigation or instead. <span style="color: #091e42;">The </span><strong>Tab navigation</strong><span style="color: #091e42;"> is always visible in the exterior view.</span></p>
<h3 id="adding-new-pages-to-your-navigation" class="sc-eNNmBn lmEGgd wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>Adding new pages to your navigation</strong></h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">While your portal evolves and new pages are added, the navigation does not adjust automatically and must therefore be changed manually. From the sub-navigation field <strong> Navigation, </strong>you select either <strong>Bar navigation</strong>, <strong>User Profile Dropdown</strong>, <strong>Tab navigation,</strong> or <strong>Sidebar</strong>.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Any page already added to the navigation will be displayed.</p>
<ul class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">
<li>Click on <strong>+ Pages</strong>.</li>
</ul>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">A drop-down menu including all pages will appear.</p>
<ol class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">
<li>Select a page from the list.</li>
<li>Click the green check mark to the right of the drop-down menu.</li>
</ol>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">The page selected will be added to your navigation.</p>
<h3 id="change-the-order-of-navigation-links" class="sc-eNNmBn lmEGgd wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>Change the order of navigation links</strong></h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><span style="color: #091e42;">Whether you just want to switch elements in an existing order or push a new page to a specific position, you may edit your navigation‘s order using the arrow icons. From the subnavigation </span><strong>Navigation,</strong><span style="color: #091e42;"> you select either </span><strong>Bar navigation</strong><span style="color: #091e42;">, </span><strong>User Profile Dropdown</strong><span style="color: #091e42;">, </span><strong>Tab navigation,</strong><span style="color: #091e42;"> or </span><strong>Sidebar</strong><span style="color: #091e42;">. </span>Find the page you would like to move within the list of pages. If your page has not been added to the navigation yet, add the page. </p>
<ul class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">
<li>Click the respective arrow icon to move the page up or down in the hierarchy.</li>
</ul>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Repeat if necessary until the page is at its final point in your navigation hierarchy.</p>
<h3 id="deleting-links-from-navigation" class="sc-eNNmBn lmEGgd wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>Deleting links from the navigation</strong></h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><span style="color: #091e42;">When pages become obsolete or redundant you can delete their links from your navigation. From the subnavigation </span><strong>Navigation</strong><span style="color: #091e42;"><strong> </strong>select either </span><strong>Bar navigation</strong><span style="color: #091e42;">, </span><strong>User Profile Dropdown</strong><span style="color: #091e42;">, </span><strong>Tab navigation,</strong><span style="color: #091e42;"> or </span><strong>Sidebar</strong><span style="color: #091e42;">.</span></p>
<ul class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">
<li><span style="color: #091e42;"><span style="color: #091e42;">Click the trashcan icon to delete a page, It is found to the right in the page‘s row. The respective page will be deleted from the navigation.</span></span></li>
</ul>
