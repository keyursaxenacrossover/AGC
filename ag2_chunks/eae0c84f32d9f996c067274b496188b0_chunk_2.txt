<h3 id="Creating-user-account" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-renderer-start-pos="2142"><strong data-renderer-mark="true">Creating user account</strong></h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-renderer-start-pos="2165">To create a new account, click on the <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Create user account</strong> button. Specify the language in which the admin area is to be shown for the new user. Enter the initial password for the new user twice. On their first login new users are automatically requested to change their password.</p>
