<p><strong>Basic information and Additional information</strong></p>
<p>Use the <strong>Basic Information</strong> tab to assign a title for the <em>Ideation Guide</em> and to write a short description of the content. You will use the <strong>Additional information</strong> tab to create further content, which can be accessed by clicking on the <strong>Further details</strong> button in the external view.</p>
<p><strong>Categories</strong></p>
<p>On the <strong>Categories</strong> tab, select the categories for which you want to request improvement suggestions. The categories will be displayed on the action ticket. The categories are created in<strong> Libraries. </strong>You can use the link to get there.</p>

<p><strong>Questionnaire and e-mail settings</strong></p>
<p>On the <strong>Questionnaire</strong> tab, select the survey project. On the <strong>E-mail Settings</strong> tab, you will select an invitation and  a reminder e-mail templates for both the second Ideation phase, the survey phase, the ideation phase, and the voting phase. You can also select the template for the result e-mail here.</p>
<p><strong>Settings</strong></p>
<p>On the <strong>Settings</strong> tab, you specify the settings like period of participation. You can also specify the time when an e-mail is to be sent as a reminder before the end of each phase. You can also enable the option to send a survey reminder and a voting reminder to all participants. If you deactivate this option, the reminders will only be sent to those participants who have not participated yet.</p>
<p><img src="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/20998222751890" alt="Settings.jpg"></p>
<p>You can allow the invitation of participants by using the e-mail addresses. You can select a company managed group. To ensure data anonymity, after activating this option, you can define the number of participations that must have taken place before the data information will be shown on the guide status page, on the voting page, and on an action ticket. After that, you can make voting settings by specifying the "Number of possible votes per category" and "Number of votes to convert into actions".</p>
<p><img src="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/20998175751058" alt="Settings 2.jpg"></p>
<p>You must save all the settings you made!</p>
<h3 id="header-6">Ideation-Guide-Workflow</h3>
<p><strong>First phase: Starting phase</strong></p>
<p>The <em>Ideation Guide</em> workflow is divided into three phases. In the first phase, the starting phase, you define the category or categories in which suggestions for improvements can be made, select participants and send out invitations.</p>
<p><strong>Second phase: Ideation phase</strong></p>
<p>After that the collection of suggestions will start. A survey must be completed. The phase can be started via the e-mail invitation or the guide status page.</p>
<p><img src="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/20998238168466" alt="Status page.jpg"></p>
<p>You will also find some information about your <em>Ideation Guide</em>: the category, the start date of the guide, the phase the guide is in, the date when the phase will end, and the participations. The buttons allows you to participate in the current phase. Using the three-point menu, you can also participate in the current phase, view the participants and end the current phase.</p>
<p><strong>Third phase: Voting phase</strong></p>
<p>After completing the second phase, the <em>Ideation Guide</em> Questionnaire, the third phase will start, voting on the suggestions received.</p>
<p><img src="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/20998192619282" alt="Voting phase
