11131">Your sample is ready – you can now get started and personally invite participants to your survey via email. To do so, you will first set the mail sender and create a mail template for your survey invitation in the next two steps.</p>

<h4 id="Creating-the-Mail-Template" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-renderer-start-pos="11944"><strong data-renderer-mark="true">Creating the Mail Template</strong></h4>
<ul class="ak-ul wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-indent-level="1">
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="11974">Change to the <em data-renderer-mark="true">Mail templates</em> menu. You can find it on the left-hand side just above the <em data-renderer-mark="true">Mail senders</em> menu.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="12084">Click on the split button <strong data-renderer-mark="true">+ New draft</strong>, and select<strong data-renderer-mark="true"> + New draft for projects.<img src="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/21017226389266"></strong></p>
</li>
</ul>
<ul class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">
<li data-renderer-start-pos="12084">A mask for creating a new mail template is opened in a new tab.</li>
<li data-renderer-start-pos="12235">For <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Type</strong>, select the option <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Invitation mail</strong> from the drop-down menu.</li>
<li data-renderer-start-pos="12307">For <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Description</strong>, enter a short description suitable for your use case into the free text field.</li>
<li data-renderer-start-pos="12406">For <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Delivery</strong>, select the option <strong data-renderer-mark="true">E-mail</strong> from the drop-down menu.</li>
<li data-renderer-start-pos="12473">For <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Mail sender</strong>, select the email address you have added in the previous step from the drop-down menu.</li>
<li data-renderer-start-pos="12579">For <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Mail format</strong>, select the option <strong data-renderer-mark="true">HTML only</strong> from the drop-down menu, and click on <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Apply format</strong>.</li>
<li data-renderer-start-pos="12679">For <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Subject</strong>, enter a subject for your email.</li>
<li data-renderer-start-pos="12727">Draft the text for your invitation email in the <em data-renderer-mark="true">Visual editor</em>. You want content like the participant’s name or the link to the survey for each participant to be dynamically replaced in the actual invite you send out. In order to archive that, you need to use placeholders. A list of all available placeholders can be displayed by clicking the question mark symbol below the field <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Subject</strong>. In the example below, the placeholder <em data-renderer-mark="true">#u_firstname#</em> will be replaced by the participant’s first name, the placeholder <em data-renderer-mark="true">#u_name#</em> will be replaced by the last name, and <em data-renderer-mark="true">#code_complete#</em> will be replaced by the link to your survey.</li>
