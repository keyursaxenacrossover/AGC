<h3 id="roles-moderator-and-survey-manager" class="sc-eNNmBn lmEGgd wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>Roles: Moderator and Survey Manager</strong></h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Moderators possess an advanced rights profile and can actively engage in editing content displayed in your portal. As an example, comments or replies and even any panelist shouts can be edited, hidden, or deleted by a moderator. Hidden content will only be visible to moderators. Further settings define, whether only moderators can create new discussions, who will be allowed to invite other panelists to discussions, or whether discussions can be closed (and re-opened) by moderators.</p>

<li>To edit, hide, or delete content, click the menu located to the right in the exterior view of your portal under the module <strong>Discussionboard</strong>
</li>
<li>Click <strong>Edit</strong>.</li>
</ul>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">The content can now be edited.</p>
<ul class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">
<li>If you would like to hide the content from the portal, click <strong>Withdraw</strong>.</li>
</ul>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">The selected content is now hidden.</p>
