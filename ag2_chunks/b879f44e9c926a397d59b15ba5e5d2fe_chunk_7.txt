<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>Inviting participants to follow-up surveys</strong></p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">With this workflow, you can invite participants to follow-up surveys. This workflow is usable as an individual workflow or in combination with the workflow “Close”.</p>
<ul class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">
<li>
<p>To invite a participant to a follow-up survey, click on the workflow button <strong>Invite to follow-up survey</strong>.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">An entry mask is opened.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><img src="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/21015069504146" alt="image2019-5-15_17-6-6.png"></p>
<ul class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">
<li>
<p>Optionally, you can leave a comment in the comment field.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Click on the button <strong>Execute</strong>.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">You have invited the participant of this task to a follow-up survey. The participant will be notified accordingly by e-mail.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>Closing tasks</strong></p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">With this workflow, you can close tasks you are responsible for.</p>
<ul class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">
<li>
<p>To close a task, click on the workflow button <strong>Close</strong>.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">An entry mask is opened.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><img src="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/21015069502354" alt="image2019-5-15_17-8-34.png"></p>
<ul class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">
<li>
<p>Optionally, you can leave a comment in the comment field.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Depending on each configuration, you additionally have the possibility to decide whether you send an invitation to a follow-up survey or not upon closing of the task, via radio button “Invite to follow-up survey?”.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Click on the button <strong>Execute</strong>.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">You have closed the task. Depending on each configuration, you have automatically sent out an invitation to a follow-up survey in addition to closing of the task, or through activation or deactivation of the respective radio button, you have decided yourself, if you have sent out an invitation to a follow-up survey or not.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Closed tasks are not visible in the list display by default. To display closed tasks in the list display, activate the status Completed in the filtering area.</p>

<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>Reopening tasks</strong></p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">With this workflow, you can reopen closed tasks.</p>
<ul class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">
<li>
<p>To reopen a closed task, click on the workflow button <strong>Reopen</strong>.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">An entry mask is opened.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><img src="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/21015069497746" alt="image2019-5-15_17-13-16.png"></p>
<ul class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">
<li>
<p>In the drop-down list, select the <strong>Responsible person</strong> who will be editing this task.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Optionally, you can leave a comment in the comment field.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Click on the button <strong>Execute</strong>.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">You have reopened the task for the person responsible for editing who will be notified accordingly by e-mail.</p>
