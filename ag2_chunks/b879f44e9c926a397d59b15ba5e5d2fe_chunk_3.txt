<h2 id="working-with-task-list-in-the-exterior-view-of-portals" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">
<strong>Working with</strong><em><strong> Task List</strong></em><strong> in the exterior view of </strong><em><strong>Portals</strong></em>
</h2>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">In the exterior view of Portals, responsible persons work on their tasks in the context of the follow-up process. You have received an e-mail (or some other message) which names you as the person responsible for administrating the follow-up process of the employee survey in your organizational unit. The e-mail usually contains your access data to the exterior view of <em>Portals</em>:</p>
<ul class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">
<li>
<p>E-mail address</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Password</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>URL for the login of the Portals exterior view</p>
</li>
</ul>
<h3 id="login-to-portals-exterior-view" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">
<strong>Login to </strong><em><strong>Portals</strong></em><strong> exterior view</strong>
</h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Click on the link in the e-mail to open the login to the exterior view of<em> Portals</em>. If this link is not working, mark it, copy it and paste it into the address field of your web browser. By clicking on <strong>Enter</strong>, you will reach the login window. Please enter your e-mail address and password into the text fields and click on the <strong>Sign In</strong> button. When logging in for the first time, this will prompt a security routine in the course of which you change your password and specify a security question. Later, you will be taken directly to the Portals exterior view. The password will have to be changed once every month. The Portals exterior view is opened.</p>
<h3 id="task-list-in-the-portals-exterior-view" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">
<em><strong>Task List</strong></em><strong> in the </strong><em><strong>Portals</strong></em><strong> exterior view</strong>
</h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">After the login, you automatically enter the Portals exterior view.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><img src="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/21015039491474" alt="image2019-5-15_16-30-40.png"></p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">On the main page of the Portals exterior view, you see, if applicable, a list with created tasks which are assigned to your organizational unit. From here, you can create new tasks as well as access, edit, copy and delete tasks. Also, you can filter, sort and search tasks.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Depending on the setting of your installation, the exterior view is opened with another page in <em>Task List</em>.</p>
<h3 id="filtering-area" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>Filtering area</strong></h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">To open the filtering area, click on the <strong>Filter</strong> button with the funnel icon. Here, you can filter created tasks by Status, Creator, Unit and Subject group. Only tasks with the status Not started and Started are listed by default. You can adjust this setting via the checkboxes in the Status section. The number next to the Filter button with the funnel icon indicates the number of filters currently being active. Via activation of the <strong>Filter buttons</strong> “Show only my own tasks” and “Show escalated tasks”, you can restrict the display of tasks in the list display to your own current tasks or to escalated tasks of your own organizational unit. Via the drop-down list, you can sort tasks by Title, Completion date, Status and Created date.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Via the search field, you can search and display tasks by entering respective characters and numbers which are included in the task variables Title, Responsible person, Creator, ID, Problem description and Task description.</p>
