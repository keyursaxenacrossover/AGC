>
</li>
<li>
<p>Tick the “Personalized dispatch” checkbox if you wish to use the recipient’s real name.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Enter the subject.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Select the sender. If the desired sender is not available, you can click on the <strong>Add mail sender </strong>link to open the menu for creating new senders (Chapter <em>12.4, p. 536</em>) in a new window.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Formulate the content of the invitation mail. You can display an overview of the wildcards you can use by clicking on the <strong>question mark </strong>icon.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>If you wish to add an attachment, it should be uploaded in the “Add an attachment” field. Locate the desired file, and click on <strong>Attach file </strong>to add it.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Click on the <strong>Preview mail </strong>button to check the result.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>In particular, check whether the wildcards are replaced correctly.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>If you want to save the mail for future use, tick the “Save as mail template (including wildcards)” checkbox and enter a title for the new template.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>If you are satisfied, click on <strong>Send</strong>.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>The <strong>Mail Transmission Report </strong>menu gives you an overview of the emails that have been sent.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Please note: The e-mails will not be sent immediately but, depending on the mail volume, will be placed in a queue and processed in groups in ten-minute intervals.</p>

<h3 id="faq1" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">What types of mail templates are available for different actions?</h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Mail templates are specific to each action. For example, "Invitation" type templates are available for sending invitation emails, while "Default" type templates can be used for any action.</p>
<h3 id="faq2" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">How can I add a new mail sender?</h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Click on the <strong>Add mail sender</strong> link in the sender selection menu to open a new window for creating new senders.</p>
<h3 id="faq3" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">When are the emails sent after clicking "Send"?</h3>
