<h3 id="h_01J3FXFRMHWDEFY5GYRCYB73B3" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Adding Panelists</h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Clicking on a panelist’s name in the list opens the detail view of that panelist’s data, which is distributed across several tabs:<img src="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/21017191607186"></p>
<ul class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">
<li>
<strong>Detail view</strong>: Contains account data, visibility settings, bonus points, and contact history.</li>
<li>
<strong>Tracking information</strong>: Displays the panelist’s tracking data.</li>
<li>
<strong>Groups, Surveys, and Tasks</strong>: Information on group memberships and assigned tasks, with options for manual changes.</li>
<li>
<strong>Sessions</strong>: Shows information on the most recent sessions.</li>
<li>
<strong>History</strong>: Displays the history of changes made to address and master data by the panelists.</li>
</ul>
<h3 id="h_01J3FXFRMH8ZVEVD1GFNPH6FK9" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Overview</h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">The <strong>Detail view</strong> tab provides an overview of key panelist data, including account data, profile visibility settings, bonus points, and contact history. Various buttons open dialogs for further information and editing options.</p>
<h4 id="h_01J3FXFRMH45V0WSJFGQS6V2AN" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Account Data</h4>
<ul class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">
<li>Lists participant data, excluding the password (viewable and changeable in special dialogs with appropriate authorization).</li>
<li>Displays language version, PCI value, duplicates status, panel status, e-mail verification status, and e-mail verification code.</li>
<li>All data except for the last change of panel status and e-mail verification code can be edited.</li>
</ul>

<ul class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">
<li>Visibility of profile data.</li>
<li>Permission to send and receive private messages.</li>
<li>Permission to tag posts.</li>
