<h2 id="data_sharing_policy">Tivian's Data Sharing Policy</h2>
<p>Tivian has a strict policy against sharing data with third parties. The company does not pass on data to external entities in any way, shape, or form. This policy ensures that your information remains confidential and is used solely for the purposes you've agreed to.</p>
<p> </p>
<h2 id="data_ownership">Data Ownership and Control</h2>
<p>Tivian operates on the principle that you, the client, remain the owner of your data at all times. The company only processes your data at your request, giving you complete control over the extent, nature, and purpose of data collection. This approach empowers you to maintain full ownership and decision-making authority over your information.</p>
<p> </p>
<h2 id="third_party_tools">Use of Third-Party Tools</h2>
<p>In line with its commitment to data privacy, Tivian does not use third-party analytics tools such as Google Analytics in its surveys. This further ensures that your data is not exposed to external parties and remains within the controlled environment of Tivian's systems.</p>
