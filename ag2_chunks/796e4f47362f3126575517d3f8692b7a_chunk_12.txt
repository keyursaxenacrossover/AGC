<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>Configured comparisons</strong></p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">You use the element <strong>Configured comparisons</strong> to access all comparison groups that you create in the <strong>Report Wizard</strong> under <strong>Settings</strong>. The members of the comparison group are determined in the second step of ordering the report.</p>
<ul class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">
<li>The comparison units that form the members of the comparison groups do not have a name but are counted as placeholders from top to bottom.</li>
<li>During the report ordering process, you ensure that the desired unit of the organizational structure is taken from the members selected in the mapping.</li>
<li>The element <strong>Configured comparisons</strong> is useful if you want to make a comparison free of the organizational structure.</li>
</ul>
