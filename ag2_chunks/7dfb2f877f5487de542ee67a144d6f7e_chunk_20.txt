-renderer-mark="true">Export all templates</strong> and an overview table of the created templates.</p>
<ul class="ak-ul wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-indent-level="1">
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="18971">You can edit, copy or delete existing form templates by clicking on the<strong data-renderer-mark="true"> pen</strong>, <strong data-renderer-mark="true">copy</strong> or <strong data-renderer-mark="true">delete</strong> icon in the right column. You change the permissions, i.e. who has access to the consent form template, by clicking on the <strong data-renderer-mark="true">permission</strong> icon.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="19206">You have a language identifier that allows you to filter the consent form templates by language.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<h3 id="Create-new-template" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-renderer-start-pos="19307">Create new template</h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-renderer-start-pos="19328">To create a new form template, you click on the button <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Create template</strong>. Fields marked with an asterisk are mandatory and must be completed. The following fields are available:</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-renderer-start-pos="19505"><strong data-renderer-mark="true">Language identifier</strong></p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-renderer-start-pos="19526">You assign the consent form template to a language by ISO code so that you can select it in the questionnaire editor matching the language of the survey.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><img src="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/21017191638674"></p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-renderer-start-pos="19526"><strong data-renderer-mark="true">Name, description, content</strong></p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-renderer-start-pos="19712">You give the template a name and write a short description text. Enter the purpose of the data processing in the free text field "Content".</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-renderer-start-pos="19853"><strong data-renderer-mark="true">Privacy information</strong></p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-renderer-start-pos="19874">You can also enter the data protection information in advance in order to save it as a template. You specify:</p>
<ul class="ak-ul wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-indent-level="1">
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="19987">Company name (controller)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="20016">Contact details</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="20035">Controller's representative (if applicable)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="20082">What personal data will be collected and used</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="20131">What special categories of personal data will be collected and used</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="20202">Legal basis for processing</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="20232">Recipient or categories of recipients of the personal data</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="20294">Transfer of data to a non-EU/EEC country or international organisation, and safeguards</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="20384">Statutory or contractual requirement</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="20424">Automated decision making</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="20453">Information on data subject rights</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="20491">Information on the right to withdraw consent</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="20535">Information on supervisory authority</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="20575">Name &amp; contact details of the data protection officer</p>
</li>
</ul>
<h3 id="Import-templates" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-renderer-start-pos="20629">Import templates</h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-renderer-start-pos="20647">Use the <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Import templates</strong> button to download and upload consent form templates.</p>
