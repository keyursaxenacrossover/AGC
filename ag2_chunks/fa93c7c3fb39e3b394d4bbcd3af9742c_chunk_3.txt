<h2 id="header-10">Portals</h2>
<h3 id="header-12">Leadership Guide in <em>Portals</em>
</h3>
<p>As of 18.4 Winter Release, you can choose between the two guide types <em><strong>Me</strong></em> and <em><strong>Us</strong></em>. The <em><strong>Me</strong></em> Guide offers you a new scenario. While you click on the <em><strong>Us </strong></em>Guide button to call up the well know Guide scenario you have been familiar with since 18.2 summer release, which will continue to be used for questioning groups or teams. You configure a 360° leadership survey by calling up the <em><strong>Me </strong></em>Guide.</p>

<p><u>Workflow</u></p>
<p>The selection and configuration options of the Guide setup are based on the settings made on the <strong>Rater-Groups</strong> tab. In the following, the columns are presented from left to right using the example of the <strong>Manager</strong> category to give a brief impression of how you can proceed.</p>
<p>Via drop-down list, you select <strong>Manager</strong> in the column "Rater-Groups-Category". This creates the <strong>Manager</strong> step in the Guide setup of <em>Portal's</em> external view (sub-item of the second Guide setup step <strong>Invitation</strong>). The name and description of the Rater Group that are displayed in the Portal's exterior view are specified in the Label and Description columns. To assign a company-managed group to the <strong>Manager</strong> category you use the drop-down you'll find in the forth column. You can use the three slide switches:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p> Allow removing preselected group</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Optional</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Adding partcipants allowed</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>The three slide buttons allow users to manage the Rater-Groups. If you allow the users to remove the groups, they can add other groups. With the slide switch of the column "Optional" you control whether the whole rater group including the selection of participants may be skipped. You allow the addition of participants with the slide switch "Adding participants allowed?". "Minimum number of participants" and "Maximum number of participants" are used to control how many participants can be added.</p>
