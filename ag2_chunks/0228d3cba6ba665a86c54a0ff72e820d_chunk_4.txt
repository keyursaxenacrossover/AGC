<h3 id="Creating-a-Single-Response-Question" data-renderer-start-pos="5658">
<strong data-renderer-mark="true">Creating a </strong><em data-renderer-mark="true"><strong data-renderer-mark="true">Single Response Question</strong></em>
</h3>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="5695">On the next page, you will create a question with a <em data-renderer-mark="true">Single response list</em>. This means that the respondent can only give one answer.</p>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="5827"><strong data-renderer-mark="true">Steps:</strong></p>
<ul class="ak-ul" data-indent-level="1">
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="5837">To create the next page, select the<strong data-renderer-mark="true"> + Page</strong> button again. Enter “Internet use” as title.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="5928">Create a new question on this page. Select the question type “<em data-renderer-mark="true">Single response” → Single response list (vertical) (111)</em>.<img src="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/21018302870162"></p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="5928">Click on <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Save and edit</strong>.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="5928">The detail view is opened.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="5928">Enter the question text (“How often do you use the internet”) and the texts of the 4 answer options (“Daily”, “Several times per week”, “Several times per month” and “Rarely”). Per default, 5 answer options are displayed. To delete the superfluous answer option, activate the checkbox in the column <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Delete.<img src="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/21018302848018"></strong></p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="5928">Click on <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Save</strong>.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="5928">Select <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Preview question</strong> to check the new question.</p>
</li>
</ul>
