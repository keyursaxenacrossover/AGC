<h3 id="editing-portal-and-portal-cms" class="sc-eNNmBn lmEGgd wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>Editing portal and portal CMS</strong></h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><span style="color: #091e42;">The other editing options are available once you have entered the administrative interface of your portal, i. e. the portal CMS. You open your portal from the </span><strong>Portal Overview</strong><span style="color: #091e42;"> by clicking on its tile.</span></p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><span style="color: #091e42;"><img src="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/21015069305746" alt="CMS portal.png"></span></p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><span style="color: #091e42;"><span style="color: #091e42;">You will be directed to the sub-navigation field </span><strong>Pages</strong><span style="color: #091e42;"> by default. You can now access the various settings available, create pages, add page modules, and determine the layout and content of your portal‘s pages. Several tabs are displayed to the left, each containing a set of options. The default Portals package comes with a set of pre-defined pages, a set of </span><strong>Standard pages,</strong> and <strong>Target pages</strong><span style="color: #091e42;">. Pages defined as </span><strong>Target pages</strong><span style="color: #091e42;"> cannot be deleted as they are crucial to intact navigation between pages. The layout configurations (</span>rows<span style="color: #091e42;"> and </span>modules<span style="color: #091e42;">) for the page currently selected are displayed on the content area to the right. New pages will appear empty as they do not contain rows or modules yet.</span></span></p>
