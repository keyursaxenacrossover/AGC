<h1 id="h_01J5ZDTS02QETB6SEX1BQJH6WV" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Overview</h1>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">This guide outlines the necessary information sales teams should provide when creating a ticket for customer support. The information varies depending on the type of order: Installation, Account Creation, Module/Addons, or Service Orders. Providing complete and accurate details ensures efficient processing and minimizes delays.</p>
