<h1 id="h_01HGKSMPRSER9ENY5QWWRZZ863">FAQ</h1>
<h3 id="faq_1">What are disposition codes used for?</h3>
<p>Disposition codes are used to track the status of participants in a survey field, providing information on whether they have been invited, started the survey, or completed it. This helps in managing and analyzing the progress of your survey.</p>
<h3 id="faq_2">How are disposition codes assigned?</h3>
<p>Most disposition codes are assigned automatically based on the participant's progress through the survey. Some codes may require manual assignment, especially for cases that cannot be automatically detected by the system.</p>
<h3 id="faq_3">Can a participant have multiple disposition codes at once?</h3>
<p>No, disposition codes are mutually exclusive. A participant can only have one disposition code at any given time.</p>
<h3 id="faq_4">How can I customize disposition codes?</h3>
<p>Some disposition codes (e.g., 16, 17, 18, 19, 33, 34, 38, 39, 40) are reserved for customization and can be used for further differentiation of participant statuses. Contact your system administrator or support team for assistance with customization.</p> export the disposition code in various places of <em>EFS</em>.</p>
