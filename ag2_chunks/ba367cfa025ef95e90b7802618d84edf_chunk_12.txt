 frequently used tags will be displayed at the end of the list. The number of tags displayed can be defined. Panelists can click tags displayed in this module. The search function will open and display the specific tag in context.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>Online users</strong></p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Displays all users currently online. Choose between displaying users' profile pictures or profile tiles. The profile pictures and tiles will direct a user to the respective profile if clicked. The Online user module lets you define the amount of users displayed.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>Shoutbox</strong></p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Any panelist can publish content through the shoutbox, whether it is text or pictures. The content can be assigned various topics available. The owner of the post and panelists with moderator roles can edit, hide, or delete shouts.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Shouts can be commented on. Comments are hidden by default and can be displayed by opening the comment section through the arrow button. Replies to comments are possible and comments can be up- or downvoted as is possible in discussions.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>Subscription overview</strong></p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Displays the user's subscriptions to topics and discussions. A panelist can access subscribed topics and discussions from here or delete subscriptions.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>Dashboard aggregated</strong></p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Displays aggregated dashboard results for HR and is therefore only available for users with HR roles.</p>
<h2 id="subnavigation-navigation" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>Subnavigation: Navigation</strong></h2>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><span style="color: #091e42;">The navigation contains four tabs: </span><strong>Bar navigation</strong><span style="color: #091e42;">, </span><strong>User profile dropdown</strong><span style="color: #091e42;">, </span><strong>Tab navigation, </strong><span style="color: #091e42;">and </span><strong>Sidebar</strong><span style="color: #091e42;">.  Generally, you can add any page available to each navigation, however, the user navigation is designed for pages and content related to the individual panelist, such as the profile page.</span></p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><span style="color: #091e42;"><img src="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/21015039225746" alt="Navigation.png"></span></p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><span style="color: #091e42;"><span style="color: #091e42;">They are referred to as content pages. Particular pages like target pages for a single discussion overview should not be placed in the navigations as the content of these pages is determined dynamically.</span></span></p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><span style="color: #091e42;"><span style="color: #091e42;"><span style="color: #091e42;">You can add links to any page on your portal‘s navigation, change their order, or delete the links.</span></span></span></p>
<h3 id="bar-navigation" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>Bar navigation</strong></h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">The <strong>Bar navigation</strong> comes in the form of an overlay menu. It can contain all available pages a panelist can navigate to in general, e. g. target pages or additional pages. To open the main menu in the exterior view, click on the menu button at the top right of the exterior view - the linked pages of the main navigation are opened.</p>
<h3 id="user-profile-dropdown" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>User profile dropdown</strong></h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">The same is valid for the <strong>User profile dropdown</strong> - you can add any content you like but ideally, it stands out from the <strong>Bar navigation</strong> to offer the panelist specific user-oriented functions, such as their profile or log out.</p>
<h3 id="tab-navigation" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>Tab navigation</strong></h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">The <strong>Tab navigation</strong> has a horizontal menu navigation, in contrast to the <strong>Bar navigation</strong>, and can either be used in addition to the main navigation or instead. <span style="color: #091e42;">The </span><strong>Tab navigation</strong><span style="color: #091e42;"> is always visible in the exterior view.</span></p>
