<h3 id="managing-news" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>Managing News</strong></h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">The starting point for news management is the <strong>Website → Standard content → News management</strong> menu. In this menu you can view and maintain the current news items, create new ones and assign them to the various lists.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">The news overview comprises all the news items created for the predefined language version and contains the following information:</p>
<ul class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">
<li>Title (internal): The internal news title.</li>
<li>Headline: The headline that will be output for the panelists.</li>
<li>Subtitle: The subheading.</li>
<li>Teaser: A short introduction to the news that will be displayed in the news list together with the link to the news item.</li>
<li>Author</li>
<li>Publication start date/time / Publication end date/time (GMT): The period in which the news item is published.</li>
<li>Displayed date of publication: Date displayed in the list of news both in the admin area and on the panel page.</li>
<li>Active:
<ul>
<li>Green traffic-light icon = Yes: The news item is displayed.</li>
<li>Red traffic-light icon = No: The news item is not displayed.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>List: Shows which news list the news item belongs to.</li>
<li>Viewed: Indicates how often the panelists have opened a news item. If a panelist opens a message twice, this is also counted as two hits.</li>
</ul>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">If you want to view news items of another language version in a given installation with several websites and/or language versions, simply change the settings in the upper section of the dialog. You can use the <strong>View</strong> function to change the number of table columns displayed as well as the number of rows per page.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">You can search the content of news items in the overview table. The advanced search allows you to selectively search by publication period, target group, activity status, list name or author.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Click on the icons in the “Actions” column to<strong> Edit, Copy,</strong> or<strong> Delete</strong> individual news items.</p>
