<h3 id="h_01J3FF967ZJJDV1VXZF7X3WJ74" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Account Statement of Individual Panelists</h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">To access an individual panelist's account statement, go to their account detail view and click on the Edit incentives button. Panelists can view their account balance and account movements in the internal area of the external view.</p>
<h3 id="h_01J3FF967ZEDH2J86AV1F8T2ZF" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Overview of Bonus Allocation for Project Participation</h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Bonus points are typically earned through participation in survey projects. Incentives are often given for reaching final pages, answering specific questions, or other user-defined events.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">In the People → Incentive management → Bonus system → Project list menu, all such allocations are tracked.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>Bonus Allocations per Project</strong></p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">The table overview includes:</p>
<ul class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">
<li>
<strong>Projects</strong>: Project titles, Project type, Project start and end dates, Number of rewarded participants (Count), Last allocation date, Bonus points assigned, and Value in currency.</li>
</ul>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Click the Excel icon in the “Actions” tab to download detailed allocations for each project.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>Bonus Allocation Within Projects per Month</strong></p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Clicking on a project name shows monthly bonus point allocations, including:</p>
<ul class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">
<li>
<strong>Month</strong>: Months with allocations for the project.</li>
<li>
<strong>Bonus points</strong>: Number of points allocated each month.</li>
<li>
<strong>Value in currency</strong>: Monetary value of points each month.</li>
</ul>
<h3 id="h_01J3FF9680CY5S77H6MJEJYGV2" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Overview of Bonus Allocation Within Campaigns</h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Bonus points can also be earned through "canvass friends" campaigns, where new panelists are recruited.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>Successful Canvassing</strong></p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">A person is considered "successfully canvassed" if they register and complete the master data survey.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>Bonus Allocation by Campaign</strong></p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">The People → Incentive management → Bonus system → Campaign list menu records all campaigns with earned bonus points. The table overview includes:</p>
<ul class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">
<li>
<strong>Campaign description</strong>, <strong>Current status</strong>, <strong>Participants recruited but not active</strong>, <strong>Recruited participants</strong>, <strong>Added points</strong> (number of points allocated), and <strong>Money value</strong> (value of points in currency).</li>
</ul>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>Bonus Allocation Within Individual Campaigns</strong></p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Clicking on a campaign name shows monthly bonus point distributions, including:</p>
<ul class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">
<li>
<strong>Month</strong>: Months with payments for the campaign.</li>
<li>
<strong>Added points</strong>: Number of points allocated each month.</li>
<li>
<strong>Money value</strong>: Monetary value of points each month.</li>
</ul>
