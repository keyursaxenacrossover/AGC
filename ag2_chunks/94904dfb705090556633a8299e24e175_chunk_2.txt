The Identity Provider will only verify that an identity belongs to the respective user. The access right for that specific user are still managed by every Application.</p><p>Hence every user that logs in at our Identity Provider can tell every Tivian application their respective identity but will not be able to access every application.</p><p></p><h4 id="header-17"><strong>If we connect our solution to the customer's Identity Povider, how will security take place at this Identity Provider?</strong></h4><p>The customer’s Identity Provider belongs to the customer. Therefore we do not have (and we should never have) any access to that provider changing anything. Security on the side of the Identity Provider is fully Managed by the customer.</p><p></p><h4 id="header-18"><strong>How will I be able to manage access to applications in our Identity Provider?</strong></h4><p>see above.</p><p>The Identity Provider will not manage any access policies for <ac:inline-comment-marker ac:ref="e9abcd09-27be-43f9-85e9-c1e5f4a41937">now</ac:inline-comment-marker>. It will only verify a user’s identity.
