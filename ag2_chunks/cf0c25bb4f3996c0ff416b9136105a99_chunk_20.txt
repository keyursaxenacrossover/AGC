em data-renderer-mark="true">print_survey</em>. It enables the translators to print the questionnaires in their languages.</p>
</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="32269">Make sure the teams have <em data-renderer-mark="true">no other ACL right</em>s.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="32320">Assign <em data-renderer-mark="true">write and read rights to the project </em>to all translator teams.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="32394">Navigate to the menu <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Projects </strong>→ {<strong data-renderer-mark="true">Selected project</strong>} → <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Questionnaire editor </strong>→<strong data-renderer-mark="true"> Language editor</strong>. In this dialog, you can assign <em data-renderer-mark="true">rights to the appropriate languages </em>to the various translator teams. Usually, translators will have read rights for the default language, which serves as reference language, and write rights for the language(s) into which they translate. Search for the required languages, click the <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Rights </strong>icon and assign “read” or “write” rights as required to the appropriate team.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="32891">Create the required staff accounts, and assign them to the respective teams.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<h3 id="Activating-a-Link-from-the-Questionnaire-to-EFS-Translator-Interface" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-renderer-start-pos="32973">
<strong data-renderer-mark="true">Activating a Link from the Questionnaire to</strong><em data-renderer-mark="true"><strong data-renderer-mark="true"> EFS Translator Interface</strong></em>
</h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-renderer-start-pos="33043">Optionally, you can activate links in the questionnaire and in the preview, which enable you, your staff and translators (depending on access rights) to jump directly into the respective editing pages in the <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Language editor </strong>or in <em data-renderer-mark="true">EFS Translator Interface</em>.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-renderer-start-pos="33300">If the translators want to make small changes during proofreading or if you want to control the translators’ work, the link saves time-consuming forward-backward skipping: You can click through the questionnaire and if you notice a problem, open the respective editing page easily.</p>
<div class="ak-editor-panel cc-tqp4ke wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-panel-type="info">
<div class="ak-editor-panel__icon"> </div>
<div class="ak-editor-panel__content">
<p data-renderer-start-pos="33582">The direct links are not available for question types 911, 921, 998, and 999.</p>
</div>
</div>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-renderer-start-pos="33661"><strong data-renderer-mark="true">Activating the links</strong></p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-renderer-start-pos="33683">In the admin area, open the menu <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Projects</strong> → {<strong data-renderer-mark="true">Selected projekt</strong>} → <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Project properties</strong>. In the section “Internal organization”, you can find the option “Activate the link to the EFS Translator Interface”. Activate “Display for all participants” and <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Save</strong>.</p>

<h3 id="faq-1" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">How do I change the interface language?</h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Click on the "Deutsch / English" link in the top navigation bar, then refresh the page to see the change.</p>
<h3 id="faq-2" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">What should I do if a project is missing from my list?</h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Contact the project manager or Questback's support team to ensure you have the correct access rights assigned.</p>
<h3 id="faq-3" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Can I translate only part of a questionnaire?</h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Yes, you can export only the changed texts by using the version control feature when exporting.</p>
