The default Portals package comes with a set of pre-defined pages, a set of </span><strong>Standard pages,</strong> and <strong>Target pages</strong><span style="color: #091e42;">. Pages defined as </span><strong>Target pages</strong><span style="color: #091e42;"> cannot be deleted as they are crucial to intact navigation between pages. The layout configurations (</span>rows<span style="color: #091e42;"> and </span>modules<span style="color: #091e42;">) for the page currently selected are displayed on the content area to the right. New pages will appear empty as they do not contain rows or modules yet.</span></span></p>
