<h3 id="faq_1" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">How can I search for participants created within a specific date range?</h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Use the extended search function and look for the creation date fields in the "Fields with Index" section. You can specify a start and end date to find participants created within that range.</p>
<h3 id="faq_2" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Can I search for multiple email addresses at once?</h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Yes, in the extended search, there's a "Search multiple e-mail addresses" function where you can enter multiple email addresses, one per line, to search for several participants simultaneously.</p>
<h3 id="faq_3" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">How do I change which columns are displayed in the participant list?</h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Click on the "View" button to open a dialog where you can select which columns to show or hide. You can also adjust the number of rows displayed per page in this dialog.</p>-justify"> </p>
