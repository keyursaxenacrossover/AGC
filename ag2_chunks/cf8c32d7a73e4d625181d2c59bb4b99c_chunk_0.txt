<h2 id="consent-form-configuration" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Consent Form Configuration</h2>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">The consent form configuration allows you to create and manage the consent form for panelist registration. This feature is available for both login dialogs via the portal and for inviting panelists via email.</p>
<h3 id="h_01J3F8Q9X2KKCYTMD0DGZCW43M" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Key Points:</h3>
<ul class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">
<li>Configure the consent form in the default language to display the checkbox.</li>
<li>One configuration applies to all portals.</li>
<li>Access the language overview in the People module under Panel Configuration Consent form configuration.</li>
</ul>

<h2 id="ways-of-entry" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Ways of Entry</h2>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Ways of entry record how panelists entered the panel and are used for grouping and evaluation purposes. The “reg_code” variable records the entry method of a panelist and can be used for group creation or evaluation (see "Creating and Editing Groups" for details). Registration keys, generated for a specified entry method, are required for authentication in closed panels and are used in promotional campaigns to identify canvassing panelists and evaluate campaign success.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><em>Note - <span style="color: #333333; background-color: #f1f2f4;" data-colorid="hn7jcbi85k">Read rights for reg_code are required for accessing the </span><strong>People → Panel configuration → Ways of entry </strong><span style="color: #333333; background-color: #f1f2f4;" data-colorid="hkw6r8z7f2">menu.</span></em></p>
<h3 id="h_01J3F8Q9X2216FZK99TRZ4PZFJ" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Key Features:</h3>
<ul class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">
<li>Create and manage ways of entry</li>
<li>Organize ways of entry using categories</li>
<li>Generate and manage registration keys</li>
<li>Import user-defined keys</li>
</ul>
