<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>2. Formatting answer categories</strong></p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">The following format should be used:</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><code>r_cat;master data variable;code;title;</code></p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Example:</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><code>r_cat;md_001;Birthday;0;Please select</code></p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">This sample answer category belongs to the variable “md_001”, in which the birthday is saved. The answer category has the code “0” and is labeled “Please select”, i.e. it is the defaulted field in a drop-down list that prompts the panelist to set the correct date.</p>
