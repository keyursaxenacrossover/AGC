 data-renderer-mark="true">URL</strong>. Afterward, you can insert it at the desired place. In case you want to change the URL, for example in order to adapt it to your company name, please proceed as follows:</p>
<ul class="ak-ul wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-indent-level="1">
<li>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-left" data-renderer-start-pos="1286">Click on the Pen symbol on the right-hand side of the URL.<img src="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/21017213492498"></p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="1286">In the free text field in the line <strong data-renderer-mark="true">New URL</strong>, enter the desired text â€“ e.g. the name of your company.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="1286">The checkbox for <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Replace URL in questions?</strong> is activated by default. Just keep this setting; it ensures that the new URL is used throughout the whole survey.<img src="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/21017226370322"></p>
</li>
</ul>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-renderer-start-pos="1286"><em>Note: Only the part after /uc/ can be defined according to your preferences. The rest of the URL is determined by the system and cannot be changed.</em></p>
<h3 id="Publishing-a-QR-Code" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-renderer-start-pos="1765"><strong data-renderer-mark="true">Publishing a QR Code</strong></h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-renderer-start-pos="1787">If you would like to publish your survey by means of print media, you can obtain a scannable QR code for your survey in EFS.</p>
<ul class="ak-ul wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-indent-level="1">
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="1915">In the line <strong data-renderer-mark="true">QR code</strong> of the <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Distribution</strong> section, click on <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Display</strong>.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="1985">A pop-up window will be displayed. Click on <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Save image</strong>. The QR code will be immediately downloaded and saved in the <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Downloads</strong> folder of your local system. To access the image, just click on the Download symbol in the upper right corner of the pop-up window; you can then directly open the picture or display the folder, in which the image has been saved.</p>
</li>
</ul>
