1">Automatic deletion of invited panelists</h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-renderer-start-pos="6592">In the <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Panel configuration</strong> of the <em data-renderer-mark="true">People </em>module, you have the new menu item <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Automatic deletion of invited panelists</strong>. Now you can automate the deletion of both the <em data-renderer-mark="true">Tell-a-friend </em>panelists (module: <em data-renderer-mark="true">Panel Website</em>) and the panelists with panel status (module: <em data-renderer-mark="true">Portals</em>).</p>
<ul class="ak-ul wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-indent-level="1">
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="6867"><strong data-renderer-mark="true">Tell a friend:</strong> You check the option "Delete invited panelists without reaction after" and set the number of years, months, or days after which the invited panelists who did not respond to the invitation should be deleted.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="7093"><strong data-renderer-mark="true">Panelist with panel status:</strong> You check the option "Delete invited panelists without response after" and set the number of years, months, or days after which the invited panelists who did not respond to the invitation should be deleted.</p>
<ul class="ak-ul" data-indent-level="2">
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="7330">In addition, you select the panel statuses of the panelists to be deleted.</p>
</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
<div class="fabric-editor-block-mark fabric-editor-alignment cc-5inqjd wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-align="center">
<h2 id="Disposition-codes" data-renderer-start-pos="7411">Disposition codes</h2>
</div>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-renderer-start-pos="7431">Participants who have one of the disposition codes 20, 21, 22 and 23 will receive the disposition code 43 after deletion of their personal data. The purpose of this new disposition code is that participants who are in an 'intermediate state' such as 'participant is responding' (disposition code 21) can no longer participate in the survey after the deletion of their data.</p>
<ul class="ak-ul wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-indent-level="1">
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="7808">Participants who do not give their consent will receive the disposition code 42.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<div class="fabric-editor-block-mark fabric-editor-alignment cc-5inqjd wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-align="center">
<h2 id="ACL-rights" data-renderer-start-pos="7893">ACL rights</h2>
</div>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-renderer-start-pos="7906">You can control the work with the <em data-renderer-mark="true">Privacy Assistant</em> by assigning three ACL rights. In <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Options</strong> under the menu item <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Team rights</strong> you assign write and/or read rights of the ACL rights to teams.</p>
