<h2 id="tivian-access-introduction">TIVIAN Access Introduction</h2>
<p>TIVIAN Access is powered by Okta.com and has been integrated into the overall TIVIAN platform to serve as a central service for all authentication workflows. It acts as the Identity provider for the platform, ensuring secure and efficient user authentication across various components.</p>

<h1 id="h_01HGKSMPRSER9ENY5QWWRZZ863">FAQ</h1>
<h3 id="faq-what-is-tivian-access">What is TIVIAN Access?</h3>
<p>TIVIAN Access is the product name for the Identity provider in the TIVIAN platform. It is powered by Okta.com and serves as a central service for all authentication workflows within the platform.</p>
<h3 id="faq-why-okta">Why is Okta.com used for TIVIAN Access?</h3>
<p>Okta.com is a leading provider of identity and access management solutions. By leveraging Okta's technology, TIVIAN Access can offer robust, secure, and scalable authentication services for the platform.</p>
<h3 id="faq-federation-scenarios">What are federation scenarios?</h3>
<p>Federation scenarios refer to different ways in which TIVIAN Access can integrate with and authenticate users across various systems, applications, or organizations. These scenarios demonstrate the flexibility of the solution in handling diverse authentication requirements.</p>
