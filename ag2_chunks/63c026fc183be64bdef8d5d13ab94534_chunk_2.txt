>Participants recruited but not active</strong>, <strong>Recruited participants</strong>, <strong>Added points</strong> (number of points allocated), and <strong>Money value</strong> (value of points in currency).</li>
</ul>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>Bonus Allocation Within Individual Campaigns</strong></p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Clicking on a campaign name shows monthly bonus point distributions, including:</p>
<ul class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">
<li>
<strong>Month</strong>: Months with payments for the campaign.</li>
<li>
<strong>Added points</strong>: Number of points allocated each month.</li>
<li>
<strong>Money value</strong>: Monetary value of points each month.</li>
</ul>
<h3 id="h_01J3FFBV6X5C2F07CF2EE3CRJB" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Viewing Panelists with a Specified Number of Bonus Points</h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">In the People → Incentive management → Bonus system → Bonus points overview menu, you can identify panelists with an account balance above a preset minimum value.</p>
<ol class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">
<li>Enter the desired number of points in the “Minimum amount of bonus points” field (enter “0” to display all panelists).</li>
<li>Optionally, restrict the selection to panelists with a specific panel status.</li>
<li>Click on Search.</li>
</ol>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">A list of panelists will be displayed, including their first name, last name, email address, pseudonym, account, other ID, panel status, bonus points, and their monetary value. To download the list, use the Excel export or CSV export buttons.</p>
<h3 id="h_01J3FFBV6X95ST3K8VWWYEZ1Y3" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Viewing Transactions for a Defined Period</h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">In the People → Incentive management → Bonus system → Transactions overview menu, you can find all transactions processed within a specific period.</p>
<ol class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">
<li>Define the time period and click Search.</li>
<li>The list will display detailed information for each transaction.</li>
<li>To download the list, use the Excel export or CSV export buttons.</li>
</ol>
<h3 id="h_01J3FFBV6XVWFGQXMY937BW59M" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Exporting Bonus Points of Former Panelists</h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">In the People → Incentive management → Bonus system → Bonus points log menu, you can download a list of panelists who have quit or were deleted, including their remaining bonus points. This is important if panelists request redemption of bonus points after quitting, as contact data are deleted upon status “deleted”. The log allows you to download necessary information.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>Note:</strong> Write rights for panel_log are necessary to access the bonus points log.</p>
<h3 id="h_01J3FFBV6X4T9WJ0063A2XCXTA" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Configuring Currency and Conversion Factor for Incentive Management</h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">To configure the display currency and conversion factor:</p>
<ol class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">
<li>Open People → Incentive management → Bonus system → Panel currency menu.</li>
<li>Define the following settings:
<ul>
<li><strong>Currency label</strong></li>
<li>
<strong>Conversion factor (currency / bonus points)</strong>: Enter the number of currency units corresponding to one bonus point. This factor is used to calculate and display the monetary value of bonus points.</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ol>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>Important Information:</strong></p>
<ul class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">
<li>Changes to the currency or conversion factor do not affect the unit “bonus points” on which calculations are based, but they do affect displayed monetary values in the incentive management system.</li>
<li>Changing the conversion factor in a live panel can alter the displayed monetary values for past payments.</li>
<li>It is recommended to configure the currency and conversion factor before the panel goes online.</li>
<li>Settings apply to the entire installation; different currencies or conversion factors for different websites or language versions are currently not possible.</li>
</ul>

<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">EFS Panel offers different options for allocating incentives:</p>
<ul class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">
<li>As a reward after completing registration.</li>

<li>As a reward to panel groups.</li>

</ul>
