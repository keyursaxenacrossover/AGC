<h2 id="general-information">General Information</h2>
<p>Support only sets up accounts on <strong>shared installations and completely new installations. </strong></p>
<p>On <strong>customer installations</strong>, the customer should always set up the accounts (members of the admin team), as Support cannot know whether the person is authorized to have access.</p>
<p>If an account needs to be created manually because the tac-script isn't available, follow these three main steps in the provided order:</p>
<ol>
<li>Create an organization</li>
<li>Create a team</li>
<li>Create user accounts</li>
</ol>
<p>Wording and definitions:</p>
<ul>
<li>An organization is the overall group which contains all teams and user accounts of a customer</li>
<li>A team contains user accounts to provide ACL rights in EFS to dedicated users</li>
<li>A user account is the account for a single person working in EFS</li>
</ul>
<h2 id="create-organization">Create an Organization</h2>
<p><img src="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/20429238779538" width="298" height="144"></p>
<ol>
<li>Log in to the installation</li>
<li>Click on System tab&gt; <strong>Users</strong> &gt; click on navigation menu <strong>Organisations</strong> &gt; click button <strong>Create organization</strong>
<ol>
<li>Provide organization name</li>
<li>Provide description</li>
<li>Set date and time specifications for the runtime of the organization</li>
<li>Set max amount of allowed users and projects for the organization</li>
</ol>
</li>
<li>Finish by clicking "Create"</li>
</ol>
