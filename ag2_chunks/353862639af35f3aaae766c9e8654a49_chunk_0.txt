<h2 id="create_opportunity">How to Create an Opportunity</h2>
<p>
  To create an opportunity in Netsuite:
</p>
<ol>
  <li>Log in to Netsuite using your "TIV Project Manager CEUR or UK or US (CRM)" role.</li>
  <li>Hover over "Transactions/Sales" and click on "Create Opportunity".</li>
  <li>Fill in all necessary information in the new opportunity form.</li>
</ol>

<h2 id="primary_information">Primary Information</h2>
<p>
  The Primary Information section includes important fields such as:
</p>
<ul>
  <li>Custom Form (do not change)</li>
  <li>Opportunity Name</li>
  <li>End User</li>
  <li>Customer</li>
  <li>Partner</li>
  <li>Comments For Invoicing</li>
  <li>Status</li>
  <li>Probability</li>
  <li>Expected Close Date</li>
  <li>Action Item</li>
  <li>Area of use</li>
  <li>Invoice Text</li>
  <li>PO#</li>
  <li>Terms of Payment</li>
</ul>

<h2 id="additional_information">Additional Information</h2>
<p>
  After filling in the Primary Information, complete the following sections:
</p>
<ul>
  <li>Classification</li>
  <li>Contract</li>
  <li>Forecasting</li>
</ul>
<p>
  These sections include fields such as Subsidiary, Contract Start/End Date, Order Type, Contract Term, Project Total, and Dropbox Link for the signed contract.
</p>

<h2 id="item_section">Item Section</h2>
<p>
  In the Item section, fill out the following fields:
</p>
<ul>
  <li>Item (e.g., T2002 for mirror, T2013 for training)</li>
  <li>Description</li>
  <li>Quantity</li>
  <li>Unit Price</li>
</ul>

<h2 id="post_contract">Post-Contract Steps</h2>
<p>
  After receiving the signed contract or purchase order:
</p>
<ol>
  <li>Update all necessary information in the opportunity.</li>
  <li>Set the opportunity status to "Closed Won".</li>
  <li>Wait for PS Ops to create a sales order.</li>
  <li>Create a project from the sales order.</li>
  <li>Connect the project to the opportunity.</li>
  <li>Ensure the correct billing address is listed.</li>
  <li>Notify Anna that the opportunity should be released.</li>
</ol>
