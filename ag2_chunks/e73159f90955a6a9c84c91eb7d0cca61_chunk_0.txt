<h1 id="h_01HGKSMPRS9JVGC6E9EA3DB98K" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Overview</h1>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">This article provides an introduction to the DXI Enterprise Feedback Suite (EFS), a comprehensive platform for managing feedback and surveys. It covers the core modules and optional features of the suite, offering insights into its functionality and capabilities.</p>
<h1 id="h_01HGKSMPRSYYE7GMM7DZ0VCKXK" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Information</h1>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">The Enterprise Feedback Suite is a powerful tool designed to help organizations collect, manage, and analyze feedback. This article focuses on the various modules and features that make up the EFS platform.</p>

<h2 id="core_modules" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Core Modules</h2>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">The Enterprise Feedback Suite consists of several core modules that form the foundation of the platform:</p>
<h3 id="projects" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><a href="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/en-us/articles/20370699710866" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Projects</a></h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">The Projects module focuses on creating and managing surveys through a graphical interface. It allows users to design questionnaires that follow research methodology, create a fluid participant experience, and trigger additional workflows for follow-ups or actions and alerts.</p>
<h3 id="people" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><a href="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/en-us/articles/20229143671058">People</a></h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">The People module is designed to manage participant data across different surveys and feedback initiatives. It supports the collection and management of background/profile data, participant recruitment, profile surveys, data quality rules, and automated processes like pulse survey send-outs and participant quarantine rules.</p>
<h3 id="portals" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><a href="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/en-us/articles/20370649009298" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Portals</a></h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Portals provide an online space for connecting with participants, allowing quick and easy launch of discussions and surveys without relying on traditional email invitations.</p>
<h3 id="website" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><a href="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/en-us/articles/20229108624146" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Website</a></h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">The Website module includes a content management system integrated within EFS Panel. It allows for the creation and maintenance of panel websites with integrated functions such as registration and contact forms.</p>
<h3 id="mysight" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><a href="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/en-us/articles/20370540566290" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">MySight</a></h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">MySight empowers users to manage, edit, and customize online dashboards. It offers advanced functionality for data analysts to perform ad-hoc analysis of any data. MySight is embedded in the Portals module, combining feedback-related activities in one place.</p>
<h3 id="platform_administration" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><a href="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/en-us/articles/20229291364754" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Platform Administration</a></h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">This module provides tools for managing users, libraries, software options, and user profiles. It also offers access to various support options.</p>
<h2 id="optional_modules" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Optional Modules</h2>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">In addition to the core modules, the Enterprise Feedback Suite offers several optional modules to enhance its functionality:</p>
<h3 id="report_manager" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><a href="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/en-us/articles/20370748444562" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Report Manager</a></h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">The Report Manager allows businesses to generate ad hoc reports based on standard report templates. This feature enables quick responses to management requests and provides deeper insights at no additional cost.</p>
