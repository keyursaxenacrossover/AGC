<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-renderer-start-pos="10431">We've looked at the figures, now let's take care of quality. The <em data-renderer-mark="true">Quality Correction</em> menu item allows you to intervene in the quality of your survey results during the field phase. This feature helps identifying participants, who simply click through the survey and don't really bother answering the questions. These answers are not relevant for the later evaluation of the survey and can be deleted. Detailed information on <em data-renderer-mark="true">Quality correction</em> can be found <a class="cc-1rn59kg" title="https://qbdocs.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/DOC/pages/1445200068/Statistics#Quality-correction" href="https://qbdocs.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/DOC/pages/1445200068/Statistics#Quality-correction" data-testid="link-with-safety" data-renderer-mark="true">here</a>.</p>
