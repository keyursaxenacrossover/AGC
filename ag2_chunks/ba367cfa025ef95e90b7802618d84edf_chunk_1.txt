e42;">. In addition to that, you will get the information on how many users are currently online and how many have logged into your portal (e.g. 16/40 would mean that 16 out of 40 registered users are currently online).</span></p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>Possible actions are:</strong></p>
<ol class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">
<li>edit portal name</li>
<li>edit URL name manually</li>
<li>View/change portal status: online or offline (You can click on offline/online button to change the portal status)</li>
<li>open the portal, access the front page</li>
<li>mobile integration, allowing usage of Portals with the app</li>
<li>view amount of registered panelists and the share of which are currently online</li>
<li>switch to the exterior view of the portal </li>
<li>3 bar menu</li>
</ol>

<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Your portal's URL will be displayed as follows: InstallationURL/portal/your_portals_URL</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Please note that the URL name will automatically adapt to any portal name changes and therefore will not need to be updated manually.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">However, if you choose to edit the URL name manually, the portal name remains unaffected. Note that this will prevent the URL from further adapting to any name changes made to the portal in the future.</p>
<h3 id="edit-the-name-of-a-portal" class="sc-eNNmBn lmEGgd wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>Edit the name of a portal</strong></h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">You can edit the name of the portal right from the tile. You click on the tile menu icon in the bottom right-hand corner of your portal tile.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">You select between copying or deleting the portal.</p>
<ul class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">
<li>If you click on the delete icon, you will be asked to confirm your choice, only then the portal will be added.</li>
<li>If you click on Copy, the portal will be copied.</li>
</ul>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><span style="color: #333333;">The deletion of a portal cannot be undone and the copying may take up to a few minutes.</span></p>
<h3 id="open-portal-to-access-exterior-view" class="sc-eNNmBn lmEGgd wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>Open the portal to access the exterior view</strong></h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><span style="color: #091e42;">The interface accessed by your panelists is called the exterior view. Panelists interact with each other or take part in discussions or surveys. While editing your portal you may want to access the exterior view on the portal from the administrative interface, to check the layout and design, etc.</span></p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><span style="color: #091e42;"><img src="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/21015039280274" alt="Tile.png"></span></p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">In this case, you can access your portal‘s default landing page from the portal tile in the Portal Overview.</p>
<ul class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">
<li>You click on the icon to the left of the URL name field.</li>
</ul>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">You will be required to log in with your panelist account information to enter the portal.</p>
<ul class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">
<li>You enter the login information</li>
</ul>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">After logging in, you will be directed to the default landing page specified within the settings area of your portal.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><img src="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/21015069310226" alt="Login.png"></p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><span style="color: #333333;">Ensure that your panelist account has sufficient access rights to allow logging into your portal.</span></p>
