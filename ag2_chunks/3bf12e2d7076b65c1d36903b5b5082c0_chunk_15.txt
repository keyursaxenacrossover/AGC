<h2 id="why_deprecate_features">Why Does Tivian Deprecate Features?</h2>
<p>Feature deprecation is necessary for several reasons:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<strong>Product &amp; Engineering:</strong> Streamlining the platform enables faster development and improvement.</li>
<li>
<strong>Customer Support, Training &amp; Implementation:</strong> Focusing on current features improve efficiency and service quality.</li>
<li>
<strong>Customer Impact:</strong> While change can be challenging, new features aim to deliver significant improvements to user experiences.</li>
</ul>
<p> </p>
<h1 id="h_01HGKSMPRSER9ENY5QWWRZZ863">FAQ</h1>
<h3 id="faq_1">What is feature deprecation?</h3>
<p>Feature deprecation is the process of phasing out or removing outdated features from the EFS platform to make way for new and improved functionality.</p>
<h3 id="faq_2">How will feature deprecation affect my use of the EFS platform?</h3>
<p>Feature deprecation may require you to transition to new features or alternative solutions. Tivian aims to provide ample notice and support to ensure a smooth transition for customers.</p>
<h3 id="faq_3">What should I do if a feature I use is being deprecated?</h3>
<p>If a feature you use is being deprecated, review the provided alternative solutions and contact Tivian support for assistance in transitioning to new features or workflows.</p>
