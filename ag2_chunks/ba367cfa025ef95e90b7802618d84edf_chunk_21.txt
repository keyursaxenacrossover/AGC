 as shouts or discussions. Topics and subtopics will be assigned during the creation of the content. If a subtopic is assigned, the superior topic will not be displayed.</span></p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Discussions can be sorted by topics, or a panelist can subscribe to the content of a specific<br>topic.</p>

<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Your topic will be created.</p>
<ul class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">
<li>A subtopic can be created by clicking on the arrow button to the left of the topic title.</li>
<li>Click <strong>+New subtopic</strong> and enter the title of the new subtopic.</li>
</ul>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">The subtopic will be displayed indented below its superior topic. Furthermore, it is possible to edit your topic by clicking on the edit button to the right and even to assign specific colors to your topics.
