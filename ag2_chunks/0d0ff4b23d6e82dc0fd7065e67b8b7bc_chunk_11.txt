<h3 id="changing-codes" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>Changing codes</strong></h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Codes can be changed as desired. To do so, you only need to change the values in the “Code” column of the table editor. Thus, an integral, positive scale can be modified to be centered around the zero point. To open the table editor, follow this click path: <strong>Workpad toolbar</strong> → <strong>Edit</strong>.</p>
<h3 id="re-sorting-answer-options" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>Re-sorting answer options</strong></h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">By default, the answer options in tables and charts are sorted in the order of the questionnaire editor (“number”). If necessary, however, you may re-sort the answers.
