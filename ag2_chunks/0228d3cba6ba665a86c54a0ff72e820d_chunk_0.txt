<p>This article provides a step-by-step guide on how to create your first survey using EFS Survey, a professional all-in-one solution for online surveys. It covers the process of creating an anonymous survey, building a questionnaire, and preparing the survey for distribution.</p>

<p>This guide focuses on the essential steps to create a basic survey, including creating an anonymous project, designing a questionnaire with various question types, and finalizing the survey for distribution.</p>
