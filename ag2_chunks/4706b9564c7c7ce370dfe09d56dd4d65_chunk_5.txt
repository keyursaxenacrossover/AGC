<h3 id="Allocating-Master-Data" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-renderer-start-pos="8211"><strong data-renderer-mark="true">Allocating Master Data</strong></h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-renderer-start-pos="8235">We would now like to assign our master data record "md_car" to the question we have just created. In that way, the answers participants give to the question are saved in the master data record. To do this, proceed as follows:</p>
<ul class="ak-ul wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-indent-level="1">
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="8464">Open the survey menu and click on the link <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Master data allocation</strong>.<img src="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/21018333458962"></p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="8464">The question that we have created for our master data survey is now displayed in the overview of master data allocations.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-left" data-renderer-start-pos="8464">Click on the pencil icon on the far right of the row.<img src="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/21018302896658"></p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="8464">From the <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Allocation to master data variable</strong> drop-down menu on the far right, select the master data record “md_car:Car owner”.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-left" data-renderer-start-pos="8464">Click on <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Save</strong>.<img src="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/21018302897554"></p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="8464">The green paperclip symbol indicates that the master data record has been correctly assigned to the question and that the values of the survey variables and of the master data record match.<img src="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/21018302901522"></p>
