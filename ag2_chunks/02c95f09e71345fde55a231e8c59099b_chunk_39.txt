-pos="33087">In order to create an export limited to particular disposition codes and/or containing disposition codes you need additional read rights for<em data-renderer-mark="true"> show_dispcode</em>. The results of protected questions are excluded from raw data exports in principle. Only by deactivating the question protection and explicitly re-activating the respective variables in the export macro afterwards, can you include these data in the export.</p>

<h1 id="h_01HGKSMPRSER9ENY5QWWRZZ863" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">FAQ</h1>
<h3 id="h_01HGKSMPRSGNQXCQXD5QXJP6N" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">How do I grant access to EFS Survey Status?</h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Create a team with appropriate access rights, grant access to specific projects, assign necessary ACL rights, and create staff accounts for the desired candidates.</p>
<h3 id="h_01HGKSMPRSKNQXCQXD5QXJP6N" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">What reporting features are available in EFS Survey Status?</h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">EFS Survey Status offers field reports, drop-out statistics, online statistics, and open-ended answers. Additional features include respondent exports and automatically generated export data records.</p>
<h3 id="h_01HGKSMPRSNQXCQXD5QXJP6N" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">How can I restrict or split the field report?</h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Use the Configuration button to restrict the field report to specific disposition codes or variable characteristics. To split the report, use the Split tab and select a desired split variable.</p>
<h3 id="h_01HGKSMPRSQQXCQXD5QXJP6N" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">How do I set up automatic export data records?</h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Create an export macro in the Export macros menu, configuring settings such as frequency, start and end dates, and variables to be exported. Assign appropriate access rights to make the exports available in EFS Survey Status.</p>
