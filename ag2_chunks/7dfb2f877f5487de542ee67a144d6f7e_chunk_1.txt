true">Tivian’s Privacy Assistant</strong></p>
<ul class="ak-ul wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-indent-level="1">
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="1653">enables users to identify (“flag”) personal data prior to the survey to ensure complete and accurate deletion of personal data which is no longer needed after the survey.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="1827">supports users to create a “consent form” to obtain informed, explicit, and valid consent from their respondents.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="1943">offers a library of purpose statements to provide adequate information to respondents about the purpose of data processing.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="2072">helps users to provide statutory information to their respondents as required by the GDPR, e.g. contact information, individual rights, retention period, and many more.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="2243">allows users to create an information sheet for respondents on their personal data which can be exported (data portability).</p>
</li>
</ul>
<div class="fabric-editor-block-mark fabric-editor-alignment cc-5inqjd wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-align="center">
<h2 id="Activating-the-Privacy-Assistant" data-renderer-start-pos="2372">Activating the Privacy Assistant</h2>
</div>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-renderer-start-pos="2407">You activate the <em data-renderer-mark="true">Privacy Assistant</em> to conduct the surveys in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) for new and existing projects. The <em data-renderer-mark="true">Privacy Assistant</em> is available for both new and existing projects and can be used for the following project types: <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Anonymous survey</strong>, <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Personalized survey</strong>, <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Employee survey</strong>, <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Panel survey</strong>, and <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Master data survey</strong>.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"> </p>
<h3 id="New-projects" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-renderer-start-pos="2776">New projects</h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-renderer-start-pos="2790">You activate the <em data-renderer-mark="true">Privacy Assistant</em> for a new project by ticking the corresponding option under the tab <strong data-renderer-mark="true">New project</strong>. The <em data-renderer-mark="true">Assistant</em> is already activated by default. The survey status can only be set to <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Active</strong> once the duration of the data retention period has been configured. You deactivate the <em data-renderer-mark="true">Privacy Assistant</em> by unchecking the box.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-renderer-start-pos="2790"><img src="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/21017221232402"></p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-renderer-start-pos="2790">You click on the info link to get a brief overview of the most important general data protection regulations for you as a user of <em data-renderer-mark="true">Survey</em>. There you will also find an overview of the scope of functionalities available in the <em data-renderer-mark="true">Privacy Assistant</em>.</p>
<h3 id="Existing-projects" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-renderer-start-pos="3370">Existing projects</h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-renderer-start-pos="3389">For existing projects, you activate the <em data-renderer-mark="true">Privacy Assistant </em>by checking the Option under <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Project properties</strong> and clicking <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Save</strong>.  The survey status is then automatically set to <strong data-renderer-mark="true">No further participations</strong>. This setting will remain in effect until you have configured the data retention period. This also applies to copies of projects that have been activated without the <em data-renderer-mark="true">Privacy Assistant</em>.</p>
