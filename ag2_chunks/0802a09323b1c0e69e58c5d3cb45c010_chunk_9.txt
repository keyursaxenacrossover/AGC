em>Action Board</em> (with a certain due date, where appropriate).</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Send e-mail</strong>: You can choose to send an e-mail to the guide initiator or to a panelist whose e-mail address you should enter. Also, select the appropriate mail template.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Update Master data</strong>: The date on which the successful process occurred is added to the selected master data variable.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">For a process that has been classified as <em>unsuccessful</em>, only the actions <strong>Send e-mail</strong> and <strong>Update Master data </strong>are available.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">In addition, you now have a process control error log that logs possible errors that may occur when executing the defined actions after a successful or unsuccessful process.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">The actions can be combined with each other and the same action can be selected several times.</p>
