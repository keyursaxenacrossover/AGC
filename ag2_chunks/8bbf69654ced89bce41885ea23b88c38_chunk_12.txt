<h1 id="severity_levels">Severity Levels</h1>
<p>There are three severity levels assigned to exception reports:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p><strong>Minor:</strong> One installation or a not commonly used Service is not available in one environment.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Medium:</strong> Multiple Installations or a heavily used Service (e.g., Mailing) is not available in one environment.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Major:</strong> All installations in one environment are not available.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<h1 id="h_01J8GYT1K0KBB8GBYZAYJTE2C2">Guidelines to follow while elevating to Engineering</h1>
<p>When submitting a ticket, it is important to provide the necessary information so the team can assess and address the issue as quickly as possible. Below are the required fields and guidelines for every ticket.</p>
<p>Important &amp; relevant information: Engineering has NO ACCESS to production or sandbox instances. Also, no 24.2 bugfix is going to be considered as it's deprecated.</p>
