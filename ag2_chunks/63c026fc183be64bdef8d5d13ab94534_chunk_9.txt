 more prizes, click on the <strong>Add row</strong> button, enter data, and Save.</li>
<li>If necessary, delete redundant rows by activating the “Delete” checkbox and saving.</li>
</ol>
<h4 id="h_01J3FG2NZQT665ZHTJSJ7EYM92" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">3. Drawing the Winners</h4>
<ol class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">
<li>Go to the <strong>3. Prize draw</strong> tab.</li>
<li>Review all details carefully.</li>
<li>Click on the <strong>Assign winners</strong> button to draw winners.</li>
<li>The status changes to “Winners drawn” and the <strong>4. Winner</strong> tab opens.</li>
</ol>
<h4 id="h_01J3FG2NZRH0FRB2D2RYX0G8VC" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">4. Paying the Winners</h4>
<ol class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">
<li>On the <strong>4. Winner</strong> tab, view the overview of winners with name, email address, and assigned prize.</li>
<li>Use the search function to select winners.</li>
<li>Use the “Actions” checkboxes and drop-down list to notify winners or change their status.</li>
<li>Export winner data using the Excel or CSV export button.</li>
</ol>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>Informing Winners:</strong></p>
<ol class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">
<li>Select winners and apply the “Send e-mail” action.</li>
<li>Write a notification or use a mail template of type “Winner mail”.</li>
<li>Use wildcards: Participant data, master data, #prize_draw# (Title of prize draw), #prize# (Prize).</li>
<li>The status changes to “Winners informed” after sending.</li>
</ol>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>Changing Statuses:</strong></p>
<ol class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">
<li>Search for winners, activate the “Actions” checkboxes.</li>
<li>Select “Set status to notified” or “Set status to paid out” and confirm with Execute.</li>
</ol>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>Exporting Winners’ Data:</strong></p>
<ol class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">
<li>Export contact details (first name, last name, email address), allocated prize, and status using Excel or CSV export buttons.</li>
<li>Read rights for “panel_email_gratification” are required to view email addresses.</li>
</ol>
<h3 id="h_01J3FG2NZR4AQ14ADR06NSZT4V" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Archiving Prize Draws</h3>
<ol class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">
<li>Click the <strong>Archive</strong> icon to archive a prize draw.</li>
<li>Archived prize draws can be read but not edited.</li>
<li>Click the <strong>Reactivate</strong> icon to reactivate a prize draw.</li>
</ol>
<h2 id="campaigns" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Campaigns</h2>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">You can conduct promotional campaigns to reward subscribers who invite new subscribers. Panelists are asked to invite others during a defined period, using a predefined invitation mail provided on the panel site. They can add personal comments and send the mail to friends. Panelists receive a reward for each successfully canvassed friend who registers and completes the master data survey. These new candidates are assigned a separate way of entry to distinguish them in the system.</p>
<h3 id="h_01J3FG9NG42TVXBFQ0G9706Y5A" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Overview of Campaigns</h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">In the People → Incentive management → Campaigns menu, you can see all promotional campaigns.</p>
<ul class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">
<li>
<strong>Displayed Information</strong>: Participating group and current status.</li>
<li>
<strong>Search Modes</strong>: Simple and extended search available. Extended search can filter campaigns started within a certain period.</li>
<li>
<strong>View Options</strong>: Click the View button to activate/deactivate table columns or change rows per page.</li>
<li>
<strong>Export Options</strong>: Download the list using the Excel export / CSV export buttons.</li>
</ul>
<h3 id="h_01J3FG9NG459DG81KNATVHB5E1" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Actions in the “Actions” Column:</h3>
<ul class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">
<li>
<strong>Edit</strong>: Opens the edit dialog.</li>
<li>
<strong>Copy Campaign</strong>: Creates a copy of the campaign.</li>
<li>
<strong>Campaign Statistics</strong>: Shows the results of the campaign.</li>
<li>
<strong>Archive</strong>: Assigns the status “archived” to the campaign (cannot be reactivated).</li>
<li>
<strong>Delete</strong>: Removes the campaign.</li>
</ul>
