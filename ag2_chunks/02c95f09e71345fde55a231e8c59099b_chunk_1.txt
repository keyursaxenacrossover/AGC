<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-renderer-start-pos="1088">In order for the team to be able to view the data of a project, it must first be granted read rights to the desired project. To do so, locate the project in the project list and assign read rights.</p>

<h3 id="Assigning-Access-Rights-to-the-Desired-Data" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-renderer-start-pos="1419"><strong data-renderer-mark="true">Assigning Access Rights to the Desired Data</strong></h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-renderer-start-pos="1464">Next you have to decide which data the members of the new team should have access to. Then, assign the respective ACL rights. </p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-renderer-start-pos="1592">You can combine the following rights:</p>
<ul class="ak-ul wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-indent-level="1">
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="1633">“read” right to <em data-renderer-mark="true">report_view_fieldrep</em>: Field report</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="1687">“read” right to <em data-renderer-mark="true">report_onlinestat</em>: Online statistics</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="1743">“read” right to <em data-renderer-mark="true">report_onlinerep</em>: Open-ended answers</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="1799">“read” right to <em data-renderer-mark="true">ct42partadm</em>: Respondent export</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="1849">“read” right to <em data-renderer-mark="true">reportlist</em> in combination with read right to the respective report or the respective export macro: reports created with <em data-renderer-mark="true">EFS Reporting +</em> and raw data exports.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-renderer-start-pos="2026">Also, check whether the team has access rights to the <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Detail view </strong>menu:. Normally the <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Detail view </strong>in <em data-renderer-mark="true">EFS Survey Status</em> should be <em data-renderer-mark="true">completely disabled</em>, as it permits direct access to the data records of the participants.</p>
<h3 id="Creating-Customer-or-Staff-Accounts" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-renderer-start-pos="2245"><strong data-renderer-mark="true">Creating Customer or Staff Accounts</strong></h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-renderer-start-pos="2282">Create accounts for your staff or customers. In the field “To which teams should the new staff member be added?”, select the newly created team. This team must also be the primary team for the new staff member.</p>
