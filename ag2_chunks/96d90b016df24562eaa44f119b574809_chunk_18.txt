<h3 id="h_01HGKSMPRS9JVGC6E9EA3DB98R" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">What is the purpose of the Action Planner?</h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">The Action Planner allows monitoring and administration of the follow-up process in employee surveys after the delivery of result reports. It helps track and manage actions taken based on survey results.</p>
<h3 id="h_01HGKSMPRSYYE7GMM7DZ0VCKXS" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">How can I create a new action in the Action Planner?</h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">To create a new action, click on the "Create action" button, enter the necessary data in the provided fields, and click "Save". You can optionally choose to add another action after saving.</p>
<h3 id="h_01HGKSMPRSBXQJ3YJXQGQX3YBW" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">What are workflows in the Action Planner?</h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Workflows in the Action Planner are predefined processes that allow users to assign, escalate, close, and reopen tasks, as well as invite participants to follow-up surveys. They help streamline the follow-up process and ensure consistent handling of actions.</p>
