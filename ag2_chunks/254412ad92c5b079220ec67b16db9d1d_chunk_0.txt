<h2 id="h_01HGKSMPRS1QWJZ3X9QBZV8XNM">Data Storage Location</h2>
<p>Tivian stores its data at the company's server center located in Frankfurt, Germany. This facility is designed to provide reliable protection against unauthorized third-party access.</p>
<p> </p>
<h2 id="h_01HGKSMPRS2XKJQ9YFGD5NZPVR">Security and Certification</h2>
<p>The data center in Frankfurt is BSI-certified and adheres to extremely stringent data protection and security standards. Key points about the security and certification include:</p>
<ul>
<li>Compliance with ISO 27001 standards</li>
<li>Based on the German IT baseline protection standard (<em>IT-Grundschutz</em>)</li>
<li>Utilizes the same standard used by German government bodies</li>
<li>Certification awarded following an audit by an ISO 27001 baseline protection auditor</li>
<li>The auditor is certified by the German Federal Office for Information Security (BSI)</li>
</ul>
