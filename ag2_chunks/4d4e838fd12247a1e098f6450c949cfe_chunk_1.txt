<h2 id="captcha">Captcha</h2>
<p>Captcha features help prevent automated attacks on your panel websites. This section covers recent changes to Captcha implementation and how to activate different Captcha options.</p>
<h3 id="captcha-changes">Changes to Captcha Features</h3>
<p>On panel websites using the Responsive v2 layout, the previous CAPTCHA implementation has been replaced by ALTCHA, a modern, free, open-source alternative. ALTCHA is GDPR compliant, does not use external services, cookies, or fingerprinting, and does not track users.</p>
