<h3 id="add-or-change-favicon-logo-or-image" class="sc-eNNmBn lmEGgd wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>Add or change favicon, logo, or image</strong></h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">You can even further personalize your portal‘s look by adding a favicon, logo, and/or portal image on the field <strong>Look &amp; Feel</strong>. When adding an image, you can allocate a background image to the portal tile, which will be visible in the portal overview subsequently. Add a favicon (the symbol displayed in a browser tab is called favicon), logo, or portal image by either</p>
<ul class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">
<li>dragging &amp; dropping a file into the field, or</li>
<li>clicking into the favicon/logo field and select an image from your computer.</li>
</ul>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Your image/logo will be saved and added to your portal.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Currently supported file types are GIF, PNG, and JPEG. It is recommended to keep file sizes small as the images will not be processed after upload.</p>
<ul class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">
<li><span style="color: #333333;">Adapting the size of the image is only possible before upload.</span></li>
</ul>
