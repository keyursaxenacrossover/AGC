<h3 id="creating-user-roles" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>Creating user roles</strong></h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">In the <strong>Projects</strong> → {Selected project} → <strong>Process configuration</strong> menu, click on the <strong>Create role</strong> button on the <strong>Roles</strong> tab.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><img src="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/21013838179346" alt="Create role.jpg"></p>
<ul class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">
<li>
<p>The system automatically assigns an ID to the role.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Select a label and an abbreviation.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Specify which reports users with this new role are to receive.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Confirm by clicking on <strong>Save</strong>.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<h3 id="changing-the-allocation-of-roles-to-reports" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>Changing the allocation of roles to reports</strong></h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">You can change the allocation of roles to reports later. To do so, go to the role overview and use the <strong>Edit</strong> icon to open the editing dialog. In the “Allocation of role to report” section, you can find all defined reports. Change the allocation as required.</p>
<h3 id="changing-rights-for-a-user-role" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>Changing rights for a user role</strong></h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Open the <strong>Projects</strong> → { Selected project} → <strong>Process configuration</strong> menu. On the <strong>Roles</strong> tab, you can find an overview of the existing roles. Select the role whose rights you intend to change and click the <strong>Rights Administration</strong> icon. A list of the available rights is opened. Rights that can not be changed, e.g. because they relate to a deactivated feature (e.g. Logistics or Action Planner), or because a more general right has been deactivated, are colored in grey.</p>

<li>
<p>Assign the rights as required and confirm with <strong>Save</strong>.</
