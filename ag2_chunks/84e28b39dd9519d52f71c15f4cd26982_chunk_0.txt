<p>The Platform Administration section covers the various administrative tools and options available to users in the EFS system, focusing on the menu bar located in the upper right corner of the interface.</p>

<h2 id="menu_bar">Menu Bar Overview</h2>
<p>The menu bar in the upper right corner of the EFS interface contains three drop-down menus: System, Support, and User. Additionally, you can find the Language button to switch your preferred display language.</p>

<h2 id="system_menu">System Menu</h2>
<p>The System menu provides access to the following items:</p>
<ul>
<li>Users</li>
<li>Libraries</li>
<li>Options</li>
</ul>
<p>For more detailed information about the System menu, please refer to the dedicated <a href="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/en-us/articles/20371037479442-System">System documentation.</a></p>
<p> </p>
<h2 id="support_menu">Support Menu</h2>
<p>The Support menu offers access to various resources and features:</p>
<ul>
<li>Video tutorials</li>
<li>Online Documentation</li>
<li>Support contact option</li>
<li>Feedback feature</li>
<li>Information on data protection and GDPR</li>
</ul>
<h2 id="profile_menu">Profile Menu</h2>
<p>The Profile menu allows users to manage their account and personal settings. Through this menu, you can:</p>
<ul>
<li>Edit your account information</li>
<li>Adjust personal settings</li>
<li>Log out of the system</li>
</ul>
<p>For more information about the Profile menu, please consult the dedicated <a href="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/en-us/articles/20230107697298-Profile">Profile documentation.</a></p>

<p>To change the display language, click on the Language button in the upper right corner of the interface and select your preferred language from the available options.</p>
