<h2 id="Mail-Report" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-renderer-start-pos="519"><strong data-renderer-mark="true">Mail Report</strong></h2>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-renderer-start-pos="532">For personalized surveys, the <em data-renderer-mark="true">Mail report</em> is your go-to place for an overview of all the emails you have sent during your survey. Once you send a mail from the <em data-renderer-mark="true">Participant administration</em>, a report entry is created, and you can display information on the delivery process and, if necessary, also intervene. If your project is an anonymous survey, you can monitor participation directly from the <a class="cc-1rn59kg" title="https://qbdocs.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/DOC/pages/4004937734/How-to+Monitoring+the+Progress+of+my+Survey#Online-Statistics" href="https://qbdocs.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/DOC/pages/4004937734/How-to+Monitoring+the+Progress+of+my+Survey#Online-Statistics" data-testid="link-with-safety" data-renderer-mark="true"><em data-renderer-mark="true">Online statistics</em></a>.</p>
<h3 id="Monitoring-and-Managing-the-Delivery-Process" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-renderer-start-pos="946"><strong data-renderer-mark="true">Monitoring and Managing the Delivery Process</strong></h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-renderer-start-pos="992">If you would like to check, who you invited to your survey, what you wrote in the invitation mail and which emails were not delivered, just follow the steps below:</p>
<ul class="ak-ul wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-indent-level="1">
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="1158">Open your project.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="1180">In the menu on the left-hand side, select the <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Mail Report</strong> menu item.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-renderer-start-pos="1252">In the upper part of the screen, a graphic shows all emails sent for the given project. In the bottom left part, you can see the total number of emails sent – in the below example, it’s 6. To the right, you can see the different statuses of the mails. In our example below, 5 emails have been sent and one is queued, i.e. it has been created, but not been sent yet. This information is also presented on a percentage basis in the form of colored bars.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><img src="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/21013837923474"></p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-renderer-start-pos="1252">In the lower part of the page, a table lists all mailing processes for your project.</p>
<ul class="ak-ul wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-indent-level="1">
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="1792">Click on the internal ID or on the subject of the mailing process to display details on all individual mails sent.<img src="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/21013837939346"></p>
