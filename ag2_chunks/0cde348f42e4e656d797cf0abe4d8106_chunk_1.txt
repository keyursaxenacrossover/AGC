<h3 id="faq_1">Why is the visual design of a survey important?</h3>
<p>A well-designed survey with an attractive background and your organization's logo creates a professional image and helps establish a clear connection with the respondents, potentially increasing participation rates.</p>
<h3 id="faq_2">What should be included in the email invitation for a survey?</h3>
<p>The email invitation should be brief, describe the survey's purpose, state the estimated completion time, and mention any incentives for participation. It serves as a "teaser" to encourage recipients to take the survey.</p>
<h3 id="faq_3">What information should be included in the survey's introductory text?</h3>
<p>The introductory text should cover the type of survey, its background and purpose, why the recipient was selected, the importance of participation, how results will be used, estimated completion time, data confidentiality, and sender information.</p>
