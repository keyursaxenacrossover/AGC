<h3 id="procedure-in-portals"><strong>Procedure in <em>Portals</em></strong></h3>
<p><span style="color: #091e42;">In the CMS area of Portals, you go to the sub-navigation: You click on </span><strong>Settings</strong><span style="color: #091e42;">, select the tab </span><strong>Target Pages</strong><span style="color: #091e42;"> and simply choose </span><strong>Reports</strong><span style="color: #091e42;"> in the line „Reports“. </span></p>
<p>You then move to the following path in the sub-navigation to release the projects for the module<strong> Report list</strong>:</p>
<p>[<em>selected project</em>] → <em>Target Pages</em> → <em>Reports</em> → <em>+ New module</em> → <em>Report list</em></p>
<p>In the next step, you set which projects are to be included in your report list. You can select the projects individually or all.</p>
