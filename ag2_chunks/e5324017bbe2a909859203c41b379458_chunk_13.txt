<h4 id="My-current-installation-has-an-Identity-Provider-(IdP)-enabled-and-the-IdP-ID-is-set.-Thus-I-am-not-able-to-login-to-the-clients-IdP---How-can-I-login-to-that-installation?" data-renderer-start-pos="3272"><strong data-renderer-mark="true">My current installation has an Identity Provider (IdP) enabled and the IdP ID is set. Thus I am not able to login to the clients IdP - How can I login to that installation?</strong></h4>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="3446">The IdP ID directly redirect the a user to the respective Identity Provider. But you can create a request on your own to navigate to Okta without the IdP ID. The easiest way to achieve that is by looking at the network traffic in your browser (see <a class="cc-1rn59kg" title="https://developers.google.com/web/tools/chrome-devtools/network" href="https://developers.google.com/web/tools/chrome-devtools/network" data-testid="link-with-safety" data-renderer-mark="true">https://developers.google.com/web/tools/chrome-devtools/network</a>).</p>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="3761">There will be a request to access.questback.com ending with /authorize in the path part of the URL. Just copy that request and remove the idp_id from the search parameters. This will bring you to the Okta without the IdP ID. Then you can login as normal by providing your email address.</p>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="4049">Another way is to navigate to <a class="cc-1rn59kg" title="https://access.questback.com" href="https://access.questback.com/" data-testid="link-with-safety" data-renderer-mark="true">https://access.questback.com</a> and login there. After successfull login get navigate to the installation of choice. You should be logged in immediately (for portals you will have to click sign-in)</p>

<h2 id="header-10">Customizations and Configurations</h2>
<h4 id="What-happens-to-customizations-regarding-the-login-experience-for-users-in-Portals?" data-renderer-start-pos="4"><strong data-renderer-mark="true">What happens to customizations regarding the login experience for users in Portals?</strong></h4>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="89">The login experience is delivered by our Identity Provider (Okta). Therefore customization regarding the login itself will not be shown anymore. In the first phase of the project (Winter release) custom login screens are not yet supported. Pages like the public-home screen still stay active. They will now contain a login button instead of the login form. The login button is customizable and will lead to the Okta login.</p>

<p data-renderer-start-pos="596">The login experience is delivered by our Identity Provider (Okta). Therefore recent customizations especially regarding the login screen will not be shown anymore.</p>
