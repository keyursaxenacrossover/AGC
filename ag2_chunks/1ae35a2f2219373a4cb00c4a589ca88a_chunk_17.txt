Data" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-renderer-start-pos="20204"><strong data-renderer-mark="true">Update Data</strong></h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-renderer-start-pos="20217">To update your panelist data with the information on the master data "md_day_of_birth" and "md_month_of_birth", we first create an export of all your panelist data. To do this, proceed as follows:</p>
<ul class="ak-ul wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-indent-level="1">
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="20417">In the <em data-renderer-mark="true">People</em> module, open the <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Export</strong> menu item on the left-hand side.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="20491">Keep the default setting <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Panel data (master data, participant data)</strong> and click <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Continue</strong>.<img src="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/21014812826002"></p>
</li>
</ul>
<ul class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">
<li data-renderer-start-pos="20491">Keep all default settings and select the option <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Participant and master data</strong> under <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Which export template shall be used?</strong>.</li>
<li data-renderer-start-pos="20491">Click on <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Export</strong>.</li>
<li data-renderer-start-pos="20491">Your export is created. Click on <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Copy file to local PC</strong>.</li>
<li data-renderer-start-pos="20491">Open the export file and delete all cells except for the columns u_firstname, u_name, u_email, md_day_of_birth and md_month_of_birth.</li>
<li data-renderer-start-pos="20491">Now enter the corresponding data of your panelists in the columns “md_day_of_birth” and “md_month_of_birth”.</li>
<li data-renderer-start-pos="20491"><img src="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/21014859729682"></li>
<li data-renderer-start-pos="20491">Save the file.</li>
</ul>
