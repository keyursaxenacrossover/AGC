<h2 id="printing-pretest-comments" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>Printing pretest comments</strong></h2>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">If you want to print the questionnaire with the pretest comments, you can use the regular print function under <strong>Projects</strong> → {Selected project} → <strong>Print version</strong>. Then, you only need to tick the “Show pretest comments” checkbox.</p>
<h2 id="selecting-the-print-language" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>Selecting the print language</strong></h2>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">When printing the questionnaire (<strong>Projects</strong> → {Selected Project} → <strong>Print version</strong>), you can also specify the language using a drop-down list. The titles originally entered for the questionnaire pages cannot be changed for printing.</p>
<h2 id="codebook" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>Codebook</strong></h2>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">The codebook contains an overview of all variables and all encodings used in a project. I.e. it shows which memory locations have been created for your questions and how the answers have been encoded. The codebook is used, for example, whenever you want to incorporate control information into your survey, e.g. when setting filters or defining triggers, and when analysing the survey results.</p>
