 individual users to an exchange team you assign access rights for the files of an exchange team to one or more user teams. You may choose from different rights configurations:</p>
<ul class="ak-ul wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-indent-level="1">
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="20468">Upload right: Equivalent to the right “read” to the exchange team. The members of a team with upload rights may upload and download files. They may, however, change or delete only their own files.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="20668">Change right: Equivalent to the right “write” to the exchange team. The members of a team with change rights can upload and download files and may change or delete all files of their exchange team.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="20869">If you assign the rights “read” and “write” to a user team, the members of this team will have all rights to the exchange team and all upload and change rights. However, they will be ignored for the function “Send info mail to team”. This configuration is useful, for example, for teams of project managers who are not involved in the daily operations.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-renderer-start-pos="21225">In order to subsequently assign one or more user teams to an exchange team or to change the initial settings, proceed as follows:</p>
<ul class="ak-ul wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-indent-level="1">
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="21358">Locate the Exchange team in the overview and click on the <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Rights</strong> icon.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="21432">This opens the dialog for rights administration familiar to other <em data-renderer-mark="true">EFS</em> menus. Assign read and/or write rights for the respective exchange team to the desired user teams as required.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="21618">Confirm your selection by clicking on <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Change rights</strong>.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<h3 id="Viewing-the-History" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-renderer-start-pos="21674"><strong data-renderer-mark="true">Viewing the History</strong></h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-renderer-start-pos="21695">All important changes and actions executed using EFS Secure Exchange functions are logged. This allows you to subsequently check whether an action has been carried out and which user is responsible for this action. It is possible, for example, to reconstruct which users downloaded a particular file. In order to view the list of changes, switch to the <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Users</strong>→ <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Exchange teams </strong>→ <strong data-renderer-mark="true">History</strong> menu.</p>
