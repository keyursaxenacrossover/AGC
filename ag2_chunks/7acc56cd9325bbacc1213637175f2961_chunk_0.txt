<h2 id="standard_layout">Standard Layout Options</h2>
<p>EFS Survey offers a standard layout option that allows users to customize various aspects of their survey's appearance:</p>
<ul>
<li>Upload college logos</li>
<li>Modify colors and fonts</li>
<li>Adjust spacing</li>
</ul>
<p> </p>
<h2 id="professional_layout">Professional Layout Editor</h2>
<p>For more advanced customization, EFS Survey provides a professional layout editor:</p>
<ul>
<li>Enables desired layout through adjustments to style sheets</li>
<li>Requires knowledge of HTML and CSS</li>
</ul>
<p> </p>
<h1 id="h_01HGKSMPRSER9ENY5QWWRZZ863">FAQ</h1>
<h3 id="faq_layout_design">Can I design the layout of my survey how I like?</h3>
<p>Yes, EFS Survey provides both standard layout options for basic customization and a professional layout editor for advanced users with HTML and CSS knowledge. This allows you to design your survey layout according to your preferences and requirements.</p>
