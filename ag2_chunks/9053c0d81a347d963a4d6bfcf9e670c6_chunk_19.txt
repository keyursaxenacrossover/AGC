<h3 id="h_01J3FXVTNVZ2086KHB3BQBFS62" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Changing Bonus Points Manually</h3>
<ol class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">
<li>
<p><strong>Open Detail View:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Select the desired panelist and click on <strong>Edit incentives</strong>.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Adjust Bonus Points:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Click on the <strong>Change bonus points</strong> button.</li>
<li>Enter the number of points to add or subtract in the “Number of points” field (use format “number.number” for floating points).</li>
<li>Set the action to “Add” or “Subtract” in the “To member account” field.</li>
<li>Enter an explanatory text in the “Reason” field (visible to the panelist on the account balance page).</li>
<li>Confirm by clicking on <strong>Submit</strong>.</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ol>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>Note:</strong> Write rights for <code>panelbonus</code> are required to manually change a panelist's bonus points.</p>

<h3 id="h_01J3FXXTZJ0H25WXM5MNKBMRRX" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Documenting Contacts</h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>EFS Panel</strong> helps document communication with panelists. Clicking the <strong>Edit contacts</strong> button opens a dialog with three tabs:</p>
<ol class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">
<li>
<strong>Contacts</strong>: Log personal and telephone contacts or e-mail exchanges not conducted via EFS Panel. Use the <strong>Add contact</strong> button to enter new events.</li>
<li>
<strong>Contacts via panel site</strong>: Logs requests made via the contact form on the panel site.</li>
<li>
<strong>Contacts via system</strong>: Logs e-mails sent to panelists via EFS Panel.</li>
</ol>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>Note</strong>: To avoid performance issues, mail archives are cleaned automatically every six months.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>Logging Contacts Outside EFS Panel:</strong></p>
<ol class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">
<li>Open the detail view for the desired panelist.</li>
<li>Click <strong>Edit contacts</strong>.</li>
<li>Click <strong>Add contact</strong> to open the form for new events.</li>
<li>Select the contact method (phone, personal, e-mail) from the “Way of contact” drop-down list.</li>
<li>The editor and date are set automatically.</li>
<li>Enter a subject and detailed information in the “Content” entry form.</li>
<li>Click <strong>Submit</strong> to save the new entry.</li>
</ol>
