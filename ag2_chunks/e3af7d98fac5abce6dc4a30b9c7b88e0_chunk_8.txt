<h2 id="h_01HGKSMPRTAYFY0X4QJDQX9Z0Q" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Session Statistics</h2>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">The <strong>Session statistics</strong> provide information on the activity of visitors and logged-in panelists on the external view of the panel. The following rules apply:</p>
<ul class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">
<li>Activities of all visitors are logged, not just logged-in panelists.</li>
<li>A session spans from the first to the last access to the website.</li>
<li>If a user logs in during a session, all page views in that session count as “performed by logged-in panelists”.</li>
<li>In panels with multiple language versions, each session is assigned to the initial language version.</li>
</ul>
<h3 id="h_01J3G2NECK2KRYHHPSMQF22F7G" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Evaluation Options</h3>
<ul class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">
<li>
<strong>Page Views</strong>: Split into views by logged-in panelists and unidentified persons.</li>
<li>
<strong>Language Version</strong>: Session data can be restricted to a specific language version.</li>
<li>
<strong>Grouping Criteria</strong>: Session data can be grouped by various criteria.</li>
<li>
<strong>Export</strong>: Data can be exported in Excel and CSV formats.</li>
</ul>
<h3 id="h_01J3G2NECM4EA9YE0TK2XAR7TW" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Advanced Analysis</h3>
<ul class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">
<li>
<strong>Website Log</strong>: Individual page views for different language versions.</li>
<li>
<strong>Session Data</strong>: Available on the <strong>Sessions</strong> tab in the detail view of individual panelists.</li>
</ul>
<h3 id="h_01J3G2NECMCPF2TY85FCK40SEW" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">History</h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">On the <strong>History</strong> tab, you can create graphical representations of your results. The data are selected in the <strong>Configurations</strong> dialog.</p>
<h3 id="h_01J3G2NECMHY4BAN3XNJ1Q1RPM" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Details</h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Find the session statistics in the menu <strong>People → Statistics → Session statistics</strong> on the <strong>Details</strong> tab. Detailed explanations of the evaluation options and the contents of the session statistics are provided in the tables.</p>
