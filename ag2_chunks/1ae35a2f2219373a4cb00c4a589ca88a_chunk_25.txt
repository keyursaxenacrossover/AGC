<h3 id='Set-up-Automatic-Action-when-PCI-Value-Drops-to-"Red"' class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-renderer-start-pos="29010"><strong data-renderer-mark="true">Set up Automatic Action when PCI Value Drops to "Red"</strong></h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-renderer-start-pos="29065">Based on your individual PCI configuration, you can now track the reliability of your panelists. By setting up a group, a grouping filter and an update rule, we now want to ensure that panelists with a "Red" PCI value are automatically invited to take a survey on their satisfaction with the panel. This way, you can find out why their reliability has decreased over time and possibly encourage them to actively participate in the panel again. To do this, follow the steps below:</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-renderer-start-pos="29547"><strong data-renderer-mark="true"><u data-renderer-mark="true">Create an email template </u></strong></p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-left" data-renderer-start-pos="29575">Create an email template for the survey invitation to be sent to panelists whose PCI value has been decreased to "Red". Follow the instructionsunder Mail templates. Below you can find an example of what the email could look like.<img src="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/21014812835218"></p>
<h3 id="Create-Survey" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-renderer-start-pos="29810"><strong data-renderer-mark="true">Create Survey</strong></h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-renderer-start-pos="29825">In the EFS <em data-renderer-mark="true">Projects</em> module, create the survey that panelists should receive after their PCI value has decreased to "Red". To do this, select the survey type "Panel survey". Ask about the satisfaction with the content of the surveys in the panel, the frequency or other reasons for the decrease in activity in the panel, for example. If you need help creating a survey project, you can find useful tips in our step-by-step guide "My first survey" and a comprehensive description of the <em>Projects </em>module in our online documentation.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-renderer-start-pos="30356"> </p>
<h3 id="Create-Group.1" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-renderer-start-pos="30358"><strong data-renderer-mark="true">Create Group</strong></h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-renderer-start-pos="30372">Create the new group "PCI value Red" as described under Groups.</p>
