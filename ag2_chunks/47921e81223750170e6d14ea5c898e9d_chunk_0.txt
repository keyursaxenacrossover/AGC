<h2 id="header-1" class="wysiwyg-text-align-left">Portals</h2>
<h3 id="header-3">User management module</h3>
<p>The <em>User management</em> module is a new page module within <em>Portals</em>. Now users who have been assigned the role of HR Manager can manage the accounts of the other portal users.</p>
<h3 id="header-5">Configuration</h3>
<p>Add the new <em>User management</em> module to a column 1 layout page (or create a new page and select the column 1 layout). Open the module configuration.</p>
<p><img src="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/21036840536594" alt="User Management module - Configuration (EN).png"></p>
<p>You have various setting options: You must assign a heading. You select the invitation template. You also specify:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>to which panel group the participants will be added</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>how many invitations may be sent per portal</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>which portal roles will be visible</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>which roles may be assigned to the other portal users</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>Furthermore, you can provide additional user information via variables. The e-mail address as contact information of the panelist is mandatory.</p>
<h4 id="header-7">The exterior view of the new module</h4>
<p>In the external view of the new module you have, for example, the possibility to invite users, to filter the user list by type and status, and a table of all users. By default, the following user information is available:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Name</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>E-mail address</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Registration</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Status</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Account type</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><img src="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/21036840256530" alt="User management - exterior view (EN).png"></p>
<p> </p>
<p> </p>
<p>You can assign the different portal roles to a portal user.</p>
<p>The three icons can be used to delete the individual users, send an e-mail regarding the password reset and a new invitation email.</p>
<p> </p>
<p><img src="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/21036853823762" alt="User management - icons (EN).png"></p>
<p>The module can only be accessed by users who have been assigned the HR Manager role.</p>

<h3 id="header-9">IDEA Guide: Offline participation</h3>
<p>To invite team members who do not have their own email address or have limited internet access to an IDEA guide survey, you can create vouchers to give these so-called offline users access to the survey and the IDEA Guide voting page.</p>
<h4 id="header-11">Configuration</h4>
<p>Click on the <strong>Invitation</strong> tab of the guide configuration of your IDEA guide and select an e-mail template for the voucher codes.</p>
<p><img src="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/21036869207442" alt="Offline users e-mail settings.png"></p>
<p> </p>
<p>Then activate the "Allow offline participations" option and define the number of possible offline participants (max. 100). You also need to select a panel group to which the offline participants will be assigned.</p>
