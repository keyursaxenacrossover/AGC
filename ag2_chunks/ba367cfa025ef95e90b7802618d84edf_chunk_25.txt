wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>Proper settings in Portal:</strong></p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">For the <em>Portals</em> app to be properly displayed, you have to make the following settings:</p>
<ul class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">
<li>Open the <strong>Settings</strong> page of your portal.</li>
<li>Under the <strong>General settings</strong> menu item, find the option <strong>Select whether a header with X-Frame-Options should be sent to the client</strong>.</li>
<li>From the drop-down menu to the right, choose <strong>Do not send</strong> for the above option.</li>
</ul>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><img src="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/21015039209106" alt="image2024-2-14_9-53-24.png"></p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"> </p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><em>Information: If you do make this setting and try to use the Portals app, this will lead to a display error in the app.</em></p>

<h1 id="h_01HGKSMPRSER9ENY5QWWRZZ863" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">FAQ</h1>
<h3 id="h_01HGKSMPRSJ0YJQX7QJXBXQX8Z4" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">How do I restrict access to my portal?</h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">You can restrict access by setting specific panelist statuses in the Access Permissions tab under Settings. You can also limit access to particular panel groups.</p>
<h3 id="h_01HGKSMPRSN0YJQX7QJXBXQX8Z5" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Can I customize the look of my portal?</h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Yes, you can customize colors, and fonts, and add a logo or favicon in the Look &amp; Feel section. You can also use custom CSS for more advanced customization.</p>
<h3 id="h_01HGKSMPRSS0YJQX7QJXBXQX8Z6" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">How do I add new content to my portal?</h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">You can add new content by creating discussion boards, adding topics, and using various page modules like Text and Multimedia, Shoutbox, or Survey modules.</p>
