<h3 id="h_01J3FG2NZQSM2JD2HZ1MTV97MJ" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Conducting a Prize Draw</h3>
<h4 id="h_01J3FG2NZQTX3502C52KW46R7Z" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">1. Creating a Prize Draw</h4>
<ol class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">
<li>Click on the <strong>Create prize draw</strong> button.</li>
<li>Enter data in the fields “Title of prize draw” and “Description”.</li>
<li>Select the group category and participating group from the drop-down list.</li>
<li>Confirm by clicking on the <strong>Save</strong> button.</li>
</ol>
<h4 id="h_01J3FG2NZQHFH04X90YHVGQ7JC" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">2. Defining Prizes</h4>
<ol class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">
<li>On the <strong>2. Prizes</strong> tab, enter the name, number, and price per unit for each prize.</li>
<li>Click <strong>Save</strong> to confirm entries.</li>
<li>To add more prizes, click on the <strong>Add row</strong> button, enter data, and Save.</li>
<li>If necessary, delete redundant rows by activating the “Delete” checkbox and saving.</li>
</ol>
<h4 id="h_01J3FG2NZQT665ZHTJSJ7EYM92" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">3. Drawing the Winners</h4>
<ol class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">
<li>Go to the <strong>3. Prize draw</strong> tab.</li>
<li>Review all details carefully.</li>
<li>Click on the <strong>Assign winners</strong> button to draw winners.</li>
<li>The status changes to “Winners drawn” and the <strong>4. Winner</strong> tab opens.</li>
</ol>
