ing-an-existing-website" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>Copying an Existing Website</strong></h3>
<ol class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify" start="1">
<li>
<p>Switch to the <strong>Website</strong> menu.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Search for the website you wish to copy. In the “Actions” column, click on the <strong>Copy website</strong> icon.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Enter the name of the new website.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Specify the existing content you wish to copy. You can copy messages, wildcards, page modules, templates and includes, form configuration, news items, FAQ, quick polls, bulletin boards, mail templates and bonus items.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Specify the language version you wish to copy.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Confirm by clicking on <strong>Create</strong>.</p>
</li>
</ol>
<h3 id="information-5" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Information</h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Please note:</p>
<ul class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">
<li>
<p>At least one language version must be copied.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Results from quick polls, contributions to forums, discussions, chats, blogs, and download files are not copied.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>The counter registering the hits for news items is reset during copying.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Social Insight Connect: The settings on the language version-specific tab<strong> Facebook configuration</strong> are not copied, Social Insight Connect will be deactivated in the copy.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"> </p>
<h3 id="copying-an-existing-language-version" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>Copying an Existing Language Version</strong></h3>
<ol class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify" start="1">
<li>
<p>Switch to the<strong> Website</strong> menu.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Search for the language version you wish to copy. In the “Actions” column, click on the <strong>Copy language version</strong> icon.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Enter the name of the new language version.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Specify the existing content you wish to copy. You can copy messages, wildcards, news items, mail templates, bonus items, quick polls, page modules, and templates, and includes, form configuration, FAQ, forums, download sections, discussions, chats, and blogs. In language versions with Social Insight Connect, the settings on the tab <strong>Facebook configuration</strong> are not copied, Social Insight Connect will be deactivated in the copy.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Confirm by clicking on <strong>Create</strong>.</p>
</li>
</ol>
