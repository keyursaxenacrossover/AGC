<h1 id="h_01HGKSMPRSER9ENY5QWWRZZ863">FAQ</h1>
<h3 id="h_01HGKSMPRT6YJGQ0XQJD4WNPVX">What information does the GDPR activities Log provide?</h3>
<p>The GDPR activities Log provides information about GDPR-related actions, including the date of the action, the specific action taken, the user who performed the action, and a detailed log entry.</p>
<h3 id="h_01HGKSMPRT7QJXZ3QJGX8NKPVT">Can I access the GDPR activities Log from other modules?</h3>
<p>The GDPR activities Log is primarily accessible through the People module. If you need to access it from other areas of the system, please consult your system administrator or refer to additional documentation.</p>
