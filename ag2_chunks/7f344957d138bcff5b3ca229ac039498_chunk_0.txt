<article>
<p>This article addresses the question of whether survey data is deleted following export in our survey platform. It provides clear information about data retention and export policies.</p>
<p> </p>
<h1 id="h_01HGKSMPRSYYE7GMM7DZ0VCKXK">Information</h1>
<p>No. Your survey data will only be deleted if you cancel your account. You can download your survey results as many times as you like as long as you hold a valid license. Data will not be deleted when exported.</p>
<p> </p>
<h1 id="h_01HGKSMPRSER9ENY5QWWRZZ863">FAQ</h1>
<h3 id="faq_data_deletion">Is survey data deleted following export?</h3>
<p>No, your survey data is not deleted after exporting. The data remains in our system and can be accessed and exported multiple times as long as you have a valid license.</p>
<h3 id="faq_multiple_exports">Can I export my survey data more than once?</h3>
<p>Yes, you can download your survey results as many times as you like, as long as you hold a valid license.</p>
<h3 id="faq_data_retention_period">How long is my survey data retained?</h3>
<p>Your survey data is retained for as long as you maintain an active account with a valid license. The data is only deleted if you cancel your account.</p>
