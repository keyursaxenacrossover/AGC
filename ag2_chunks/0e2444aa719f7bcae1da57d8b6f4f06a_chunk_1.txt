 groups by topic for better organization.</li>
<li>
<strong>Edit Group:</strong> Change category, title, and description.</li>
<li>
<strong>History:</strong> View actions performed during group creation.</li>
<li>
<strong>Copy Group:</strong> Duplicate a group.</li>
<li>
<strong>Archive/Delete Group:</strong> Archive or delete groups as needed.</li>

<strong>Batch Actions:</strong> Perform actions on multiple groups at once (e.g., delete, empty, archive/reactivate).</li>

<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>Default Panel Group:</strong></p>
<ul class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">

</ul>
<h3 id="h_01J3FZR3PV26VVE6F5TJZWXBJH" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Organizing Groups into Categories</h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>Managing Categories:</strong></p>
<ul class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">
<li>Open the manage categories dialog from the group overview.</li>
<li>Create, edit, or delete categories, and view existing categories with labels, descriptions, and number of assigned groups.</li>
</ul>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>Assigning Groups to Categories:</strong></p>
<ul class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">
<li>When creating a group, select the appropriate category from the drop-down list.</li>
<li>Change the category of an existing group by editing its properties.</li>
</ul>
<h3 id="h_01J3FZR3PVP7HAYTV0H0KG5QDJ" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Marking Groups for Better Monitoring</h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>Monitored Groups:</strong></p>
<ul class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">
<li>Specially marked in the list of groups.</li>
<li>Use the “Monitored groups” filter in extended search to include/exclude them from the display.</li>
<li>Track group size over time via <strong>People → Statistics → Panel overview → Group history</strong>.</li>
<li>Mark a group as monitored by editing the group and checking the “Mark group as 'monitored group'” option.</li>
</ul>
<h3 id="h_01J3FZR3PV3C2CNSF870C3KYDJ" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Creating and Importing Groups</h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>Creating Groups:</strong></p>
<ol class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">
<li>Click <strong>Create group</strong> in the Groups menu.</li>
<li>Select a category, enter a title and description.</li>
<li>Optionally, mark as a monitored group.<img src="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/21014859640978">
</li>
<li>Confirm by clicking <strong>Create group</strong>.</li>
</ol>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>Importing Groups:</strong></p>
<ol class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">
<li>Choose a linking variable (e.g., pseudonym).</li>
<li>Extract and list the linking variable values in a CSV file.</li>
<li>Click <strong>Import group</strong> in the Groups menu.</li>
<li>Set the category, title, and description.</li>
<li>Optionally, mark as a monitored group.</li>
<li>Select the linking variable and locate the file on your hard disk.</li>
<li>Confirm by clicking <strong>Next</strong>.</li>
</ol>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>Note:</strong> Empty rows or the value “0” will be ignored during import.</p>
