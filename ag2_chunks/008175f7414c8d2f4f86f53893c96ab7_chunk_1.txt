<h3 id="panel-projects" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>Panel Projects</strong></h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><span style="color: #333333;">Panel surveys are assigned to samples only. </span><span style="color: #333333;">There is an option located in the project properties allowing us to push a survey to all panelists.</span></p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">You navigate: </p>
<ul class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">
<li>
<em>Projects</em> → <em>Project properties</em> → <em>Survey options</em>
</li>
</ul>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Check the option <strong>All panelists can participate in the survey (independent from being in a sample for the survey)</strong>. This will allow all panelists assigned to a portal to see and participate in your survey.</p>
<h2 id="feedback-assistent" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>Feedback Assistant</strong></h2>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">The page module <em>Feedback Assistant</em> enables portal users to create template-based personalized ad hoc surveys in Portals. <span style="color: #091e42;">When creating a new survey in the page module </span><em>Feedback Assistant</em><span style="color: #091e42;">, portal users in their role of Survey Manager can select contacts from the circles they want to invite to their (predefined and centrally managed) survey. Also, they can collect feedback and view the results on the dashboards of <em>MySight</em></span><span style="color: #091e42;">. <span style="color: #333333;">Only portal users in their role as Survey Managers can use the page module </span><em>Feedback Assistant</em><span style="color: #333333;">.</span></span></p>
<h3 id="exterior-view" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>Exterior view</strong></h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><span style="color: #091e42;">On the overview page of the module, you can search for created </span><em>Feedback Assistant</em><span style="color: #091e42;"> surveys, filter the status of the surveys, and sort the view by creation date. </span></p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">The status of a survey is labeled with an according <strong>color bar</strong> at the top of each tile:</p>
<ul class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">
<li>Blue bar: The survey has not started yet</li>
<li>Green bar: The survey has started.</li>
<li>Yellow bar: The survey will soon be closed.</li>
<li>Red bar: The survey is closed.</li>
</ul>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">In the right upper corner of each tile, the remaining time until the expiration of the survey is viewable.</p>
