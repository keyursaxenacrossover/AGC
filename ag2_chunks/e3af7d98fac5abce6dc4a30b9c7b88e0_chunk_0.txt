<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">This article covers various statistical features available in the People menu, including panel data, community statistics, way of entry statistics, session statistics, panel statistics, master data coverage, and panel quotas. These tools help panel administrators monitor and analyze the composition and activity of their panel population.</p>

<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">The statistics feature in the People menu provide comprehensive tools for panel administrators to assess panel health, track community engagement, analyze panelist entry methods, monitor session activity, evaluate panel composition, and manage panel quotas.</p>

<h2 id="h_01HGKSMPRT0YJQX9QJXBX8YDWM" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Panel Data</h2>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">The People → Statistics → Panel overview menu provides an overview on the state of the panel population (“panel health”). The key data are presented on tabs.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><img src="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/21017213457938"></p>
<h3 id="h_01J3G28QMF7GEVPSAGGF9TAPQB" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Panelist Data</h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">This tab features a dynamic evaluation tool for splitting your panelist population by any master data or system master data. To configure, click the <strong>Configuration</strong> button. Results are graphically represented. Note: Only activated panel statuses are displayed.</p>
<h3 id="h_01J3G28QMF3S9NYNG9SWR3GW06" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Tracking Information</h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Tracking data for the entire panel. Refer to the "Panel Tracking" chapter for more information.</p>
<h3 id="h_01J3G28QMFWCYZF2YSZ7CH1WP7" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Panel Projects and Master Data Surveys Online</h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Lists currently active panel and master data surveys. Click on a project title to view the field report, with results graphically represented.</p>
<h3 id="h_01J3G28QMFP4NJKZ7FXJTTKPM5" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Completed Panel Projects and Master Data Surveys</h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Shows panel and master data surveys that have ended within the last 4 weeks.</p>
<h3 id="h_01J3G28QMFQSNMJ3YZKJYEDBRF" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Group History</h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Tracks the development of group sizes over time, specifically for groups marked for monitoring.</p>
<h3 id="h_01J3G28QMFVS6J4645BYQHVADR" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Marking Groups for Better Monitoring</h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">To simplify tracking of important groups, use the "Monitored groups" feature:</p>
<ul class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">
<li>
<strong>Special Marking</strong>: Groups will be marked in the group list.</li>
<li>
<strong>Display Limitation</strong>: Use the “Monitored groups” option in the extended search to limit or exclude these groups.</li>
<li>
<strong>Group History</strong>: Displays development over time (People → Statistics → Panel overview → tab Group history).</li>
</ul>
