<h1 id="h_01JBKX3EKHPSM19Z4DDD70ECQZ">Overview</h1>
<p>This article gives you a quick rundown of how to properly request a blank/clone installation in the DXi Support portal</p>
<p><strong>NOTE: THIS ARTICLE IS MEANT TO BE INTERNAL FOR THE TIME BEING, AS IT IS ONLY AIMED AT KANTAR/IPSOS. A PDF COPY OF IT WAS SHARED WITH EMIN, TO CIRCULATE IT FURTHER WITH THESE 2 CUSTOMERS.</strong></p>
<h1 id="h_01JBKX3JTS5EBSQB10MDAMN12Q">Instructions</h1>
<ol>
<li>Login to the DXi Support portal: <a href="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/en-us/">https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/en-us/</a>
</li>
<li>Click on the chat bubble at the bottom-right</li>
<li>You will be greeted by ATLAS-chat, and it will ask you for your instance URL</li>
<li>Once you provide the instance URL, you will have the option to request for a New installation</li>
<li>Type in <strong>New installation<br><img src="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/22350292868882" width="295" height="321"><br></strong>
</li>
<li>Choose the type of installation you are requesting using the provided <strong>buttons</strong>
</li>
<li>At this stage, you will have 3 options:<br><img src="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/22350292870034" width="239" height="316"><br>
<ol>
<li>
<strong>Just type in the request, making sure you include all required details</strong> - ATLAS-chat will pickup the details from your input and confirm them with you before proceeding further</li>
<li>
<strong>I want to fill a form</strong> - this will bring up a form which will let you insert those details<br><img src="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/22350292871058" width="234" height="307">
</li>
<li>
<strong>Create ticket</strong> - this will allow you to freely write down your request into ATLAS-chat; however, note that it is extremely important that you provide us at least with the details asked by ATLAS</li>
</ol>
</li>
<li> Once you submit the request details, ATLAS will process your request and create a ticket for you, and you will be provided with a ticket number, along with a direct URL to access it.</li>
