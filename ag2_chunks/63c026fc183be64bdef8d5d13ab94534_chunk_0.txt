<h2 id="bonus_system" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Bonus System</h2>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">The bonus system allows you to manage and track incentives given to panelists for various activities. <em>(Access to the bonus system menu is protected by the ACL right panelbonus: Read rights are required.)</em></p>
<h3 id="account_statement" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Account Statement</h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">The Account Statement tab provides an overview of all account movements in the Panel. It includes:</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><img src="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/21014859656722"></p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>Additions and Subtractions</strong></p>
<ul class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">
<li>
<strong>Additions and subtractions</strong> are listed in both points and currency.</li>
<li>Monthly details can be viewed by clicking on the specific month, with yearly aggregated values.</li>
<li>
<strong>Total sum</strong> row displays the lifetime sum of all additions and subtractions.</li>
</ul>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>Balance Section</strong></p>
<ul class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">
<li>Double-check the incentive management calculations using double-entry bookkeeping:
<ul>
<li>
<strong>Sum of additions and subtractions</strong>: Calculated by summing all account movements.</li>
<li>
<strong>Sum of all panelist balances</strong>: Sum of the current status of all panelistsâ€™ accounts.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>These values should be identical. Report discrepancies to support.</li>
</ul>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>Monthly Statement</strong></p>
<ul class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">
<li>View account history for a particular month by clicking on the desired month.</li>
<li>
<strong>Additions</strong>: Aggregated by type with details on number of additions, total points, and corresponding currency. Types include:
<ul>
<li>Project: Bonus points from surveys.</li>
<li>Registration: Points for new registrations.</li>
<li>Recruiting friends: Points from Tell-a-Friend campaigns.</li>
<li>Forum participation: Points for forum discussion participation.</li>
<li>Cancellation: Points credited if a payment request is cancelled.</li>
<li>Moderator allocation: Points manually allocated by moderators.</li>
<li>Other gratifications: Points from special programs.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
<strong>Subtractions</strong>: Aggregated by type with details on number of subtractions, total points, and corresponding currency. Types include:
<ul>
<li>Bonus: Points redeemed for bonuses, debited upon payment request.</li>
<li>Moderator subtraction: Points manually subtracted by moderators.</li>
<li>Points reset actions: Points reset to zero.</li>
<li>Other subtractions: Points subtracted from special programs.<img src="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/21014859658514">
</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>Current Bonus Points Distribution</strong></p>
<ul class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">
<li>
<strong>Panel Status</strong>: The "Listing according to panel status" tab shows the distribution of bonus points by panel status.</li>
<li>
<strong>Language Version</strong>: The "Listing according to language version" tab shows the distribution of bonus points by language version.</li>
</ul>
<h3 id="h_01J3FF967ZJJDV1VXZF7X3WJ74" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Account Statement of Individual Panelists</h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">To access an individual panelist's account statement, go to their account detail view and click on the Edit incentives button. Panelists can view their account balance and account movements in the internal area of the external view.</p>
