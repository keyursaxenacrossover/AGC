<h3 id="h_01HGKSMPRWM7YQX8YJ8YQXQJ8YJXB">How can panelists opt-in to receive private messages?</h3>
<p>Panelists need to activate the "Receive private messages" visibility option in their account settings.</p>
<h3 id="h_01HGKSMPRWRJ8YQXQJ8YJXBKP7YQXQ">What's the difference between panelist polls and anonymous polls?</h3>
<p>Panelist polls require a login and allow for data matching with panelist profiles, while anonymous polls are open to all visitors but provide less detailed data.</p>

<h1 id="h_01HGKSMPRWCQXQJ8YJXBKP7YQX8YJ">FAQ</h1>
<h3 id="h_01HGKSMPRWM7YQX8YJ8YQXQJ8YJXB">How can panelists opt-in to receive private messages?</h3>
<p>Panelists need to activate the "Receive private messages" visibility option in their account settings.</p>
<h3 id="h_01HGKSMPRWRJ8YQXQJ8YJXBKP7YQXQ">What's the difference between panelist polls and anonymous polls?</h3>
<p>Panelist polls require a login and allow for data matching with panelist profiles, while anonymous polls are open to all visitors but provide less detailed data.</p>
<h3 id="h_01HGKSMPRWW8YJ8YQXQJ8YJXBKP7YQ">How are bonus points awarded for completing tasks?</h3>
<p>Bonus points are automatically awarded when a panelist clicks on the task link, completing the assigned activity.</p>
