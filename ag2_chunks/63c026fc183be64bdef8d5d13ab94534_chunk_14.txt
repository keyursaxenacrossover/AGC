<li>Go to People → Incentive management → Campaign menus.</li>
<li>Click the <strong>Create campaign</strong> button to open the form for a new campaign.<img src="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/21014859659666">
</li>
<li>Enter the necessary data and click <strong>Save</strong>.</li>
<li>The campaign, now in “Created” status, will appear in the overview table. Click <strong>Activate</strong> to start the campaign.</li>
</ol>
<p><strong>Editing a Campaign</strong></p>
<ol>
<li>Click on the campaign name in the overview table to access the “Edit campaign” form.</li>
<li>This form is similar to the creation form, with an added “Status” field showing the current status.</li>
<li>Edit the entries as needed and click <strong>Save</strong> to apply changes.</li>
</ol>
<h3 id="h_01J3FGKCRZG71E98MTQ5SXKZEH">Allocating Candidates from a Campaign to a Way of Entry</h3>
<p>For each new promotional campaign, a dedicated way of entry with the same name is automatically created to collect canvassed candidates and facilitate the creation of special groups.</p>
<ol>
<li>
<p><strong>Locate the Way of Entry:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Go to People → Panel configuration → Ways of entry to find the automatically-generated way of entry.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Create a Group:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Ensure this way of entry is used only for the specific campaign to create a group containing panelists canvassed in the campaign.</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ol>
<p><strong>Note:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Use specific tell-a-friend links for invitations in promotional campaigns. Insert these links with the #url# wildcard, not the normal registration keys, which are allocated to specific ways of entry.</li>
</ul>
<h3 id="h_01J3FGKCRZK23KPB10WANM5WVN">Checking the Success of the Campaign</h3>
<p>Access campaign statistics via the icon in the campaigns overview. The statistics include:</p>
<ol>
<li>
<p><strong>Detailed Campaign Success Data:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Provides detailed information describing the campaign's success.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Promoter Participation Overview:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Lists all participating promoters and their personal campaign results.</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ol>
<p><strong>Downloadable Data:</strong></p>
<ol>
<li>
<p><strong>List of Recruiting Attempts:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Contains all recruiting attempts, including promoter name, friend’s email, and if successful, the registration date and master data survey status.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>List of Invited People:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Lists all invited persons who have not responded or completed the registration form, including the invitation code sent in the tell-a-friend mail.</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ol>
