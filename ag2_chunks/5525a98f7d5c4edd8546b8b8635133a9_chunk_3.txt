<p data-renderer-start-pos="6926">Navigate to <strong>Users</strong> to check whether the intended recipient already has an account</p>
</li>

<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="7178">If this is not the case, navigate to teams, create a team using the reporting-rights template, navigate to projects, search for the project the report should be granted for, click the <strong>§</strong> icon and grant read-access to the team</p>
<ol class="ak-ol" start="1" data-indent-level="2">
<li>
