<h2 id="Changing-the-Password" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-renderer-start-pos="2511"><strong data-renderer-mark="true">Changing the Password</strong></h2>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-renderer-start-pos="2534">When you log into the EFS Translator Interface for the first time, you are asked to change your password and define a security question. The security question is required to establish your identity if you forget your password.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-renderer-start-pos="2762">The request to change your password is repeated monthly. If you want to change your password on your own, go to the <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Account data </strong>→<strong data-renderer-mark="true"> Password </strong>menu. (Please click on your name below the left-hand menu, then open the <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Password </strong>menu to access this dialog.)</p>
<h2 id="Updating-Address-Data" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-renderer-start-pos="3014"><strong data-renderer-mark="true">Updating Address Data</strong></h2>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-renderer-start-pos="3037">When creating your acccount, the project manager has entered your address data. In the menu <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Account data</strong>, you can view and update these data. Please take special care to keep your e-mail address up-to-date and working: If you forget your password, the process to reset it runs via this e-mail address.</p>
