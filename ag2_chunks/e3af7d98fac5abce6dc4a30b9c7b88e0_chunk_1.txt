wyg-text-align-justify">Shows panel and master data surveys that have ended within the last 4 weeks.</p>
<h3 id="h_01J3G28QMFQSNMJ3YZKJYEDBRF" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Group History</h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Tracks the development of group sizes over time, specifically for groups marked for monitoring.</p>
<h3 id="h_01J3G28QMFVS6J4645BYQHVADR" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Marking Groups for Better Monitoring</h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">To simplify tracking of important groups, use the "Monitored groups" feature:</p>
<ul class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">
<li>
<strong>Special Marking</strong>: Groups will be marked in the group list.</li>
<li>
<strong>Display Limitation</strong>: Use the “Monitored groups” option in the extended search to limit or exclude these groups.</li>
<li>
<strong>Group History</strong>: Displays development over time (People → Statistics → Panel overview → tab Group history).</li>
</ul>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">To mark a group, navigate to the People → Groups menu, click the <strong>Edit group</strong> icon, tick the “Mark group as 'monitored group'” checkbox, and confirm by clicking <strong>Save group</strong>.</p>

<h2 id="h_01HGKSMPRT4YFXQX3QJCQX8Z9N" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Community Statistics</h2>

<li>Panel group, website, language version</li>
<li>Option to include activities of deleted panelists</li>
