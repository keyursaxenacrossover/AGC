<h4 id="h_01J3G471CX16CQENKG1PZE11FG" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Steps to Configure Updates</h4>
<ol class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">
<li>Go to <strong>People → Statistics → Master data coverage</strong> and open the <strong>Configuration</strong> tab.</li>
<li>If the update is already active, you’ll see the times of the last and next scheduled update.<img src="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/21017226313874">
</li>
<li>If not activated, check the box in the <strong>Activate</strong> field.</li>
<li>In the <strong>Time of update</strong> field, set the desired daily update time (default is 00:00 GMT).</li>
<li>Click <strong>Save</strong> to confirm the settings.</li>
</ol>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">These steps ensure your master data coverage is assessed regularly, helping maintain data quality and completeness.</p>
