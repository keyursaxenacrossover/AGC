<h4 id="Evaluating-Questions-by-Means-of-Diagrams" data-renderer-start-pos="6321"><strong data-renderer-mark="true">Evaluating Questions by Means of Diagrams</strong></h4>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="6364">If you intend to export your report as a PowerPoint presentation or PDF file after completion, it is a good idea to evaluate questions in form of diagrams. To change the presentation of the data from a table to a diagram, proceed as follows:</p>
<ul class="ak-ul" data-indent-level="1">
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="6609">In the <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Structure</strong> area, click on the question, whose presentation you would like to change.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="6703">The question is displayed in the <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Workpad</strong> area. Click on the arrow next to the bar icon. There are various chart options to choose from. For our test report, we select<strong data-renderer-mark="true"> Column chart</strong>.<img src="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/21015166398482"></p>
</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li data-renderer-start-pos="6703">The result data of your question is now displayed as a column chart. Click on the arrow next to the bar icon in the <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Workpad </strong>area again, and select the <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Chart layout</strong> option at the bottom of the list.</li>
<li data-renderer-start-pos="6703">You can now customize the layout of your chart as you wish. Under <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Chart type</strong>, you can select your preferred color scheme for your chart from the drop-down menu on the right. For our example chart, we select the option <strong data-renderer-mark="true">QB standard</strong>.</li>
<li data-renderer-start-pos="6703">Under <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Display options</strong>, you can specify that the respective response values are displayed above columns and a legend is displayed below the chart. Just select the corresponding checkbox.</li>
<li data-renderer-start-pos="6703">Under <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Values of the chart</strong>, you can choose, whether values should be displayed as a percentage or as a number.</li>
<li data-renderer-start-pos="6703">Click on <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Save</strong>.</li>
<li data-renderer-start-pos="6703">Your chart is now displayed according to the selected options in the <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Workpad</strong> area.<img src="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/21015136336914">
</li>
</ul>

<h3 id="Applying-Filters" data-renderer-start-pos="7739"><strong data-renderer-mark="true">Applying Filters</strong></h3>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="7757">By means of filters, you can restrict the basis of your report – i.e. the number of people to be included in your analysis. In the following, we would like to use two examples to show you in detail how you can apply filters to your entire report or to specific sections of your report.</
