<h2 id="bonus-points-configuration" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>Bonus points configuration</strong></h2>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">If you wish to reward participation in a panel survey or a master data survey, you can choose among the following options:</p>
<ul class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">
<li>
<p>Allocation of bonus points on the system's final page: Usually, you want to reward the completion of the questionnaire. Specify the desired amount of bonus points in the menu<strong> Project properties</strong> →<strong> Survey messages</strong> menu in the “Number of bonus points to allot when reaching standard final page” field.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Allocation of bonus points on additionally created intermediate final pages: If you want to allocate points on intermediate final pages as well, you can define the desired amounts for each page in the menu <strong>Project properties</strong> →<strong> Bonus points configuration</strong>.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Bonus triggers: Using a bonus trigger, you can reward the arrival at a specific questionnaire page, the correct answering of a question, or another self-defined event with an allocation.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">The same panelist can receive several rewards in one survey, if necessary, e.g. one reward per trigger and one reward on the final page.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">You can reward the completion of a survey with bonus points. Subtracting bonus points is not reasonable in this situation, and therefore not permitted.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>Defining the reason for the allocation</strong></p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">The reason given in the panelists’ account statements and the bonus points log is defined on the <strong>Project properties</strong> →<strong> Survey messages</strong> menu in the „Entry into the panelist’s bonus points history“ field for all allocations of the project. If you allocate bonus points at several points of your questionnaire, please take care to enter a general, meaningful text: It is not possible to define a dedicated text for the bonus points log for every trigger and every final page.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">In multilingual projects, enter the translation of the reason in the <strong>Language editor</strong> → <strong>Survey messages</strong> menu.</p>
<h2 id="template-configuration" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>Template configuration</strong></h2>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">In the menu<strong> Project Properties → Template configuration </strong>you can create templates for the <em>Portals</em> module <em>Guides</em> and the <em>Portals</em> module <em>Feedback Assistant</em>.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>Usage</strong></p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Under <strong>Usage,</strong> you define for which of the two <em>Portals</em> modules – <em>Guides</em> or <em>Feedback Assistant</em> – you want to create a template, and you also choose, on which of the two modules the used <em>PE</em> project should be based. Click <strong>Save</strong> after you have made your selection. Only after you have saved your settings, the connection between the <em>PE</em> project and the selected <em>Portals</em> module becomes effective. The selected <em>PE</em> project can naturally also be used for the other <em>Portals</em> module. Just choose the desired module and click on <strong>Save</strong>.</p>
