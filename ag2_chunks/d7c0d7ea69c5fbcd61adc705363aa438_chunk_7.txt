<h3 id="h_01HGKSMPRTX0JDQX3JDQKX3NR0" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">How can I export contact request data?</h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">You can export contact request data using the Excel export or CSV export buttons in the Contact Request List under People → Contacts.</p>
<h3 id="h_01HGKSMPRTX0JDQX3JDQKX3NR1" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">What's the difference between Personal and Managed Groups?</h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Personal Groups are created by individual portal users for their own contact lists, while Managed Groups are generated in the administration area and consist of dynamically composed, hierarchically dependent groups accessible to multiple users.</p>
<h3 id="h_01HGKSMPRTX0JDQX3JDQKX3NR2" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">How can I protect panelists' email addresses from unauthorized access?</h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">You can use the EFS rights system to hide email addresses for specific user teams in various menus and functions. This includes setting up appropriate ACL rights and object rights for different user teams.</p>
