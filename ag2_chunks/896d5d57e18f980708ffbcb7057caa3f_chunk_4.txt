<h2 id="best_practices">EFS Best Practices</h2>
<p>The recommended approach is to:</p>
<ol>
<li>Use unsubscribe placeholders in the email text to ensure participants always have a way to unsubscribe.</li>
<li>Set the "List-Unsubscribe Header" for RFC compliance and potential email server requirements.</li>
<li>Consider using additional headers like "List-Unsubscribe-Post" for enhanced functionality with supporting email clients.</li>
</ol>
<h1 id="h_01HGKSMPRSER9ENY5QWWRZZ863">FAQ</h1>
<h3 id="faq_1">Why are unsubscribe features limited on collective installations?</h3>
<p>The limitation was originally implemented to prevent customers on collective installations from blocking participants for other customers. If a participant from Customer A globally unsubscribed, they would not receive emails from Customer B either.</p>
<h3 id="faq_2">How can I implement a one-click unsubscribe feature?</h3>
<p>While EFS doesn't directly support one-click unsubscribe, you can use additional headers like "List-Unsubscribe-Post" to enable this functionality for email clients that support it. However, this is not widely supported by most email clients.</p>
