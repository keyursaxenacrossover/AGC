<h2 id="The-GDPR-settings-in-the-Questionnaire-editor" data-renderer-start-pos="8962">The GDPR settings in the Questionnaire editor</h2>
</div>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-renderer-start-pos="9010">After activating the <em data-renderer-mark="true">Privacy Assistant</em>, the next step will be to edit a Consent form. By using the Consent form, you inform the participants about the purpose of the data processing and the most important privacy information such as the name of the company, the contact data, the representation or the type of personal data and their use. You also specify the retention period.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify" data-renderer-start-pos="9389">You will find the GDPR settings area in the questionnaire editor, where you can define the data retention period and edit the consent form, mark personal data and create the consent form in different languages.</p>

<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="9603">The GDPR menu can be opened under the menu item by either marking one of the links <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Personal data flagging</strong>, <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Duration of data retention</strong> or <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Consent form</strong> or clicking on the <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Edit</strong> icons.<img src="https://discoverxi-supportdesk.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/21017191622930"></p>
