wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><span style="color: #091e42;">The template configuration for </span><em>Feedback Assistant</em><span style="color: #091e42;"> can be found in the respective personalized project under </span><strong>Project Properties</strong><span style="color: #091e42;"> and is divided into different areas. The access to each template configuration is controlled via the rights and role system of EFS. EFS administrators with respective rights can extensively configure each personalized project as templates for use and choice within the module </span><em>Feedback Assistant</em><span style="color: #091e42;">.</span></p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>Basic information</strong></p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Here, administrators define a title as well as a description and a background image to make your template in the module <em>Feedback Assistant</em> visible.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>Manager participation</strong></p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">You control the manager's participation in a <em>Feedback Assistant</em> survey by specifying whether the manager never or always participates in a survey or whether the manager can decide whether to participate.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>MySight Dashboard</strong></p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">The MySight configuration for the use of dashboards is optional in the context of configuring <em>Feedback Assistant</em>. You can use the <em>Feedback Assistant</em> without having configured <em><a class="unresolved" href="https://qbdocs.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/DOC/pages/24248910/Template+configuration+for+Feedback+Assistant">MYS</a></em>.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>Allocating the template to certain portals</strong></p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">In the area <strong>Portals</strong>, all portals of the installation are listed. By activating the checkboxes, you can control in which portals your template will be available within the module <em>Feedback Assistant</em>. The links guide you directly to each CMS of a portal</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>Defining mail templates for invitations and reminders</strong></p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">You can allocate mail templates of the type “Projects” to the template to be configured, for invitation mails as well as reminder mails for participants of the <em>Feedback Assistant</em> survey.</p>
<ol class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">
<li>Click on <strong>Mail templates</strong> in the Survey menu of your project.</li>
<li>Open the drop-down menu <strong>New draft</strong> and select the option <strong>New draft for projects</strong>.</li>
<li>Select the <strong>Type</strong> of the template <strong>Invitation mail</strong> or <strong>Reminder mail</strong>.</li>
<li>Fill in at least the mandatory fields <strong>Description</strong>, <strong>Subject,</strong> and <strong>Mail text</strong> for the template and select a respective <strong>Mail sender</strong>.</li>
<li>Click on <strong>Save</strong>.</li>
<li>Switch to the <strong>Template configuration</strong> of your project under <strong>Project Properties</strong> and activate the checkbox(es) of the desired mail template(s).</li>
</ol>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>Selecting panelist variables</strong></p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">If panelists take part in your survey, here you can select the panelist-specific variables you want to be available for further use, e.g. for evaluation in the dashboard of the respective <em>Feedback Assistant</em> survey or for mail templates to send out personalized invitations.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>Selecting manager variables</strong></p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Master data variables that are solely allocated to the respective survey manager initiating the <em>Feedback Assistant</em> survey via the respective module in Portals can be selected in this section and appended to the template for displaying hierarchies for instance or later evaluation in the dashboard.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>Creating and/or selecting context variables</strong></p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Create and/or select from existing context variables the variables you want to offer to the survey manager within the module <em>Feedback Assistant</em> for free definition. Accordingly selected and filled variables will be available for evaluation in the dashboard afterwards.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Upon creation of context variables, you can influence the display mode by selecting between <strong>Checkbox</strong>, <strong>Date,</strong> and <strong>Text</strong>.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>Synchronizing panelist data</strong></p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Via the functionality „Synchronize panelist data”, you can keep the data of panelists as participants of <em>Feedback Assistant</em> surveys
