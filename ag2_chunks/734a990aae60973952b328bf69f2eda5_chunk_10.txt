<h2 id="managing-personalized-links-for-external-survey-start" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify"><strong>Managing personalized links for external survey start</strong></h2>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">When redirecting sample members to an external survey, personalized links must be used. The following features for managing these links are available:</p>
<ul class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">
<li>
<p><strong>Import links</strong>: Opens the import dialog.</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>The CSV file must contain the survey link in the first column and the URL has to start with the protocol (http:// or https://), the placeholder #panel_url#, or any other placeholder specifying the protocol. Optionally you may add the panelists’ pseudonym, <em>u_account</em>,<em> u_other_id</em>, or <em>panelist_code</em> in the second column. In this case, the links will be assigned to the panel members accordingly.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>You can only import or update links for panelists who have not yet started the survey.</p>
</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Export links</strong>: Triggers the export of all links.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Delete links</strong>: All links are deleted after a confirmation prompt.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">For details on the external survey start, please see the dedicated manual which is available in the customer center. Please note that this document is currently in revision.</p>

<h3 id="faq-1" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">What is the maximum size of a sample?</h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">A sample may contain up to 500,000 panelists. However, it's recommended to draw several small samples for large panels or complex survey projects to maintain performance.</p>
<h3 id="faq-2" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">Can a panelist be in multiple samples for the same project?</h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">No, a panelist can only be allocated to one sample per project.</p>
<h3 id="faq-3" class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">How can I exclude participants from other projects in my sample?</h3>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-justify">On the "Define basic set" tab, you can choose projects whose participants should be excluded from the current sample draw.</p> on the external survey start, please see the dedicated manual which is available in the customer center.
